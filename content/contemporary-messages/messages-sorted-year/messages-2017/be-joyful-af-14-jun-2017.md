# "Be Joyful" advice from Goldie

June 14<sup>th</sup>, 2017

Abbotsford, B. C. Canada

Received by A.F.

I am Goldie. God bless you. Bless you, beloved souls. You are precious to our hearts. You give us joy. God bless you.
