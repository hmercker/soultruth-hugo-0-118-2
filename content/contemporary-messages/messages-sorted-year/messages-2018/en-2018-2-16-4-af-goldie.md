# Bring Music!

February 16<sup>th</sup>, 2018

Onlie Circle of Light, Hollis Woods Community Church, Queens, NY, USA

Goldie received by Al Fike

God bless you. Bring music into this place. Bring those whose hearts are stirred by the beautiful sounds and voices, instruments of music; such a gift to humanity, these beau-tiful souls that stir your soul with their heartfelt songs and expressions of creativity.
Bring joy into your midst for what is a life without joy? What is a life that is lost in seri-ousness and worry? Bring healing, for in the healing of the mind and the soul and the body there is upliftment and hope. Bring Love, for without Love nothing is possible. To uplift the spirit these simple things, my beloveds, these simple things will disarm all those who are cautious, will encourage many to feel free to express themselves, will open and inspire the souls.

It does not take much, truly these simple things are your message and can be the message of this place. I know you wish to see this place filled with joy and beauty, Love. So it shall be for God has already filled it with His Love. The Light is here. Now bring the souls, bring them, draw them in with the sweetness of your voices, the caring of your embrace, the joy of your laughter. Bring them in. I will be with you dancing and laughing. I will increase your joy. I will be with you and many, many others as well in this beautiful dedication of service and Love.

God bless you. I am Goldie and I love you. I love you.
