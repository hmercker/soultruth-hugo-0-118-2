# The Lattice of Light is a Magnificent Support For Spiritual Growth

December 30<sup>th</sup>, 2018

Online Prayer Circles, Abbotsford, BC, Canada

Keea Atta Kem received by Maureen Cardoso

Beloved souls, it is Keea. My dear friends, God hears your prayers, your prayers to be drawn into one another during this hour of prayer where you join near and far, desiring to have God’s Love replenish your souls, cleanse your souls, uplift your spirits, and come close. If you could see from our side of life what you are generating at this time, you would be astonished. For, as each of you yearn from your soul to be with God creating an inflowing of this Love into your soul, God beams down, delivering through His Holy Spirit to each of you a great Light, an inflowing of this Love. As each of you is drawn into this wide, global prayer, your lights beam from your souls to each other’s souls. Beloveds, it is an extraordinary view that we see and we know that this will continue to grow where this netting of light brings a great beam to your world. It provides strength to your soul and it provides strength to your families, to your friends, to your cities, to your countries, and so forth.

Beloveds, this is going to strengthen, this lattice that you pray for, this Lattice of Light. Many, many souls will be drawn into praying in this way with you, for it is a most remarkable way to easily reach one another and to know you have a friend near in spirit praying with you, praying for you, thinking of you, and caring for you. For, does this not make you feel great comfort and a closeness to one another? Yes, beloved souls, this is a most magnificent support system for your spiritual growth and the way in which you can easily spread the Truths of God, the Truths of Love with one another by simply being in the comforts of your own home and readily reaching out to one another and praying together.

I wish to acknowledge your efforts, beloveds. It is a most extraordinary view that we have and we are all very joyous as we join in your hour of prayer. I am very privileged to be the one to come and speak and let you know of our joy in what we see growing between this most beloved group of souls who are dedicated to awakening themselves by the Love of God, dedicating their time and efforts to coming to the understanding of their souls, the awakening of their souls, the strengthening of their souls, the appreciation of their soul, who you truly are.

We pray with you now, and we surround you. We send our love. We add our light. We add our energies of healing to each and every one of you. May you continue to yearn for God’s Love. May you continue to come to know your soul, to awaken your soul, to release those parts of you in your soul that are not in harmony with Love, and to allow your entire being to be changed by the Love of God. Beloveds, it’s a most profound experience to witness, your love for one another, your love for God, and the strength that we see growing in each and every one of you. Your strength, the desire to be in service, to do God’s Will, to be aligned in harmony with these Laws of Creation, for this is what will change your world, beloveds. This is what will change your world.

Our love is with you. We support you and uphold you. We are so very, very proud of each and every one of you, your dedication, and the Love that grows. You are dedicated to God. What an extraordinary journey you are upon, continue beloveds. I am Keea Atta Kem and I am so very happy to speak with you this evening. God bless each and every one of you. Our love is with you, God bless you.
