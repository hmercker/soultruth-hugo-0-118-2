# The Great Family of God's Love

February 21<sup>st</sup>, 2018

Onlie Circle of Light, Monroeville, PA, USA

Andrew received by Al Fike

I am Andrew. The Father’s Love continues to flow into your souls and as you have come together in this prayer, drawing one another close, feeling the love from your hearts for one another and for God and so you uplift your conditions, your demean-ours and you are open to all the Light and Love that is present here in this room, the Presence of God in your midst, the openings of those inner selves, your souls, that tender place within you receiving the blessing of His Essence continually flow-ing into your souls.  

As you pray together in this way beloved souls, there is an aspect of this blessing of the Father’s Love that also reaches out and touches God and touches each other, connecting you all in the bond of Love that does not diminish or disappear but will stay with you for all eternity my beloveds, this bond of Love, this connection that you make in Love. 

As you progress in this world and in the world of spirit and into the Celestial King-dom, there is something within you that is within each one around you creating a sense of family, of being a part of one another. As you go out into the world and continue to pray with others and to love and to be a channel of Love in this world, so you increase your family. You allow the faculty of the soul infilled with the Fa-ther’s Love to reach out to each one that is connected soul-to-soul, so that there is this bond, this Light, this network of Light that connects and connects and connects to many, many souls creating this beautiful lattice of Light, a lattice of Light that God intends to spread throughout the entire world encasing this world in Love, in Light, in the power of this Love, to bring souls together in a way that brings meaning, love, awareness, a sense of being a part of each other. 

This is a powerful blessing. The blessing of the Father’s Love has many aspects to its habitation within the soul, creating these blessings that may benefit not only you but many others. So, you have become family, my beloved souls, and you shall ev-er be family and thus your family grows with each effort you make in prayer togeth-er to receive the Father’s Love, to send your love to another and that love is recip-rocated by the other, creating a beautiful blessing, this cord of Light that stretches from one to another, to another, bringing you together in a sense that you do in-deed belong to one another and to God. The Father’s children, the Father’s chil-dren, together all in this great wave of Love from the great Soul of God.

May your family continue to grow, my beloveds. When you set forth in the world give love wherever you go. Allow these connections to take place. Allow God to build this wondrous network of Light and thus you become His channels of Love in the world and thus His plan for the salvation of mankind is fulfilled in this way as it will be in many other ways.

Work together my beloveds. Work as one, in Love. Though you are all unique and beautiful and have many unique gifts, this one gift that I describe belongs to you all and can be utilized by God to bless many.

God bless you my beloveds. I am Andrew and I love you and I too am connected with you as you are connected to me and many angels, and so this great family blossoms in Love and continues to grow. God bless you my beloveds. God bless you.
