Eastern United States Soul Awakening Tour, Nashville, TN, USA

The preparations are well underway. We angels are prepared to serve our brothers and sisters who are arriving and who will be present in the next few days. You will be surprised and delighted at the outcome of this retreat, for it will be different than any you have had before. There will be sincere longing to know truth, to be uplifted and to come into better conditions, for these are not novices as you might expect. Your prayers will be strengthened. Your Father will uplift you as a group. Make the most of this retreat, for it may be the only one among these souls. Keep in mind the focus of God, so near and dear to these souls. Let your love flow willingly to each one and you will move them forward. Thank you for allowing me to speak. I am your brother, Peter, and we will be with you en masse. May god bless you.
