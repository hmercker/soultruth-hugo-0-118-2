# I am Redeemed by the Gift of God's Love

January 6<sup>th</sup>, 2024

Trinidad Retreat, Trinidad

Yogananda received by Al Fike

I am Yogananda. You wonder, one such as I, whether I am in the Celestial Heavens where the teachings of Jesus reigns supreme. I tell you, I am a student of this truth. Even though my life on Earth taught, in many ways, something that was different and more of the mental understanding of spirituality, I now know of the soul's understanding more clearly. The mind, my mind and my soul have come together in accord with truth.

I encourage you, beloved and beautiful friends, to seek of the soul. It is different from the mind. It will take you to consciousness beyond the mind. Help you to understand that it is not of the mind, but of the soul, which is, as our dear friend has explained, a reflection of God.

Come with us now and we will uplift you beyond this Earthly plane to that place of blissful awakening and communing with the Creator of all the universe. Come with me. Come with me. We will find our way together. The angels will hold your hands. You will be lifted up, lifted up into the bliss of love. I am Yogananda and I bless you with love. Join me, join me in prayer for the Father's Love. We will know this together, part of a fraternity that lasts for all eternity. Beloved souls, God bless you.
