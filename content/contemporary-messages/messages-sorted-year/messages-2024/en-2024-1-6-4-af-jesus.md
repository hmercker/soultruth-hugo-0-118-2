# May We Be Transformed by the Gift of God’s Love

January 6<sup>th</sup>, 2024

Trinidad Retreat, Trinidad

Jesus received by Al Fike

The glory of God is upon you, beloveds. I come. I am your brother and friend. I am Jesus. I come to affirm the truth and the power of God's Love for each of you.

I come as God's instrument to bring this power more clearly and abundantly to this place, amongst you, in the glory and wonderment of God's blessing. Open yourselves and know the power and glory of His blessings, beloved souls. It is for you, a gift. It is for you a manifestation of Love from God. The power and the glory of Heaven is upon you and shall bring untold blessings and transformation.

I acknowledge each one of you. Know that I love you. Your souls are precious to God. You shall know this in good time. You shall know this while on this Earth. You shall know this because your heart and soul seek it and wish to know in a conscious way that you are loved by God. God bless you. God bless you.

**Comments by Al Fike on Jesus’ Manifestations to Those in Spirit World**

This is a modest group of souls coming together, but the number of spirits and angels over here is exponentially larger than what this group is. We drew, and we have a purpose of drawing many, many seeking souls in the spirit world. It spread out in all directions over thousands. Jesus came. I don't know whether anybody saw him in his manifestation, but  certainly many spirits did. I was told that many who had never seen him saw him for the first time as he stood up and circled in the room.

We provided a great service to many, including many of your relatives that are beyond the Earthly plane now. They're here watching because they are curious. It's not that you go from here to truth, light and love immediately. You have to, like anything, like in your own life in the material plane, you've got to progress, you've got to work towards it. This is a blessing and a gift to your relatives, your ancestors, and many, many more. You help facilitate this and that's also a blessing for you to help you to work towards greater light.

We are all in the journey of progression of the soul. It doesn't stop ever. We have acknowledged this by coming together in this beautiful Circle of Light. I hope you have felt the touch of the Divine within your soul, maybe inspired by the words of the angels. Possibly you have seen something. I hope you have felt something, and you have come to a realization, some understanding that might have been beyond what you had in the past.
