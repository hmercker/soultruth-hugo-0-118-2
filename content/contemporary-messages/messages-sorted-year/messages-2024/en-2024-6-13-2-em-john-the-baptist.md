# To Say That You Are Blessed Is Inadequate

June 13<sup>th</sup>, 2024

One Nation Circle of Light, Liverpool, NY, USA

John the Baptist received by Elizabeth Mack

I am here. I am John the Baptist. Beloved children of God, I seek no more from you than your hearts turning toward your Heavenly Father. For in truth, your Creator, your Father, is the Source of all things. Giving allegiance, giving your best, your love, your admiration, your commitment, your hands and hearts in service to God is the highest endeavour a human being can choose.

Once, I said I was not worthy. I am worthy. I am my Father's son. His Love, His Light, shone upon my soul and changed everything that was within me into a glorious, gloriously loving entity, a soul that exists now forever. This is the path that you are on also. This path of great transformation from being merely an image to being the Substance of the great Soul of our Creator. How blessed are we? All that is needed to accomplish this endeavour is to ask and receive this glorious gift of God’s Love into our souls, step by step, moving closer and closer to the very Fountainhead of this Love.

My dear children, to say that you are blessed is inadequate. This Love shines through your souls so brightly, effervescently, if you will. How I wish you could see how beautiful, how sparkly and beautiful, are your souls. Even though you are not completely transformed, you are the most beautiful of God’s creatures. I thank you for listening and for accepting my appreciation of your progress upon the Earth. Mine was in the spirit world. You are truly God’s children upon this Earth. I honour you as such.

May God continue to bless you mightily and continuously. May your souls drink up all of this Love and receive all of the blessings that the Father has for each one of you. God bless you. I am John the Baptist and my love is with you as well. God bless you.
