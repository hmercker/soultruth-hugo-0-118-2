# Doors are Opening for You

March 7<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Direct Voice, Gibsons, BC, Canada

Andrew received by Al Fike

Beloved friends, I am Andrew. I wish to assure you that as you continue to strive towards the highest and seek the great and highest gift of all, the Divine Love of God into your souls, so you are helping God to open these mighty doors, heavy that they are, closed for so long in the history of humanity. Yet you, through your efforts, have the capacity and strength and faith that will allow these doors to open. Do not doubt yourselves, my friends. Do not look to another to do this heavy lifting, but have confidence and faith in your own gifts, your own being, to be that individual, that channel that will fulfil God’s Will in the unfolding of His plan.

So much awaits each one of you and we have promised much to each of you. Yet, I know you look upon your daily life in its mundane way and nature and wonder, “when will I fulfil my purpose, that which God has planned for me?” Yet, in many ways you are fulfilling God’s plan for each of you. You journey upon a path that God has designated for you. You seek to fulfil the awakening of your soul through prayer, through allowing that awakening to bring the perceptions and the understanding of truth to your conscious self. So it comes drop by drop, and so you awaken bit by bit. The accumulation of this effort will bring the rewards, the manifestations and those more powerful expressions of your gifts forward. They are very close, indeed.

As the world changes and the conditions of your world intensify, that which is within you responds and awakens accordingly. Do you not feel this within yourself, my friends? Do you not see that God is ushering you through that mighty door and you are responding in love and faith and trust in His guidance? Do not let go of the power of love within you to bring to you the insights and the perceptions and the joy which come with a soul responding to God’s Touch. You are truly his children and you will find your way. Though indeed, the expressions of your instrumentality are somewhat subtle and do not bring that intensity and drama that you wish to unfold and be expressed in such a way that others will see, will come to know that God is working through you and God’s Truth is manifesting around you, yet this truth and light and love continues to brighten and is stronger with each day. Do you not feel different? For, you are different. The process of transformation is changing you and at times, you feel the discomfort of this continued growth, the pains that come, the adjustments that are necessary for you to awaken. Values and ideas, concepts that you’ve had in the past, are transforming and falling away so that you are in greater alignment with God, more fully in the flow of His Will, more truly expressing the power of love, the power of the wisdom of the soul. These things will continue to unfold, will continue to bring change and will continue to manifest as you walk in faith. Pray with intensity and love. Open yourselves wide so that God may touch you deeply.

We are all moving together upon this trajectory, this journey of hope, hope that humanity will change its ways, hope that all will come into harmony, hope that love will rule the day. So, you are not alone in your endeavours. You are not lost in any way, but continue to seek for higher truth, for more powerful guidance and clarity and light reflecting the love within your soul, that you may walk in joy, my beloved and beautiful friends. Walk in joy and peace and see the true purpose that God has placed before you and the true journey that God has devised so intricately, so beautifully for each of you.

May your eyes open, my friends. May you come to see and in that seeing, a great joy, a bursting forth of exuberance and acknowledgement and revelation for what God has done and God has brought you to upon this wonderful path of love. God bless you, my friends. I am Andrew. My love for you is great, great, beloveds. We are together, always we shall be together. God bless you.
