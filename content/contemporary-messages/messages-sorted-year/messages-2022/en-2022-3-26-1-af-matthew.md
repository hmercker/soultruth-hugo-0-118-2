# Your Prayers Help Raise World Consciousness

March 26<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Monroeville, PA, USA

Matthew received by Al Fike

…flow freely into your souls, my beloved friends. I am Matthew. You recognize the needs of your world, that light is most needed. And whatever light reflects, so harmony must follow. Indeed, your world, such a diverse place that it is, is greatly challenged to bring harmony and peace to it. For, the differences in thought and deed amongst humanity, they are great; so many cultures, so many beliefs, so many objectives and ideas to follow.

Yet, what is it that can unify humanity? What is it that could bring harmony and peace? It is in focusing the consciousness of humanity from that of the mind to those deeper parts of themselves which reside within the soul. In this way, life would find deeper purpose and deeper expression. Yet, this simple act of defying the dominance of the mind and encouraging the awakening of the soul is deeply challenging to many, for they do not understand the process, the possibilities that come in the yearnings of the soul meeting the Will of God, forging that connection, that bond between our true natures, our true selves, with that of the Creator of the Universe.

Instead, many are content with mindful pursuits, with ignoring the desires of the heart and the soul and forging ahead towards material gain and security. You challenge this, beloved souls, when you come together in these Circles of Light. You are making the statement that there is something more important, fulfilling, powerful and beautiful than that of the human mind in the process of creating the human condition, that you have come to realize that love is the answer to all dilemmas and conditions that are not in harmony with God. You have come to realize that within your own life, in and your own experience with life, that the highest road is that of the soul receiving the Gift of Love from God and integrating this truth in your life.

May you come to be a bright example of this. May this integration create expressions and the revealing of aspects of yourselves that are not well-expressed upon the earthly plane but are truly of yourself and your spirit. For, this is the next step of humanity, to understand these other, finer, more subtle aspects of themselves and to nurture them. Not in the way that is controlled by another or dictated by another, but in the way that is the personal journey towards light and truth and love, forging a relationship with their Creator that is personal and powerful, that informs their life, informs their understanding of life, informs who they truly are and what life truly is.

All of these mysteries can be solved and opened to truth and awareness by the individual’s undertaking and desire to do so. But many are lost and mired within the human condition and the material world. They cannot see beyond it. They do not understand or appreciate what blessings may come from such an effort. But as the world changes and as the conditions in your world worsen and become more challenging to each individual who inhabit this world of yours, so it is for you to be an example and to show the way to the lost souls, so that rather than suffer more and be mired more deeply within the disharmony of the human condition, that they may find a way out, that they may come to know the deep desires of their souls, that they may come to know the deep truth of life, that they may come to know the power of love to transform and to heal.

As you come together in these circles, beloved souls, so you come together to affirm these things of which I speak. You come together to support one another and to say *“yes, I understand. I am truly your brother, your sister in this understanding, in this experience, this remarkable and extraordinary experience of having the soul touched by God.”* And God will continue to uphold you, to weave these connections of light, one to another, to ensure that you have one another. As this world continues to change, your bonds will strengthen, your love and light will grow and expand as you continue to reach forth to God and continue to reach forth to one another. It is important that you do so, it is crucial. For without such efforts, you are more readily lost to the human condition and drifting within that darkness. With the strength of numbers, of love amongst you, of acknowledging this great gift that you carry, then you reinforce the light and are upheld in light and shall carry on in light, greater light, greater love.

It all comes as you pray together and as you pray in your private place of prayer, as you reach out to one another, as you think of one another, as you send love to one another. And as you open yourselves to the great forces of light and love and truth that are reaching towards you and uplifting you upon this journey of light, God is orchestrating all of these things. God is ensuring that you are secured in light, protected in light, awakened in light, and shall do so, not only for the all the days that you walk upon this earth, but for all eternity, you will walk in light and be a child of God truly as you are meant to be.

Beloved souls, continue to open your hearts to one another, open your hearts to God and open your hearts to your brothers and sisters in the world. For it is in this very act that the world may change and be healed. You pray to be God’s instruments in the world, so God will use you as you are willing, as you are open, as you desire to be used. So, the opportunities will flood into your lives and the possibilities will open ever greater and the beauty of your instrumentality and your gifts will flourish in this.


Beloved souls, you are a gift to one another. God has given you the gift of life. God will continue to gift you with many things, many blessings. And so, your life is filled with gifts and blessings and may you consciously acknowledge and be aware of how truly gifted and blessed you are. For it is God’s desire that you reach for the highest, that you acknowledge the highest and that you are joyful in this acknowledgment and this experience and this effort to be upon the road Divine, to seek His Love and all that comes with it.

God bless you, beloved souls. I am Matthew. My love is with you. I walk with you as do many. God bless you, beloved souls, upon this journey towards greater light and truth and love. God bless you.
