# Welcomes First Guest to Arrive at Monroeville Retreat

March 25<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Monroeville, PA, USA

Augustine received by Al Fike

…love be upon you all, beloved souls. I am Augustine, your teacher. We are not so concerned about the infirmities of the flesh, my beloved and beautiful friends. For, each of you possesses a soul that is perfect and beautiful and lacks for nothing. Each of you may nourish this soul in your prayers and longings to God, Who has all that you require so that your souls may awaken, your souls may open and flourish in love. There is only one thing that may hold you from this blessing, and that is your lack of application in prayer and longing to receive. So, you carry the responsibility and the choice. It does not come to you as a natural thing that God has instilled within you since the beginning of your creation.

God has given you many gifts and has blessed you with many things, but this one gift, the gift of His Love, is something that must come with your beckoning to God that He may give you this highest and most holy of gifts, that your soul may be awakened drop by drop, blessing upon blessing, until you have accumulated enough of this gift, these Waters of Life so that you may be transformed by it and awakened from it.

So, you come together in these Circles of Light, so you come together with God in your own prayers, at times where you acknowledge and desire His blessing. This is the way of your life upon this Earth and also in the spirit world, that it is dependent upon your choice and your desire. It cannot be any other way for this is the law, it cannot come any other way. For, without your desire and reception of this gift, it remains outside of your being, awaiting your effort and openness, awaiting that moment where the pure desire of your soul rings forth to God and asks for this blessing to come within. Many of you have experienced this blessing, this Touch from God. For some of you, it is very new and not well understood. Some have been receiving this gift for a long time upon your Earth plane, but all of you remain equally children of God and as such, are brothers and sisters walking this path together, receiving in this communal unity of prayer, this Circle of Light.

Uphold one another, beloveds. Seek to be the channel, the comforter, the brother, the sister, to those you are with. For as you love one another, so you enhance the conditions, the light around you, and make an atmosphere which is conducive to drawing more of God’s Love into your midst and a better opportunity to receive. And of course, you draw the angels close to you. Each of you have been assigned an angel and in this, you are being upheld, protected and blessed in light. You will come to know that you are loved, that God provides every way and means for you to grow in light, for your souls to expand in love, for you to awaken to the Truth of God’s Love. It comes in increments. It comes as you make your efforts. It comes in abundance and a powerful flow focused upon your soul. But you hold the key that shall unlock the door, to open the way for His Love to enter therein.

May you exercise your gift of free will, along with those feelings and longings within your soul, to make that passageway open and clear for the gift that God so desires for you to have. Your Heavenly Father has great love for each of you, great love indeed, more than you can possibly imagine, beloveds, more than you can possibly receive. So that Love is so great that it is for you to know it, to understand it, to acknowledge it, to have faith that you may receive it. Beloved children, you are the hope for the world. Those who aspire to awaken within their souls will lead the world beyond the error and darkness of the human condition and bring the truth to humanity.

Beloved and beautiful children, how precious you are to God and to your angel friends and to the bright spirits of the spirit world. For, the world is in need of uplifting, reclamation, healing, harmony, and it is the power of love that shall bring these things forth. It is the power of love that is what is required to awaken humanity. So, you come to the great Fountainhead, thirsting within your souls, seeking this gift. So you shall slake your thirst in the Living Waters of God’s Love. Yet that thirst will return again and again and so shall you, my friends, return to this great Source. For, you cannot receive a finite amount, but is given to you drop by drop. For all eternity, you shall drink and know the pleasure and joy of this elixir of eternal life and love.

Blessings to you, beloved souls, blessings to each of you. May you draw in many more to seek this gift, for God’s Love cannot be taken that the well will dry, for it is eternal. It is eternal. God bless you, my beloved souls. I am your teacher Augustine. I pray with you for these moments together. God bless you. My love, my love is with you too.
