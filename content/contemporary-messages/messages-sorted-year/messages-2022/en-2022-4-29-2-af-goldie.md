# There are no Flags in Heaven

April 29<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Holland America Cruise

Goldie received by Al Fike

I am Goldie. I do not wish to disappoint my dear friend, but there are no make-believe flags in heaven or any other flag. There are no countries, just the country of God. So when you move into, through the gates of heaven, you must remove all your insignias, be naked to God and joyed to do so.

May God bless you all on that journey. I love you. Yes, today is a day of joy. Be joyed, my friends, for you are in the Grace of God, and you walk in the Light of God, and you are loved by God. What more could one want but to be in God’s Grace and Love and Light? God bless you. Goldie loves you. God bless you.
