# We are All Part of the Coming Transition of the World

May 29<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Online Prayer Circle, Gibsons, BC, Canada

Jesus received by Al Fike

I come, beloveds, I am Jesus. I am your brother. I come to acknowledge you all in the great Light of Truth, which is the Father’s Touch upon your soul. I come because I stand with you in your struggles and in your triumphs, in your daily life, as you reach closer to God, as you make those efforts and prayers to be in God’s grace, to have your soul opened to the inflowing of His Essence. I come because I love you. I love you as true brothers and sisters. We are part of this family, this family of love. So in this unity, we bring greater light to your world, the world that continues to be in flux and is trending towards great change and transition from old to new, from the old conditions of humanity to a New World, where all of these unfortunate choices and actions of humanity will fall away, beloveds, will fall away in time.

The guidance of God, and all the angels and your stellar friends and the bright spirits will be close, will help uplift humanity in this great transitional period. So you must, beloved souls, continue to grow and awaken in love. You must prepare yourselves for what is coming and what God may use of you in your gifts, your presence, your light in the world. There is a plan, a great plan that will be enacted upon your world so that the opportunities and Portals and Lattice of Light, the great fabric of the children of God, will be laid upon the world. Each and every soul upon this world will come to know that they are loved and will come to choose love - or not.

It is a wondrous time upon your Earth plane, and each of you has been called and many more shall be called to be a part of something magnificent - a great healing of your world, a healing of all within it where your prayers are answered, beloved souls. Your prayers for this world will be answered in ways and means that will astound you, will bring wonderment to you, will bring great joy as you see truly, God in action. His magnificent and wondrous blessings upon this world will manifest in dramatic ways, in subtle ways, in gentle ways, and in powerful ways. God’s intention for the world will come to be, and will change this world forever, beloved souls, you are at the beginning of a great transition, and you shall witness its forthcoming within the world and be a part and play your part in this wondrous unfolding of God’s Will for this beloved Earth.

May God bless each and every one of you, and know that I know each of you. I know you well, and I have prayed with you, beloveds. I have been with you, each one, and I have whispered truth into your ear. I have come in prayer and desire to see you uplifted, to see you come into the great and magnificent Light of the Father’s Love, more fully and richly, and to be transformed by this great gift. We are truly brothers and sisters in this world, and I am with you, beloveds, I am with you in love.

God bless you. I am Jesus, Master of the Celestial Kingdom, and your humble brother. I humbly ask that you follow the way of truth and be a channel of Light in the world. Beloved souls, you are needed and shall always be, for the world is in great need and there are those who walk in light who may fulfill God’s intention to touch and bless His wayward children. May you truly hear the call that God has for each of you and know His Will within your heart and your mind, and act with the love of your soul with each day as God’s instruments.

Blessings to you, beloveds, blessings of love. I am with you. God is touching your souls at this very moment. May you open to this gift and touch. May you feel the expansion of your souls, the power of His Touch upon each of you, beloveds. Beloveds, be at peace and know that God is with you at this moment. God bless you. I love you. God bless you.
