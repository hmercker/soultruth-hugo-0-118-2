# Your Perceptions are Limited by Your Minds

March 25<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Monroeville, PA, USA

Jesus received by Al Fike

…souls, I come; I am Jesus. May the torrents of God’s Living Waters flow upon you, engulfing you in love. May you drink deep, these Waters. May you feel the closeness of God’s Presence and the peace that passes all understanding. May all these blessings be yours, my beloved brothers and sisters. For, with your prayers and endeavors and desires, so these blessings come in all their bounty and beauty. They come to awaken you. They come to uphold you. 
 
So, you speak to one another about your journeys, how you struggle, how you triumph upon this journey towards God. And it is like looking through some device that gives you a small portion of the picture of the truth and you stare intently through this peephole towards the truth. Yet, there is so much more to know, to come to understand, to truly live and awaken to. You will come to understand and awaken upon this journey of love. 
 
Beloved souls, have patience with yourselves, have compassion for your struggles, have faith in your journey, and release all your doubts and cares and worries and be in the joy and the wonderment that is God’s Blessing upon each of you. You cannot know all the truth. We do not know all the truth. But that truth that you know, may you hold it close to your bosom, may you live it and breathe it and be in alignment with it. For, as you embrace the truth and live it in all that you do and all that you are, so this allows the opening of greater truth, so this allows God’s Blessings to flow more readily upon you and within you. 
 
None of you are lost, beloveds. All of you have found what your souls have been looking for, what God has been desiring for you and blessing you in His answers to your prayers. Be joyed with this, be encouraged that you have found your way and that upon this journey, all progress that you make, all strides forward will not be removed. There will be no setbacks for you, merely another step and another step until your strides and your pacing become so quickened with love that you will sprint into the arms of God and know the great joy of this, the expansive understanding and truth of it, the true knowings of your soul bringing to you the gift of great wisdom and truth. God has His Hand firmly upon you, beloved children, and we are close, very close.
 
So when you seek us, when you desire for us to be with you, know that it is just a breath away, that indeed, we are within your understanding and experience and truth merely by releasing your doubts and your belief that you cannot know or truly comprehend our presence. For, it is your choice and your opportunity that determines whether you know us as you wish to know us, for we can come close enough and make our presence known if you desire this and wish to be enlightened. . 
 
Beloved souls, each of you have many gifts, each of you carry the truth of God’s Love within your hearts and souls and minds. What more do you require to know the truth, to experience the truth, to be in alignment with the truth? So we are together, and together we shall journey forward and be in harmony with all that is meant to be, all that is God’s Will for you and through you to the world. It comes in increments, faltering steps, challenges and tests, trials and tribulations, but it comes nonetheless, beloveds. It cannot help but come and move forward within your consciousness and your beings, because you seek the Great Healer, the Great Catalyst, the transformation of your soul through the Father’s Essence residing within you. 
 
So you will know and so you can know. So, all that you believe cannot be, is but your fear and your illusions. For all can be, all will be in your consciousness and experience of God as you continue to grow in this Love and awaken in your soul. These things shall come and come in accordance with what is in harmony, what is meant from God as a gift to you personally. It shall come and you will know and not doubt again, but truly know, beloved souls. 
 
Be blessed. Be blessed in the great Touch of God upon you, awakening, awakening all of who you are and what you can be. Beloved souls, you are loved. You are loved. God bless you. I am your brother and friend, Jesus. I come to be with you all in this time of prayer and blessings. God bless you. God bless you, beloved souls.
