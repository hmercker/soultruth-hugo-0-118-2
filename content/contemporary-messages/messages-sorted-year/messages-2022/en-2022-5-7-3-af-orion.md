# Announces His Presence and Other Stellar Beings Here to Help Us on Earth

May 7<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Blackpool, UK

Orion received by Al Fike

…you, my friends; I am Orion. I come from another place, a place that is within God's Creation, which is beautiful and in harmony with His Laws of Love. The great edict of God is that all must progress, and that within the flow of progression there is transformation, there is change, there is the flow and intention towards greater harmony.

You are given the gift and the understanding of how transformation of the soul may be accomplished. You are seeking the blessing of God, the Source of all creation, and that God has opened the way for every soul to avail themselves of this transformational experience and journey towards greater light and harmony, towards the awakening of those aspects of the soul that cannot be awakened in any other way. So you are accepting this great edict from God, that all must be upon the journey of transformation towards greater harmony and greater expression.

I encourage you my friends, as all have encouraged you, to continue on this journey and make it a conscious choice; the shifting of your priorities and your perceptions towards the goal of life, which is this eternal awakening, harmonization in love, the power of love that fuels this journey.

Beloved souls, each of you have great potential, are filled with many possibilities. Each of you have gifts and abilities that you have no conception of at this time. But as you continue on this journey of transformation, you will come to know these things within you, and use them and manifest them, to bring greater light to the universe, greater harmony and alignment with God's plan and intention in His creation, His firmament.

Where I live, we are far along this path, understand the journey more fully, and have expressed many potentials within our souls and yet, we are not finished. We are as you are, upon an eternal journey. So the transformation will never end, and you will become, and become again and again, what you are meant to be, and that will change as time progresses, as you progress through the eternal flow of God's Will and Intention for His children.

His children inhabit many places in the universe. There are many souls scattered throughout, and your time will come soon. Humanity will come to know this, understand their place within it, and you will see yourselves differently. The limitations of your thinking will be removed, and you will come to know great truths, great understanding of your place within the universe God. We will come to assist you in this. We will walk with you and show you the way beyond the disharmony that exists today in your world, to a place of greater harmony and truth. God's plan ensures that all His children, all the souls of the universe, will find harmony. The enactment of His plan has many aspects to it, and we are but one part of this great unfolding intention of God to bring humanity into harmony and light.

May you find your place within this plan. May you know the eternal journey of awakening and accept the gift of each day that will bring some aspect of this awakening to your conscious self, so that you may journey smoothly towards greater light and harmony and love, towards greater truth, greater understanding and meaning, as to the purpose of life.

There are many forces arrayed around you that are intent on helping you and uplifting you. We are but one of many. The angels of God continue to step forth and help you, assist you upon your journey. God continues to pour His Light upon you so that you are protected from the darkness, that you are given what you require in order to progress and awaken in love. So much is being given to those who are open and desire to step forward upon this journey. Each of you have angels by your side, and others who continue to dedicate themselves to God's intention and Will to have humanity uplifted and transformed.

So each of you will find your way in this. Each of you will come to a conscious awareness of the journey that you take and the purpose that comes with this journey. For each of you are uniquely gifted and have within you great potentials, the ability to assist your brothers and sisters in this world, to help uplift all and bring them towards greater light, harmony, peace, joy, love. Every breath is an opportunity to step forward in this way. Every moment challenges you to make that choice. May you come to accept the invitation that God has given to each of you to step beyond your present condition towards that which is of greater light and harmony, to be in greater alignment with the Great Soul, the Creator of all, and to truly, truly know the joy of this, the wonderment, the awakening that comes with the inflow of His Love into your souls.

This great truth is universal, and you have come to know it, my beloved friends. So you draw this great array of forces of beautiful souls to walk with you, to be close to you, to assist upon this journey. How blessed you are. But that blessing did not come arbitrarily. It came because you made the choice, you desire this, and so through your desire comes the fulfilment of God's laws. So with the fulfilment of God's laws comes the awakening of the soul and the transformation from within that will change all of you and make you into that beautiful, sweet soul, that being, that child of God that you are meant to be.

We are with you, my friends. We are with you, and our prayers are for your continued and speedy transformation into light, purity, and love. This is the key that will change the world. Blessings to you my friends, I am Orion, and I love you. You are surrounded by love, and you shall be as you come together in this circle, where this circle draws powerful forces into your midst. God bless you and keep you in love.
