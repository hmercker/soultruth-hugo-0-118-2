# This Country is in Great Need of our Prayers

May 5<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Blackpool, UK

Faith Nyquist received by Al Fike

This country is in need of your prayers. So many are suffering in their hearts and are in need of upliftment and hope and a refocusing of their perspectives and desires. The conditions in this place, in this country, are difficult. Such a concentration of thought and mindful perspectives and so very little of the soul. Though this is not unusual in the world, it is important that, in your prayers and efforts together, in these moments together, that you pray for this country. Pray for all the souls within this place so that there is a melting away of the conditions and darkness that is here. There are some on this island who have great metaphysical knowledge, knowledge of spirit, knowledge of the spirit body and all of those things of the natural order that they refer to as spirituality. But very few understand the deeper level of the soul and what the soul has to bring to the journey of spiritual awakening.

This is the message that you bring, my beloved and beautiful friends. This is the message we wish for you to bring, not only here but around the world, to help others to understand the power of the soul, the beauty of the soul, the awakening of the soul for God’s Love, so that they may see the world differently, that the reality that they carry now will be transformed into something that is more in harmony with the soul, and in harmony and alignment with God. This is not done through metaphysical teaching. It is done through prayer, through the deep feelings that come from within each individual, no matter the intensity and the difficulty that may come as the soul expresses itself.

Truly, this expression and release of difficult conditions opens the way, creates a crack within the soul where God may creep in, where that light might flow and start to work on the individual to transform and to heal. Whether that be through the natural way of love or the Divine Love, there is still a healing and an opening and a release and the individual travels toward light, greater light, greater understanding.

You carry a powerful truth. You are an example of this truth. You are beautiful lights in the world, and you must shine forth, my friends, shine forth as you encounter these conditions. Walk in faith, walk in trust of God’s Will and the flow of His Will. As you do so, you will find that you will be an agent of change, that you will be His instruments, His channels of love in the world and that many souls will be touched, not only in the flesh, but those in the spirit as well. For there are many spirits crowded within this place, this island, as well as those mortals, millions upon millions of souls in need of the release from the conditions, long established, long established by generations upon generations.

So there is a need for these patterns and paradigms of thought to be challenged and a crack within these long held beliefs and ideas, so that more light might penetrate, that there be a lightening and an uplifting and a healing for all your brothers and sisters who are close, who are in this place, in this vicinity of the world. Yes, the work is great, the challenges are great, but you are here because God has called you here.

So I ask you to pray for this place, pray for the peoples of this place, pray for the beautiful sanctuary that may be a beacon of light for many to be drawn to and benefit from. There is so much that may come of your efforts together. It can be powerful and it can be a catalyst for change. So I ask you to continue in your prayers, to pray as often as you might, to be vigilant, to be true to the truth that you know and are aware, and to be trusting of your own soul and the great power of your soul as God’s instruments in the world.

My dear brother Mandus had a great influence upon many because he trusted his gifts, he trusted in God, and he was guided. Here in this small group are powerful healers and souls of light. May you have the influence, may you carry the blessings, may all flow and awaken and bring the blessings of healing and peace and love. Each of you have great potential. It is a matter of stepping forward and trusting in the Will of God, the guidance of God, the blessings of God through you for the many. So it shall be in this world so in need, so in need of upliftment and grace and love.

Have compassion for your brothers and sisters. Have compassion for this place and the conditions that continue to hold humanity in its thrall, in the power of its embrace, which is not of light but often of darkness. God is using you every moment, every breath, as you are here to help neutralize these conditions. Be aware of this when you are sleeping, when you are praying, when you are conversing together. When you come together in light and harmony, much can be accomplished.

Beloved souls, you are embarking upon a great invitation and challenge to be God’s instruments here, to affect change here. Indeed, you will not see with your eyes and know with your mind, the effects to any great degree, but I tell you, the forces of angels have amassed with you in order to work with God, to be that great channel of light while you pray and are together. Have faith in this. For those who can see, we will show you. We will open the way for you to understand. Beloved souls, you are given a great gift to be together. May you indeed take full advantage of this gift and truly be together in light and harmony and love, truly in alignment with God’s Will.

May God bless you, beloveds. I am Faith. I am so joyed to see you here, to see each of you in light, such beautiful lights. Thank you. I thank you for your time and your efforts to be in this place together. Each who are coming are being prepared and indeed, much will be accomplished and manifest in your little group, beloved souls. May God bless you, bless you in light, bless you in light and love. I love you. God bless you.
