# Uses Every Opportunity to Minister to Dark Spirits

September 23<sup>rd</sup>, 2022

Online Circle of Light, Gibsons, BC, Canada

Jesus received by Al Fike

Beloved and beautiful brothers and sisters, I am Jesus. Know that when you pray together in any group or Circle of Light that many spirits are drawn to you. For as you gather and your sincere prayers build and are directed towards your Heavenly Father, so a light is built and a light is sustained by your efforts and your desires of the soul. Thus, we are able to be with you and pray with you in this light. As we do so, angels are engaged in bringing many spirits into your midst, surrounding you and observing you. Why would not God utilise every opportunity to help uplift the souls of humanity whether they be incarnate or discarnate? They are still souls seeking light, seeking truth. So we utilise these moments to teach and to heal and to uplift. These moments are common and are a part of every time that you pray together. Even now, there are many spirits in the spirit world who are observing and are being blessed as there are individuals whom you pray for, as there are your own souls being touched by the Father’s Love. These gifts and blessings come to each and every one of you and many, many more as you ignite the laws expressed in love and light in your world and in the world of spirit.

All dark spirits are given an opportunity to be in the light through your ministrations and ours and God’s Touch upon them. God’s mercy for all those who are in darkness is great. Our love for all souls, whether they are in darkness or light, is great. And we urge you, beloved souls, to have compassion and love for those who are lost and those who continue to be in the darkness. For it is a law that all souls of God’s Creation will find light in time. Indeed, your responsibility because you know the truth and you know the way towards light, is to express this truth, to demonstrate this truth and to share this truth with all those who are willing to listen, to observe and to be a part of this simple prayer to God.

Beloved souls, there are many listening at this moment and there are many listening at every moment you gather in prayer. When you pray by yourself, know that your loved ones are close and that there are other spirits close who are eager to be uplifted and to seek relief from their pain and their darkness. Many of you are unaware of the role that you play, how crucial this role is in the darkened world, for you seek light sincerely and you express light with a soul that is being ignited with God’s Love. So you become a channel of love in the world. So you will be known by many as a light. And so we work together, this great collaboration of you on the Earth plane and we in the Celestial Kingdom and those in the bright spirit spheres, all conspire to bring greater light to your world and to the worlds of the dark spirit spheres who are in need of light.

Continue with your efforts, beloveds. Continue to be clear and bright channels of love. Continue to study and apply these truths and walk in your life as an example of them. Be a channel of love, beloved souls, and in that you will attract many, those in light and those in darkness. Though, understand that you are protected from the dark spirits by we in the Celestial Kingdom and God’s Touch upon you and the light that shines within you. Do not fear that darkness. Do not be concerned for it, other than with love and compassion and prayers. For we all work to uplift humanity, do we not? We all seek to be a channel of love, do we not? So you play your part as do we as we continue to progress in the Father’s Love, in the light of truth, as an instrument of God. So we are together, brothers and sisters, in a great collaboration, a great journey to help uplift the world and uplift one another and uplift each and every one in light.

God bless you, beloveds. I am your brother and friend, Jesus. I come to be with you in light, to be with you in prayers. And not only you, beloved souls, but the many who gather around from the spirit world, for there are many and they seek and yearn for relief from their pain and darkness and confusion. So we make every effort, as do you, to bring light to our brothers and sisters. God bless you, beloveds, God bless you. My love is with you, beloved souls. My love is with you, with you all and your loved ones and many more in your world. God bless you.
