# We Are All Loved

July 5<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Online Prayer Circle, Gibsins, BC, Canada

Andrew received by Al Fike

No matter where you are upon this journey of soul awakening, know that there is and are blessings given equally to all of you. Some of you are more sensitive to this and can perceive this and others wish to know but often feel inadequate in the way of knowing. But this does not affect the quality and intensity of love that God has for each of you. And in that love comes the flow, the touch of light, of blessings, of peace that is meant for you to receive that showers upon you as you pray. Know that you are His children and that as His children you are safe, you are guided and protected. You will not lose your way, beloved souls, but that way will become more clear as you grow in the Father’s Love.

As you grow in your perceptions, the faculties of your soul, there will be clarity. The power of knowing, the knowing of your soul, will override all other speculations and thoughts of your mind regarding the truth, regarding any situation that you are in and the life that you now live. It is the power and wisdom of your soul that will guide you forward, will open the way to truth and greater truth, and as you pray, the peace that passes all understanding will envelope you. As you seek for the Father’s Love, so your soul will open to receive this gift. Through your soul you will know this, perceive it and feel it. Through your soul, the truth will shine through to your consciousness and bring you clarity. You will be empowered with truth, beloveds, empowered with love and light, bright, growing and expanding by the Touch of God.

Beloved souls, be in this light. Step out from the human condition into this light and make every effort to sustain yourself in this light, for it is your effort and our effort and God’s Touch that keeps you there. But when you falter, then you are vulnerable to the darkness and the pain and the conditions that are not in harmony with God and God’s Laws of Love. Choose to be in harmony, beloved souls. Choose to step into light always and to have faith that that light shall indeed envelope you, awaken you, and uphold you. You are loved. You are in the grace of God. You are beautiful souls, children of God. May the grace of God’s Touch be with you always. May the yearning and longing of your soul be strong and grow with each day. May you know the peace that passes all understanding and live within that peace. Beloveds, live within that light, live within the truth, and you will be protected from all that is not of light and truth and love. The grace of God, may it always be upon you. God bless you, beloveds. I am Andrew and I pray with you today. God bless you all.
