# Stepping Forward in Service

March 28<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Monroeville, PA, USA

Mylora received by Al Fike

Blessings of God’s Love be with you, my beloved friends. I am Mylora, and I come to be with you in these prayers, in your sacred times together, these times so precious now, such a gift. Beloved souls, each of you may be great channels of light in your lives, in this world. Do you not see this within you, this soul that you carry that is you, that has such great potential, that God is nurturing and blessing with every day so that it may grow and expand into that mighty, mighty expression of God’s Creation, with all its beauty and potentials, all its love and glory and light?

You are each this soul, this precious soul that is meant to go out into the world and be a channel of light and love for all that you meet. So, as you leave this place, may you see yourself in this light, in this way, and step forward into the world. I know it is often safest to be at home in the quietness, in the safety, in the light of your own home, and God can use you even there in many ways. But it is important that you are amongst your brothers and sisters, walking freely as a light so that God may touch many other souls.

Do not look inward, but outward, beloveds. I know you have the blessing and safety of this beautiful circle and your beautiful friends, and indeed it is wonderful that you pray together and are together in love. In this blessing, you are strengthened, you are in greater light and love. And in this blessing, you are given the empowerment to go forth in the world, to be in the world as God’s channels. See the world, see the world as many doors are awaiting to be opened. See the world as a place where God is guiding you, that you may be used in your instrumentality, your gifts, for the blessings of others. See the world as God created it and have compassion for all those who are asleep, who are ignorant, who are in fear, who do not know the way of love.

Be true to love in all the ways and means that love is expressed and given in your life. Do not let the darkness impede your efforts, but be true and strong in light and we will be with you. You are protected and you are guided, and we are with you, all of you, on this journey.

Disciples of the Master, this is your role, to bring the truth that he brought, to express the truth that he has taught, to be the instrument that God is continually developing within you, to be strong in light, joyful in light and love, beloved souls. The world needs your presence, the world needs your gifts, the world needs your love. And as you continue to walk in the world in this way, so God brings His powerful blessings upon you and through you. See the opportunities that God has put before you, my beloved friends. Allow the workings of His Great Soul to manifest through your soul, and the light grows and grows. Despite all the darkness, the light grows. It shall someday eradicate all that is not of light, is not of God.

Know the freedom of God’s great blessing upon you. Beloved souls, you are so loved, you are so loved, beloveds. I am your friend, Mylora, and I love you. I am close to many of you. God bless you in this peace, in this love. God bless you.
