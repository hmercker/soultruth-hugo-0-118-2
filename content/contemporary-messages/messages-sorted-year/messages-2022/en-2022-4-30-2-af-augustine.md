# Reassures Us of Celestial Presence

April 30<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Holland America Cruise

Augustine received by Al Fike

Do not be surprised at the collaborations that may come into your midst as you continue to pray for and open to the Will of God and the opportunity to serve God. For God will bring forth many different individuals, and also souls, who reside on different planets in this universe.

As you reach higher, so what comes will be somewhat different. It may challenge your thinking. It may come as a surprise. But indeed, when you are encountering such things, I urge you to confirm within your soul that there is harmony and love. For we will not bring close to you those things that are not harmonious, and loving, and part of the flow of truth. As for individuals you may encounter on the earthly plane, their thinking may be different from yours. They may not have such clarity of soul and light within their souls, but they are light-filled beings whose intentions are to bring harmony and light to this world.

Indeed, you are very blessed to have your angel friends close, to have God’s Touch upon you, to have the Light of His Love within you, and all of these blessings that come with His Love. Not many upon your world are so blessed. But indeed, as we continue to assist you in your travels, and your efforts, your prayers, your Circles of Light, you will draw in many to this truth and this love.

As you are well-aware, every soul must begin at the beginning, not progressed and enlightened, but rather in a state of need, semi-darkness, but filled with desire and hope, and willing to go beyond their mindful restrictions and fears. These souls you must reach out to. Do not judge or feel reticent to reach out to these individuals which God places upon your path. Rather, to allow the flow of His Love through you, to allow the flow of truth through your lips, to allow light to engulf you and to engulf them.

Conversely, within the world of spirit, there are many who are not well-developed spiritually, who are close and observing you, and have a deep desire to learn. So, they too may come close. Although we will protect and ensure that you are not vulnerable to conditions and forces that are not of the highest. So you need not worry, unless you overtly and mindfully attract such influence. Keep your thoughts upon the highest, beloveds. Keep your thoughts upon God and your desires to be awakened within your souls, and to be close to God’s great Soul. In this way, all will be in harmony with truth and you will not succumb to some ruse by spirits who wish to manipulate and bring you into lower levels of consciousness. This will not be the case, beloveds, you will be safe. You pray for the cloak of protection. You pray to be used for the highest good and purpose. You pray for God’s Love to flow within your soul. These prayers are heard by God, and the responses which God gives are that of light and harmony and truth.

Beloved souls, you are in the Embrace of God and you shall be in the Grace of God. No matter where you may be or where you may go, you shall be in God’s Embrace and know the harmony of this, and the blessings of this, and the peace and the joy of this. Be assured, beloveds, that all is well upon the journey, the journey of love, the journey of peace and joy. There is much awaiting each of you, awakenings that will come, truth that is waiting for your understanding, and for you to receive and comprehend and absorb.

God’s plan is great and expansive and powerful. God shall indeed ensure that you walk within His Will. God’s blessing is yours, beloveds, is yours. You are on the threshold of greater light and understanding, and shall always be, provided you desire this and your will is in harmony with the highest.

God bless you, beloveds. I am your teacher, Augustine. I am with you. Many are with you to ensure you are protected and guided. Be at peace. Be true to your journey towards light, towards the highest, the greatest blessing of all, which is God’s Love. God bless you. God bless you.
