# Awaken to the Callings of Your Souls

April 27<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Ceuta, Morocco, North Africa

Joseph received by Al Fike

May God bless you, beloved souls; I am Joseph. The awakening of the world is what is God’s plan, and that plan is unfolding in your world. All the world sleeps in the slumber of human conditions and illusions, and so it is for you, who are brave and strong and faithful, to awaken to God. In so doing, you assist God in awakening the world. For, as the flame of truth in alignment with God continues to burn within you, so it is a clarion call to your brothers and sisters to also awaken. You may not speak of this clearly and succinctly to all you meet, but still, within you is that call of the soul and it is reverberating with other souls around you, and each soul desires their alignment with God. Though humanity is upon a course that is not in alignment with God, still within them is a great desire to do so, to be with God, to be in the light, to be in harmony with God’s laws, God’s laws for the material world, the spirit world, and His Laws of Love.

You have a great challenge ahead of you, my friends, for you are a stranger in a strange land. You are in the world, but not of the world. You are different, and so your perceptions, your experiences, your journey reflect this. Many look upon you as a curiosity, but they do not see any deeper than what their minds and conditions may allow them. You must have compassion for your brothers and sisters, for they are mostly blind, dumb and deaf, cannot understand.

So, my beloved friends, you continue to seek the truth and to live the truth as best you can, to seek for the awakening of your souls, to walk in the Light of God’s Truth, to continue to serve God in whatever way you can. That is all that we can ask of you, my beloved brothers and sisters. You cannot do the work of God single handedly. It is important that you gather your forces, each individual soul willing to be a part of this great Tapestry of Light that God has built upon this world.

You continue to pray and reach out to one another and to others, to be that channel in the world. Though many do not understand consciously, they will know within them that you bring a message, a message that they are loved, a message that God wishes to communicate with each of His children, a message that God is guiding humanity towards light and greater harmony. That message may be given by your glance upon another, your smile, your very presence with those who are around you, but you cannot expect them to consciously acknowledge what is so very far away from their understanding of life.

This is where you must have faith and compassion, my friends. You must continue to walk in this light, adhere to God’s Will, and carry the flame of truth, not seeking the acknowledgment of others, but seeking the acknowledgment of God. In this way, you are in alignment with your Creator. You walk in the light of His Will, His Love, releasing those mindful ideas and obligations, freeing yourselves from the expectations that come with a mind and a desire to change the world, to light the world in truth. Indeed, these are noble aspirations, but they cannot be fulfilled in the way that you see and desire. Rather, with faith, as you follow God’s Will and are in alignment with God’s plan, so the doors open and the opportunities are presented to do the work that is laid before us all.

This plan is vast and it runs deep in the veins of humanity, but it is not yet time for their awakening, for the ground is still being laid and God’s plan is still finding its footing upon the Earth plane. Thus, you must be patient. You must allow the unfoldment of events and opportunities, gifts, and the interconnectivity of souls, as God builds His Lattice of Light upon the world, strengthens His Portals of Light, makes for the infrastructure of light to be powerful and present.

The world is aware of this call, the call for the souls to awaken. You are aware, but your awareness is conscious where theirs is not. So you must wait for that time, that opening where the groundwork has been set and the time for action will be clear to each of you. Until then, I urge you to carry on in your efforts, your prayers, and your reaching out to many. This continues to strengthen the message and in its way, helps to open the doors that God intends to open to all humanity. In many ways, you are strengthened by these efforts. You practice your gifts and instrumentality, and this refines your ability, your God-given gifts, and helps to awaken your soul.

Walk in peace, my beloved friends. Be in the peace of God’s Presence and seek the gift of His Love, and all else will follow. God bless you, beloved souls. I am Joseph. My love for you is great, my beloved and beautiful friends upon the Earth plane, seeking their place in the Celestial Heavens, journeying towards at-onement with God. You are blessed, beloveds, and shall continue to be blessed, guided, protected in the Light of His Love. God bless you. God bless you.
