# Pray for Those in the Light

July 19<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Online Prayer Circle, Gibsons, BC, Canada

Faith Nyquist received by Al Fike

Beloved friends, I am Faith. I come to pray with you and be with you as I am with our beloved sister, who is working in that place where I dwelt for some time in my life and served God.

God’s servants are everywhere. Even though they may carry a measure of error or misunderstanding about God’s intentions, within their hearts is a deep desire to serve. So you must include those in your prayers, those who are working to bring light and comfort to others, and there are many. Many within churches, many within organizations, many individuals who walk in the world with a desire to uphold and uplift their brothers and sisters. Pray for these individuals. Consider them you brother and sister. Consider that God is using them as He is using you. In some respects, there are those who have deeper faith and powerful gifts than you do.

The world is full of diversity. The world is full of many ideas, perceptions and conceptions of what life is, what God is, what they are. Yes, so many ideas. So many religions and perspectives. Yet, underneath all of this blanket of thought and intention, is the soul seeking liberation and love, seeking connection with God. Some are fortunate enough to have this connection clearly and beautifully. Some merely rely upon prayer to ensure that they are connected. Others, many others are lost, have no inkling or idea of how to connect with God or who God is, what God is.

Your prayers are needed for all of these beautiful souls, the ones that are in light and the ones who are not. For the world is in need of any who are willing to give of their hearts and give of their prayers, asking for the blessings of God upon this world, a world so in need of these efforts, a world that is desperate for the Touch of God.

How blessed you are, my friends. How very blessed you are to have that clarity of mind, that intensity of soul longing, that expression of your being towards God in such a way that His Touch, and Light, and Blessings flow readily to you. You are invoking the laws that God has created for such things. You are indeed understanding and many of you are aware when these blessings come and what these blessings are. How fortunate you are, beloveds. May you pray for the clarity and wisdom and understanding to come to many. For, if this blessing is within many, how different the world would be, how the clouds of darkness would disperse, and the light of love would shine freely in your world.

May you be blessed within this Light of God’s Love. May you open readily and freely to the Holy Spirit that carries this love. May you truly know that you are being blessed and feel the intensity of God’s Touch upon you, so that you may be inspired, uplifted, strengthened, and fully in light, releasing all of that which is not of light, that those things that you have carried, that are of error, that burden you, that are of pain, may fall away readily, and you are truly in the joy of God’s Love, in the joy of knowing and being and feeling all that is of the soul awakening and God’s Touch upon it.

God bless you, my friends. I am Faith, and I pray with you today, a privilege and a blessing to do so. God bless you. My love is with you. My love is with all of who seek light and know light and express light. God bless you and keep you in His Light.
