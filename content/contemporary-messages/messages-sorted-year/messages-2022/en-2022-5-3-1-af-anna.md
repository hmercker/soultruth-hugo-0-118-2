# The Work in Circles of Light Continues 

May 3<sup>rd</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Barcelona, Spain

Anna received by Al Fike

I am Anna. We have been drawing you into all your Circles of Light that you are a part of. We have been utilizing the connections that you share amongst your brothers and sisters, so that the work that you have in your daily routine is not interrupted by your travels.

Although you may not know this and feel this because of the encumbrance of your fleshy body affecting your subtle perceptions and awarenesses of the spirit, nonetheless you are being used in many ways. Whether that awareness comes to you or not, it is still a truth. I wish to emphasise that when you are on these travels, these treks to embrace others in the world, that your work is not interrupted at home. Your connections with others that you have made over the years are not severed as you are so far away from their presence.

Beloved souls, there is no distance between souls when the love bond is created and is strong. So you will find that you may do both the work that you have dedicated yourselves to over the years, and the routines that have been established, and those travels that you make, may come together in harmony. In time, you will know and understand this, as there are many more travels awaiting each of you. As you are called, so the way is open for this sort of service, which is very important and necessary in the world.

You see about you in a city that is so entrenched in materialism and in the many ways that have been established over hundreds of years, that on the surface it may appear that nothing different has happened or been expressed in regards to spirituality. But below the surface much has happened. There has been the effect of your light and your instrumentality as a channel of God’s Love and Light, have affected many souls. These effects may not emerge for a long time, but the imprint upon the soul has been made and therefore, a door awaits to be opened. Those dear souls may choose to open or not, depending upon their free will, but you have been used to imprint this door upon the soul. Therefore, they have the opportunity, at some point, to come to this truth.

We cannot, nor can you, interfere or impress upon another this truth so that they may choose to explore it and to understand it. But in the realms of the soul, there are possibilities and connections that can be made, soul to soul, that have nothing to do with the mind. In this way, as time progresses, as the individual experiences their life and progresses through the material world and the spirit world, this gift that has been given will eventually emerge and come to consciousness. It may come to the individual quickly and overtly, or it may take centuries of time, but it is not for you to judge or have expectations in regards to this process. As you are well aware, this is God’s plan for the salvation of humanity. All you can do is be His instrument, to walk in the world as His instrument, and to be open to His guidance, to walk in the Light of His Love always, to have love for all you meet. God will touch those souls. God knows best what is for their souls. God will bless those souls accordingly.

I urge you to walk in faith, to have great peace in your hearts, and allow God to do His work by ensuring that you are in light, that your thoughts are that of the highest, that your intentions are that of service and love. In this way, you open the door for God to work and for us to come close. You know this when you are in harmony, you feel this alignment with all who are close to you in the spirit, God’s Touch fueling your footsteps upon the world. It is a simple process, but one which is inhibited by both your physicality and mind, and that of the human condition. So, these things are not readily accomplished. They take a great deal of focus and dedication from each of you in order to be in alignment.

So, I urge you to monitor your thoughts, to be in harmony with yourself and with others, to be true to the truth, and think of God often. Think of your relationship with God, how much God loves you. Think of your relationship with we in the Celestial realms and how much we love you. Think of this world as a place where every footstep has the potential for you to be an instrument for God, to be a channel of love for God. As I have said, you may not be aware of what God is doing, how He may utilize you as His instrument, but if your mind is clear of any obstructions and you continue to pray and align yourself to God, then all light may flow, all blessings may come. You will find yourself walking in the world as a light, that love will flow, that all will be in harmony, that you will be protected and guided, that you will be free of the worries and cares that so many of your brothers and sisters carry in this world.

Each day is a day of practice, of making effort, of focus, of prayerful desire, of loving intentions, so that God may utilize you more fully with each day that passes, as you become more attuned and in greater alignment with this truth and your own unique instrumentality. You will work for God in many ways. Some of them you will know and many, many opportunities that have been realized will not be known.

Beloveds, every breath is a breath for God. Every moment is a dedication to service. Every part of you may be utilized to help awaken humanity. For, there is much within you, beloved souls, much that is still waiting for the transformation of love within, that you may truly be that clear and powerful instrument for God. Have trust and faith in God’s intentions for you. His Love for you will indeed, heal all that is within, and remove that which is not in harmony with love. God continues to minister to you through His angels, through the touch of His Spirit upon you.

So, you are continuing on a progressive journey, a journey for all eternity to at-onement and harmony and alignment with God. So it flows, beloveds. If you look back upon your life, you can see the progress you have made. As you look forward, have great hope and desire for all that is coming and all that is needed of you. May you walk upon the world with the intention of being God’s instrument, of being strong, and clear, and beautiful in love. So you will be, and much will come of it.

May God bless you, beloveds, upon your own journey, through your own unique passage of life, and the instrumentalities and blessings and gifts that come with it. May God’s Love continue to awaken you and strengthen you and beautify you in light and love. I am your friend, Anna. I am happy to be with you today and will be with you for this day. God bless you, beloveds. God bless you deeply and beautifully in love.
