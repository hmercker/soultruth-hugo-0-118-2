# You Have Sacred Truths Within You

April 18<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Holland America Cruise

Jesus received by Elizabeth Mack

I am the son, the first son of our Father. Yes, I am here. I am Jesus. I am with you often, each one of you, leading you home. We are gifted to be joining you and blessing you with the Father’s Love. For, it is true. You have a sacred truth within you to share with your brothers and sisters, wherever you go. It is my wish that you do so, that we may bring our siblings along with us to reach the Father’s Kingdom. We have been told many times that there is room for all of us. Yes, it is the Father’s Will that all join with Him in this truth, in this Love.

We ask that you be open to giving this love, sharing this love, to all whom you meet and bringing them, yes, the joy that our sister spoke of this morning. We have much to be thankful for, and it is the Father’s Will that it be shared among all His children and you all have been chosen to do this. We are grateful that you are cooperating. You will see more benefit as your days pass, doing the will of the Father, reaching high to bring about this transformation.

We thank you for taking this message at this time. We love you. God bless each and every one of you with His Love.
