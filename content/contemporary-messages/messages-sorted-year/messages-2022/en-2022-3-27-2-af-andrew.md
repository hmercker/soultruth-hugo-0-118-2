# Our Relationship with God

March 27<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Monroeville, PA, USA

Andrew received by Al Fike

…souls, I am Andrew. I come to thank you for your efforts, your prayers, your reaching out to one another, claiming one another in love. How important it is in your world to reach out in love and acceptance and recognition as you continue to awaken to your own beings. To do so in such a way that you are a part of a great Circle of Light, a great Family of Love, strengthens the light in your life and in this world.

God is both that Quiet Whisper that says, *“I love you”* and the mighty Creator that has created the universe. God cannot be defined in words but in who God is, in all God’s Manifestations and Spirit, and most of all the existence of God as Soul. Soul is without form, beloveds. Soul is soul. God says, *“I Am”* and so God is, has always been. You may put whatever label you wish upon God and yet this is inadequate, does not fully define or express or bring to wholeness, a definition. Your understanding of God will come with every time you are in communion, every time you open your heart, every time you have a recognizable experience that will bring you closer to that soulful awareness, your soul to God’s Soul, your love to God’s Love.

So in your world, in this material place full of words and thoughts, there are great difficulties and barriers to truth because of the impediments of these conditions and the human condition. But each of you struggles and yearns and make great effort towards understanding and communion with the Creator of all. So this struggle continues with each of you. So the joy and openness of this experience shall grow and expand as you continue to be awakened within your soul. God brings every opportunity forward for you to do so. There is nothing from God that impedes this journey, this building and forging of a relationship between your soul and God’s Soul.

May you find an opening, a sense, a feeling, a perception, a welling up of joy and love that will add to and bring about a greater awareness of God. For, it is all these things within you which for many of you become fleeting and temporary, but will in time be the foundation of your awareness, where God becomes a powerful and consistent part of your conscious self, and that all the speculation, all the thoughts that run through your minds are absorbed by the very presence of the I Am, which is God. In this way, you will know. In this way, all those parts of you that doubt, are confused and do not understand, will be healed and integrated and become a whole; a whole that is complete as much as it can be complete at any given moment, this awareness of God, this knowing of God, this love of God.

For, God’s intention for each one of you is that you come to know the very nature of the Being that is God, that you come to be in alignment with this, that you are truly satisfied and gratified by this knowing, and that in this knowing comes a joy so deep and resonant that you cannot but be a beautiful child, a creation of God. It is in the great gift of the Essence of Soul that is God, that you will come to know and be in this perfect place of knowing and being.

Beloved souls, you know the way and the journey to that place. You have shared this journey together for some moments in your life, and this can only but reinforce and strengthen the journey and the knowing. Do not let thoughts interfere with your love of one another. Know that you are beyond the words, that who you are is incapable of words and thought, but is truly the expression of God’s Creation in the universe, that unique creation which you are and shall always be; loved, nurtured, awakened, strengthened, growing exponentially and eternally in the great awareness of love, the Great Soul of God nurturing you for all eternity. This connection, this way, this wonderment is why you come together. What motivates you to travel from afar to make that effort to affirm to one another, *“I am loved by God. I know God and shall continue to discover evermore of who and what God is.”*

Be in the flow of His Will. Be upon the journey of the awakening, the building and forging of such a connection, such a relationship, that all other connections and relationships will be secondary to this one great and wondrous gift that God wishes for you to know and be within this great reality which is God.

My beloveds, beautiful friends, may you journey forth from this place with greater faith, wisdom and knowledge that God is truly with you at every moment, that God truly loves you beyond any understanding and capacity to know, that you are the child of the Creator, that you are His most beloved. All comes in this awakening of the soul, in the knowing of the soul and the joy of the soul.

Beloved, beloved friends, we thank you for your time here, for all that you do for one another and all that you do together, and all that you will do for your brothers and sisters in the world and all whom you encounter. May there be love, an expression of this truth. May you continue to unfold in this expression. May you continue to act and be a reflection of the highest of all truth, the greatest of all gifts, and that is love.

God bless you, beloveds. I am Andrew and I am with you. I am with you in these efforts. I am with you in love as are many, many beautiful angels who stand by you at this moment and shall be with you as you journey in life, because you have drawn, through your desires and efforts and prayers, the great blessing of love that ensures that you are guided, protected, loved and cared for by the angels of God. God bless you, beloveds. God bless you on your journey. My love is with you, beautiful souls of love.
