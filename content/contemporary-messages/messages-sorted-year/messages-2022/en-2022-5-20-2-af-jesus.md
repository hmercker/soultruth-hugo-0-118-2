# Brings an Everlasting Gift to Our Souls

May 20<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Online Prayer Circle, Gibsons, BC, Canada

Jesus received by Al Fike

I come, beloveds, I come and I bring a gift, a blessing to each of you and to all who are connected to this beautiful Circle of Light. I come to bless you all and bring God's blessings to each of you, precious lights in the world, precious souls.

You are being given a gift, a gift to help ease your journey through life. A light that shall stay with you, beloveds, and protect you, and help you to understand and know God's Will and guidance for your life. This gift is enhanced by the light within your soul, and the gift that has been given to help you in your awakening. Much conspires to awaken you all, beloveds, to assist you upon this journey that you may be that beautiful light in the world, that you may fulfill God's Will for you, to be His instruments upon the Earth.

You are surrounded by angels. You are given every opportunity to grow in love. May you grasp each opportunity as it is given. May you spend your days and time contemplating God, contemplating your own soul, contemplating how you may live a life that is within the grace and harmony of God's Creation, that your souls may awaken and enliven with love. In this, the transformation that is intended may evolve and awaken in the journey that God has made for each of you. Your particular path to God, may this open wide, be bright and filled with inspiration and joy, beloved souls. You are blessed, and you are loved, and I love you. I shall always be with you. I am Jesus, and I am with you. God bless you, beloved souls. God bless you in His Light. God bless you in His Love. God bless you with every breath.
