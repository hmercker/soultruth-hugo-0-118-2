# We Enliven the Lattice of Light as We Pray for God's Love

August 8<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Online Prayer Circle, Gibsons, BC, Canada

Jesus received by Al Fike

Beloved souls, I come, I am your brother, Jesus. I come to pray with you as you have elicited my presence in your prayers and in your thoughts and your desires. I come as your elder brother. I come as I always do, to join you in prayer for the highest of blessings, the gift of God’s Essence, that it may flow into your souls, beloveds.

It is for you to open to this gift, for it is fully present and waiting for your desire to receive it. The Holy Spirit will convey this blessing to each of you, and God makes all provision for you to be blessed in this way. He invites you forth in prayer and desire of the soul so that you may be awakened and come to know the reality of God’s universe, the power of God’s Love that will open the doors, open the awareness, open all that is within you to this Truth. Each of you as you pray together, are enlivening the Lattice of Light in the world, and through all your connections, your love for others, those who are in your life, these strands of Light are connected to each one. So as we pray together, we enliven this beautiful lattice, and we bring forth, as God’s channels and instruments, blessings through these conduits of Light to your brothers and sisters.

As we grow as a community, as a group of like-minded individuals seeking the highest of truth, seeking to be a channel of love in the world, though these blessings will flow through all the ways and means that God has provided so that many may be blessed, so that there is the beginnings of an awakening upon your Earth, an awakening which God has waited for eons to take place. In God’s infinite patience and love for His children, He awaits the opportunity, the time, to awaken His children to the Truth. And you have heard His call. You have responded in love. You are open to God’s Will in your prayers. We are with you close, beloved souls. The angels of heaven, the bright spirits, and your Stellar friends all join in this prayer for your world and for own yourselves so that in time, the light that is expanding through your efforts and networks will encompass the globe and will bring truth to many. Be patient, beloved souls, consistent, and have great faith in the Will and plan of God to awaken humanity. Know that we are all as one in this great plan, that we are all working together to fulfill God’s intentions, God’s desire for the upliftment of humanity, the awakening of their souls to love, and that the Love of His great Soul may indeed cleanse and heal and transform all that which is within you, beloved souls. And that in this transformation, you will bring the truth to your brothers and sisters. You will be compelled to speak. You will be compelled to reach out in loving ways, in compassion and in joy, to your brothers and sisters. This is all part of God’s plan, that in one, the light grows and through that touching others and through them others, until the whole world is aglow in light.

Beloveds, can you not see the future of humanity? It is towards light and harmony and truth, this simple truth, this powerful Truth of Love encompassing all and healing all the wounds and the desperations, and all that holds humanity from light will dissipate and be transformed in light. We are on that road together, and we shall carry the banner of truth and light the way with our souls in alignment with God. We are His children and as such, we are brothers and sisters in the truth. Be assured, beloved souls, that all that God intends for this world will come to be. For God’s Will will be done upon this Earth as it is in heaven.

Blessed are you, beloved souls. Blessed is this world as each of you prays for its healing and its upliftment in light and harmony. So your prayers will be answered, so the world will be healed, so love will abound upon this world. Beloved souls, may you have the vision of the future that is coming and may you find great comfort and joy in this vision. We are all seeking to serve God in our own way, and so we shall. For now and forevermore, we shall be God’s servants in love. God bless you, beloveds, I am your brother and friend, Jesus. I embrace you all in love and I pray with you all, for your awakening and the awakening of this world. I love you, I love you dearly. I love you. God bless you, beloved souls, God bless you.
