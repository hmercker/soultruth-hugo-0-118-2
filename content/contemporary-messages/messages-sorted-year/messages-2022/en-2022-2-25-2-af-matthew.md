# Each One of Us Has a Purpose

February 25<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Online Prayer Circle, Gibsons, BC, Canada

Matthew received by Al Fike

Dear beloved souls, I am Matthew. There is purpose within each one of you, the purpose that can be gleaned as you awaken within your soul. For God has put within you something that only you alone can do and you alone can do in your own unique and beautiful way that is a reflection of the gifts of your soul, the gifts that all of you possess as a creation of God. Seek to know this purpose, for as you walk in the Light of God and receive His great Love, so this purpose will emerge. Maybe not as something so obvious and distinct, but it will come forth in various ways through various means and opportunities, channels for you that are reflective of your beautiful soul.

If all the souls upon your world were to reflect their purpose, it would be a very dynamic place in light and love in action and all things that are in harmony with God’s Will. For, that purpose is a reflection of God’s Will within you that you may, indeed, express what you were meant to do and to be in the world. This gives your soul great joy, my beloved friends, when you acknowledge this purpose within you and express it. Oh, such joy comes as a reflection of your alignment and agreement with God, to act and to be His instrument in this unique and beautiful way.

Each of you are blessed with many gifts. Each of you have much to offer to the world. May you come to find what it is that is within you that will reflect the beauty and wonderment of your unique soul so that as you progress through life and enter into the spirit world, you will look upon your life and see with great satisfaction and appreciation the gifts that God has given you and the purpose that God has implanted within you. There is much yet to be explored within your being, beloved souls, there is so much that may be expressed. And, indeed, as you continue to grow in His Love, you will find that these expressions will come more readily and this purpose will flow more freely in the Love and Light of God, in the Will of God. You will be within His great river, this flow that is your life and God’s intention for your life.

Beloved souls, may you be blessed with insight, with understanding, with acknowledging the gifts and beauty that you possess and truly be that wonderful child of God, a light in the world, a beautiful light, a channel of love. May God bless you, beloved souls. I am Matthew. I am happy to be with you today in prayer, to pray with you in love, in the blessings of God’s Love. God bless you and Matthew loves you. God bless you.
