# Step Forward in Service

March 28<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Monroeville, PA, USA

Jesus received by Al Fike

I come to be with you. May you continue to step forward in love, carry the flame of Truth wherever you go. Set aside all conditions that are contrary to the Laws of Love and don the robes that you have been given to signify your place as God’s instruments in the world. Extinguish all doubts and skepticism. Walk in the freedom and joy of the Truth.

Though the world may challenge you, when you are in alignment with God, these challenges cannot hurt or thwart you in your efforts. You are helping to usher in a new age of humanity. Be joyed that you are a part of this, that God has chosen you and that you have chosen God, so that the flow of His Will may work through each of you in wondrous and powerful ways, expressing the glory and truth and wonderment of Love, it’s transformation working within your souls and reflecting through you to the world.

May you be that beacon of the Light, beloved souls, that is not ever extinguished, but grows brighter, grows brighter in love, that your souls may shine forth in all its glory, in all its wonderment, and that you may carry this Light with your head high and your feet firmly upon the path of love. It is time for commitment, my beloveds. It is time to walk the path that God has designated for each of you. It is time to put aside all that which would distract you from this, for it is important that you seize the moment and walk the path.

Each of you has a role to play, a gift to give, a purpose to be expressed. May you find your way to all of these things as God’s Love lights your path and awakens you to that wondrous part, an aspect of your being that is emerging, insisting on its expression and full awakening in the world. In this you will know great joy, fulfillment, all that you have desired in your life will come to be in ways and expressions that you cannot imagine, but will find a fulfillment of it as you step forward time and time again, ever forward in light and truth as my disciples, beloveds, as my disciples. You will be given special dispensations and blessings to help guide and protect and strengthen you. Many angels will accompany you, many blessings will fall upon you and great Light shall be with you.

My beloveds, may you truly see with the eyes of your souls and know with your swelling hearts, the great joy and understanding of God’s plan for you and for the salvation of humanity. There is much work to do and few who are willing, but those that are will draw others who will join you. So the momentum grows and the light builds and the plan unfolds.

God bless you, beloveds, I am your brother and friend. I am with you, know this, that I am with each of you. Do not doubt but know this within your hearts and your minds and your souls, that you have me as your friend, your companion, your protector and your guide.

God bless you, beloveds, God bless you. We journey together always on this path of Love. I love you each, each a special place in my heart. I love you, beloveds, I am with you. God bless you.
