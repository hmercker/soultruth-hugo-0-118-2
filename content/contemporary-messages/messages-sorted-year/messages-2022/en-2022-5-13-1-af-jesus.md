# Opening Blessing for Portal at Les Chapel Monnaie

May 13<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Les Chapel Monet, Guernsey

Jesus received by Al Fike

May this place be a sanctuary of light for all those who come through its doors. May God’s Presence be here and great peace befall upon each one who may seek God’s blessings. I come, beloved souls; I am Jesus. I come to bless you, and I have called you, precious souls of the earthly plane. I call you to be lights in the world, to continue to seek to be lights in the world, and I urge you to open yourselves to God, to awaken to God more deeply with each day. In this way you seek for the highest of blessings. In this way, you will find yourself in the flow of God’s blessings and intentions for each of you, opening the door to your instrumentality and gifts, awakening deep and beautiful things within you. As God’s Love pours into you, so all comes awakened and harmony ensues with this blessing.

As my dear brother has said, I spoke this Truth years ago and I continue to teach this Truth even now and forever more. For it is the power of Love, the blessing of Love, the awakening that comes with Love that shall change this world. And I come with a message that the world is changing rapidly, that those things of the past are beginning to fall away and a new way of being in the world is emerging. It is emerging in the hearts of humanity and it is pushing and making efforts to emerge from every heart, every individual. For, the time of man’s domination of the mind and with the mind, is fast falling away and the time of soul awakening is coming, beloved souls. And yes, these places of light are important and powerful centers to help incite this great change and transformation of humanity, and God’s blessings are upon you all, beloveds.

Those who wish to pursue their relationship with their Creator will find powerful openings and awakenings in their consciousness and will know great peace and joy, will know the power of God’s Love, the Essence of His Soul being poured into your soul, the living waters of God to quench your thirst, to open and awaken the senses of the soul, the knowings of the soul, the wisdom of the soul.These things come with this blessing, and God is pouring His Light and blessings upon this world. For, now is the time to shift, to shift from the will of man to the will of God in ways that will bring great harmony and love and peace to the world.

It is coming beloveds, and I tell you, each of you may be an instrument to bring this about. It will not be through powerful mechanisms or words, it will be through love. It will be through light and your ascribing to the Truth of Love, and in this way you will touch many souls, beloveds, many souls. You will draw to you the angels and the bright spirits and the beautiful entities of other worlds that come to assist humanity to make this great leap of consciousness, a leap towards greater love and harmony in the world. So much is coming, beloveds, and we come to usher in a new age, an age of love, an age of light, and enlightenment, enlightenment of the soul so that all may know from that place, the journey of life and the purpose of life.

Beloved souls, may you come to open yourselves to this great blessing and know God’s Touch deeply. Awaken to this, awaken my beloved souls, beautiful brothers and sisters of the world. I urge you to awaken to your deeper selves so that all may come, as I have said. So that you may change and be transformed into that being that God intends for you to be, that beautiful soul that is crying out saying, please recognize who I am and what I am, for I am your true self. I am that place that is unshakeable and is eternal. Awaken beloved souls, to this part of yourself that longs to be awake and true to itself.

Peace be with you, beloved souls. I come. I am the Master of the Celestial Heavens. I am your brother. I am your friend, and I come to speak to you and embrace you in love and acknowledge your beautiful lights. God bless you. I am Jesus and I love you.

…place, that it may have a deep purpose in the world, awakening many souls.

…Bless and sanctify this ground. Make it part of these blessings of light that are meant for the world, part of the gifts that God, my beloved Father has to give to humanity, part of the plan that God has for the salvation of humanity. This place will play its role and in time, many will come and receive blessings of healing and upliftment and vision and love. It is meant to be a portal of truth and light for all humanity. This humble place is filled with the power of God and you are all a part of it and immersed within it. Blessed souls, be at peace and know that you have been called to the light. God bless you, beloveds. I am with you, and if you call me in this place, I shall come to be with you in prayer. This is my promise and it shall be so.
