# Bring Flowers to Les Chapel Monnaie

May 13<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Les Chapel Monet, Guernsey

Goldie received by Al Fike

Bring flowers to this place. Not those pretend flowers but flowers that are alive as dedications to God and say I love you and I love life and I love all that is life. That way you confirm the truth of why you were created. You were created to love and to be immersed in life. Beloveds, our beautiful master, Jesus has come to bless you and open more of this portal of light and I urge you to take advantage of what has been given and what will be given through these few days together, for many more will come to speak to you and to bring their wisdom forward. Even our friends from the stars will come.

Beloved souls, may your hearts be open, your minds be open, and your souls be open to what is being offered and given in love. God bless you, my friends. My love is with you. And we will close this circle now, until we meet again.
