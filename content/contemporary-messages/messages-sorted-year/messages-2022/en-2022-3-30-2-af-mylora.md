# Blessings

March 30<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Monroeville, PA, USA

Mylora received by Al Fike

May Love be upon you, beloved souls, I am Mylora and I come to pray with you today in your beautiful Circle of Light. The world needs lights like yourselves. I encourage you to be a light in the world, to be a true channel of Love and goodness, compassion, healing, peace, giving comfort to those around you, giving love to all you meet. For when you do this, my beloved friends, then you bring God's blessings through you to others. You bring all that God wishes to give to those who are in need, those who are receptive. Often you cannot know who may benefit from these blessings because as you walk in the world you are often distracted by your thoughts and the earthly conditions. But when you pray to be used as a channel for God's Love, an instrument for His Will, then you allow that door to open and those blessings to flow whether you are aware or not, they come, they come because you have asked in prayer for them to come and be a part of your daily life.

Continue in your thoughts to see each child as a child of God, to walk the road of light and love without hesitation or distraction but to move forward, to move ever forward in loving intention as God lights your way upon the path. You are all lights, you are all beautiful souls. May you come to see yourself as such and know that we walk with you, seeking every opportunity as you do to be God's instrument in the world. And so we may come close when you desire us to do so. When you ask God to open that channel within you, so many gifts and blessings flow, many angels are present and light flows through you to everyone you meet. My friends, you are loved and you are lovely, lovely souls. Be true to this, be truly expressive of it and your days will be blessed and many will benefit from you and who you are as a light and child of God.

God bless you, beloveds, God bless you. I am Mylora and I love you. God bless you.
