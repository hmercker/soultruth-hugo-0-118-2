# Go to that Place of Soul Beyond the Mind Where All will be Revealed

March 26<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Monroeville, PA, USA

Orion received by Al Fike

Reach, reach beyond your minds to that place of soul and all will be revealed, all will come, all truth will flow into your consciousness. For the soul is capable, is equipped, has the capacity to know all things, to know all truth. It is the mind that sets limitations, the intellect that insists on linear and logical explanations; where the soul, in its beauty and capacities, can know all as a single awareness and unfolding of truth. This you cannot attain, my beloved and beautiful friends. This is waiting for each of you, provided you are willing to step beyond the thresholds and safety of a mind well engaged and secured, and trust in that openness and expansion that comes with a soul freed from the shackles, awakened in love and shown the way by God’s Touch.

Beloveds, you have been given a great opportunity. You are being given wonderful blessings and upliftments so that you may truly go beyond these limitations. May you choose to do so and walk with the universe of the many souls who are freed, who are your brothers and sisters and can help to guide you forward.

I am Orion. My love and blessings with you, beloved friends. God bless you in the limitless possibilities of a soul awakened in love. God bless you, my friends.
