# We are All Being Called into Service

March 27<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Monroeville, PA, USA

Isaiah received by Al Fike

God’s blessings are upon you, beloved souls. As God pours His Love into this place, enriching the atmosphere thickening it with love, so you are being blessed, beloved souls, so you are being blessed and uplifted. And as you commune together in God’s Love, so great changes are taking place within your souls. The transformation that comes with this Love is permanent and is expansive. You cannot step back, beloveds, you will step forward and continue to step forward into greater light, greater love. In so doing, those capacities that God has put within each one of you, unique and beautiful as they are, will awaken and find their expression in your lives. In this awakening, God will guide you, God will protect you. God will ensure you that these gifts that have been given and have been awakened in love will be expressed in unique and beautiful ways in your lives and in this world.

Have faith in God’s Plan, beloved souls. Have faith in the journey that God has laid before you and awaken to all that God wishes to give to you. For it is in this awakening, it is in traveling upon this path, that your life will find its fulfillment and that the purpose that God has put within each one of you will be awakened and find its mark upon the world. You are all children of God. None of you have been left out of this wonderful family gathering and blessing. And many more have benefited from your efforts and prayers in these times together. You become the beacon of light as you pray in this way, as you walk in this light, as you put your vulnerability, your desires, your efforts and thoughts towards being light and receiving the great gift of His Love.

You are all blessed, beloved souls, in the great flow and wonderment, which is God’s Holy Spirit touching your soul, awakening you to all that is meant to be. We are unified in light and truth and love. We are His children and God is nurturing each one of us. His desire is the fulfillment of all that you are and all that you can be, and this comes with the soul awakened in love. In this blessing, so the light reverberates out from your soul, through your spirit and your bodies, your minds, into the world reflecting the great gift of His Love. Beloved souls, you are truly blessed, you are truly blessed in His Gift and Touch.

I am Isaiah and I come to speak to you today in this great effort to shift the consciousness of the world, to shift within you all that is not in alignment with this truth. Pray for your world beloved souls, pray for your loved ones. Pray for all that you meet and all that you see, that there be peace and harmony. The awakening, the awakening must come and shall come to the world. Beloved souls, we are together in this desire to bring peace and harmony, to bring love and truth. May you continue to walk this path, not just fleetingly, but whole heartedly, to be God’s channels of love in the world, to dedicate yourselves to this one great purpose. And in that dedication, the unique and beautiful gifts that you carry will emerge and blossom in love, power, strength, light, beauty.

Beloveds, we beseech you to carry the banner of truth wherever you go, to be that example of truth and love in every breath, to be the willing servants of God and to do so in conscious prayer and effort in your lives. In this way, nothing will impede you and distract you, and you will know the joy, the expansiveness, the wonderment of a soul in alignment with God.

May God bless you upon this journey, beloved souls, and keep you true to your soul’s desire to walk with God. God bless you. God bless you beloved souls. Awaken, awaken. God bless you.
