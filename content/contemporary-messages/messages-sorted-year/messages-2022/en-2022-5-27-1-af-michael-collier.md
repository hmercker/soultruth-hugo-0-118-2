# Journey of Soul Consciousness

May 27<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Online Prayer Circle, Gibsons, BC, Canada

Michael Collier received by Al Fike

May God’s Love continue to flow into your souls, and may you yearn for this with great intensity and longing, for this is the way that God reaches you, my friends. God reaches you through your longings, through your prayers, through the direction of your consciousness towards the Divine. I am Michael Collier, and I come to encourage you, to help you to understand how your souls awaken.

When one begins this journey where God awakens your soul, heals your soul, enlightens your soul with His Love, it is often very subtle and you are not so much aware of changes within you or the effects of this Blessing upon you. It is more like water that moves underground. You are not aware of it and yet it is there. But in time, as the pressure builds, so to speak, as the light within your soul continues to grow, there are eruptions that enter into your conscious self; experiences, sensitivities, awarenesses, that come to the consciousness of the individual, not only in prayer but in their daily lives. And this Light within the soul breaks through the encrustations around it and in time, reaches the consciousness, the minds of the individual.

So, a change begins. Those tender shoots of love and awareness begin to enfold the individual. It is still a subtle thing, for the power of the material conditions of your world engulf you daily and draw your attention towards them. But indeed, the power of Love is persistent and will continue to make its effects upon you until there comes a time when the mind is ready to embrace and become aware of this new and tender forming of conscious, soulful self.

You must take care, my friends, to nurture this part of you that is vulnerable and tender, that God continues to nurture and awaken you. But indeed, your responsibility is to accept and to yearn for the consciousness of the soul, to awaken to its wonders.
Take time, my beloved friends, and contemplate those things within you. Allow soul consciousness to enter your reality. Ask God to continue to awaken you. Ask God to continue to awaken your soul in all the beauty, the filaments of your soul, awakening, coming alive with the power of Love.

You are still young, my beloved friends. You are still, in some ways, untested and somewhat fragile, but you continue upon this journey with persistence and faith. I encourage you to carry on in the ways that you are, making efforts to awaken, to allow, to expand in love. Surely in time, you will come to know your soul well. Your soul will become the predominant reality within you, as you grow in love, as you continue to accept and embrace the power of this blessing and the changes that it brings.

So your perceptions, your understanding, your sense of self and your sense of the world will change and grow in alignment with your soul’s awakening. This awakening comes in spurts and starts, not a smooth transition from one well-empowered consciousness, which is of the mind, to that of the soul. Rather, it is often a bit of a struggle; your consciousness pulled between one and the other, your awareness opening somewhat and then closing, then opening again to those finer and higher experiences of the soul.

The mind is stubborn and strong, for it has been nurtured from the beginning of your life. Now you switch your perspective and your focus from that of the mind to the soul, and this is not easy in the material world. But you have around you and with you, your angel friends, to help support and reinforce the perspective of the soul. And, of course, God is blessing you, His Hand resting upon you. You are being guided. You are being blessed and healed, transformed by Love.

Do not feel that you are alone, my friends, for you are not. There is so much present around each one of you, so many angels, the blessings of God upon you, helping to uphold and envelop and reinforce the light. The Light of God’s Love continues to do its work. It is ideal that you might slip into this new consciousness in such a way that it is not a struggle, but a welcome relief from the struggles of the mind, a welcome embracing of a new consciousness, a new awareness that is of the soul. It is bright and joyful and clear and simple. It does not require all the machinations of the mind’s reckonings. Rather, in the soul is the capacity to know the Truth of God and the Truth God’s Universe. And these things will flow and awaken you as you are ready, as you are willing to release those barriers that are around your soul and the insistence of your mind, which does not accept this newfound consciousness.

My beloved friends, you have a strength growing within you, a soul that is pushing forth like that underground stream, which will erupt in beautiful, pure waters of Love within your conscious self, as the power of Love grows within you, so this must be. It is inevitable that the consciousness of the soul must emerge and truly become a part of your reality.

My friends, we are here to help you birth your soul. We are here because God has called us to be your midwives, your support, your friends, as you find the New Birth, the birth of your soul awakening and enveloping and transforming your consciousness, your being, into something Divine. Beloved souls, continue to pray for this, to pray for the awakening, pray for the Love that brings the awakening. Pray that humanity may awaken. As you awaken, you will be the beacon of light that will call forth your brothers and sisters, and they may be inspired by your example and your presence.

My friends, the soul continues to exert its influence, and may you embrace the subtleties, the beauty, the wonderment of your soul. Thank you for listening to me today. I am Michael Collier, and I am glad to be with you. May God’s Blessings continue and strengthen and intensify, so that all that is not of God may dissipate and all that is of God may truly be your consciousness and your reality. God bless you, my friends, God bless you in Love. And my love is with you, with you all. God bless you.
