# More on Preparing for Earth Changes in Times to Come

April 3<sup>rd</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Houston, TX, USA

Professor Salyards received by Al Fike

Blessings to you all, my friends, I am Professor Salyards. May the blessing of God’s Love continue to awaken you and strengthen you and bring wisdom to your minds and your souls, so that you may be able to navigate any and all circumstances and conditions that may be a part of your world and a part of your life.

I wish you to keep in mind that flexibility is important in your life. To firmly commit to a certain approach and directive may be a waste of your energies and time, as you may be required to move away from your homes and the security that you have known, and have the flexibility of one who is mobile and able to avoid those areas of more intense disruptions and difficulties. Although it is certainly a wise decision to have food stored and the necessities of life available to you, for you may be in need of these things and these things may be of scarcity in times of great need.

But we ask you to be more attuned to your soul and the consciousness of soul. This, bringing you into greater alignment with God and with what God may guide you to do, may inspire you in many ways, may use you as a channel and light in the world, a world that will be in great need. You must contemplate what this is and requires of you. Flexibility of mind and a state of peacefulness and centeredness, as you call it, is important. For, if you cannot be in rapport with God in prayer, and open and receptive to His Blessings upon you, then of course, the guidance cannot come and the wisdom cannot be manifest in ways and means that are a part of the greater plan.

For you are not alone, nor are you singly acting and dealing with the conditions of the world. Rather you are together, soul to soul, linked as a great and strong chain of light that helps to secure each of you together, and you with many others. On this level of understanding and experience, then of course, through these connections, soul to soul, you may be able to help one another. For, some of you will experience great duress at  times, while others are not, and this could reverse itself. As you are linked in this way, the power of this light that you share, the wisdom of your souls that you carry, the blessings of love that continue to awaken and empower you, so you will be able to support and uphold one another.

Yes, the material circumstances and needs must be taken into account, but the greater need and necessity is that of a soul in alignment, not only within the individual but between individuals. In this way, you are strong. In this way, when you are challenged, you have the support, not only of your mortal friends, but your angel friends and God’s Touch. When all of these elements are in alignment and you walk in faith and without fear, then the solutions to various problems and crises will become apparent.

I wish to assure you that the manifestations of God’s blessings will become very tangible in these times that are ahead, that indeed, you are not required to be completely prepared so that you can sustain months, maybe years of difficult and challenging conditions. Rather, you are part of a great collaboration, a coming together of many elements and aspects, souls, the Soul of God, that will be brought together in harmony, thereby manifesting many things that you may require in surprising and miraculous ways. I am not suggesting that you leave everything to chance and faith and to walk without some knowledge and preparation for what is to come. Indeed, your part in this is important and necessary. But I say these things to assure you that no matter what conditions you may face, what may happen in your life and your home, your families, that there will be solutions presented and blessings given.

For God means to ensure the continuation of life, the continuation of humanity. But that continuation is reliant on what you might call a reset of the human condition and the ways in which humanity may exist upon this world. In this shift, this change, this wiping away of old conditions and thoughts and ways of being, then the new way is brought forward and you are able to see clearly what that way is, how God intends for you to be in the world. Not a world of great deprivation and fear and chaos, but a world of peace and love and light, where harmony prevails, rather than this unfortunate and decrepit view and expression of humanity as it is.

You will be strengthened. You will be given the knowledge, the strength, the way to navigate through these conditions, provided you step forward in faith and you continue to nurture your souls, that inner part of yourself, so that it is strong and capable and predominant in your life.

It is human nature that you may hide away from the difficulties rather than face the challenges head on. This is the way of humanity, who sleeps and continues to insist upon sleeping, and not acknowledging that there are deep concerns and deep problems in this precious world of yours. When we say we wish for you to awaken, we wish for you to awaken in all ways, in all aspects of your being so that you are clear, knowledgeable, wise, loving, and even joyful, as the world continues to transition. So you are able to ride that wave with a skill and ability, confidence and faith, where you are not in jeopardy, but you are joyfully accepting the transitional period, anticipating what will come as a result.

Being attuned to God’s Will, my friends, is the greatest strength you may carry. For God will guide you with every day. God will show you the ways and means to navigate these conditions. God will awaken you further. God will help you to see clearly what you must do and how you must do it. The vast majority of humanity is used to stumbling along in their life, ignorant of so much, unaware of their true selves, unaware of the true purpose of life. Now comes the time where God awakens His children and shows them the truth.

Many will be very disheartened, confused, seeking to go back to their slumbers, seeking to deny their own misdeeds, seeking to blame others for problems and conditions that have been created collectively in this world. You are required to stand up and speak the truth, to do so with love and humility, but with a strength, a strength that is undeniable. Its clarity, its light, its essence, which will be sustained by God, will draw many who are lost into your midst.

One man cannot save the world, my friends, but many who are engaging in this practice and preparation of the soul, of the mind, of the body, will be capable of leading many. You wonder how this may be so. You wonder, “how will I be strong enough, capable enough to lead, when I see my own frailties and my own compliance with the human condition?” But, my friends, God will empower you. The angels will be close, so very close. And you will come to know a part of you, a strength within you that you did not know that you had, but will become most apparent as the conditions of the world impinge itself upon you and upon all.

So God continues to enact His plan for the salvation of humanity. God continues to open doors for you and for many others, and to bring many blessings that will help prepare and strengthen you for the task ahead. For now, it is important that you gather yourself in faith and prayer, seeking ever greater understanding of truth, being strong in love. For, is not the greatest power of all in love, the Love Divine?

I know I speak plainly to you, my friends, as have others on other occasions, but we cannot stress enough, that powerful change is coming to your world, and that the world needs those who are clearly cognizant of what the meaning of this change is and what is needed in order to build a new world. Humanity is capable of rebuilding after that which has been built becomes irrelevant and unusable, for it requires new approaches, new ways of seeing the world, seeing one another.

Each of you have gifts and potentials that you do not know you have yet. But all of these beautiful flowers within your soul will blossom, creating such a garden of light and love, of truth and wisdom. This will help to inspire those who may have skills that you do not have, but do not have the wisdom that you carry. When the power of God’s Expression and Touch through you and others and upon this world become evident, then others will turn their heads to you, will come close and seek your counsel and wisdom. You, in turn, will seek your counsel and wisdom from God, and this flow of blessings, this manna from God, will help to awaken humanity, and they will see with the eyes, new eyes, fresh eyes, the beginnings of a new world, a world of peace and harmony. So you will find yourselves in positions of power, but power that is manifest in love and manifest through God. So you will come to that place, my friends, that will astound, but within your soul, you are knowledgeable and knowing of it.

May God bless you on that journey. Yes, there is much to know, but that knowledge will come when you are ready and in need of it. For the meantime, these rudimentary preparations are a blessing for you. You will find yourself being inspired to do various things, acquiring various materials, coming to the knowledge and understanding of what is needed, step by step, my friends, step by step.

I thank you. Blessings to you, my brothers and sisters, blessings indeed. God bless you. I am your Professor Salyards. I am with you. God bless you.
