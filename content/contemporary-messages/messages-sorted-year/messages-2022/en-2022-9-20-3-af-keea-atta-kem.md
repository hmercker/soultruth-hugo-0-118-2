# Deep Blessing of Peace and Love

September 20<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Direct Voice, Gibsons, BC, Canada

Keea Atta Kem received by Al Fike

Beloved children, I am Keea Atta Kem. Be at peace, beloveds, and know that you are embraced by God, that in this time of prayer together you are removed from the human condition. The only thing that stops you from being with God is that which is in your mind and those conditions of the Earth that you carry with you as you traverse your life.

Be at peace, beloveds, and know that you are loved and that as you continue to persevere through the earthly life, God is with you and God is guiding you. Be at peace, beloveds, for the greatest influence and power in your life is that of God. May His Will, His Guidance, His Touch of Love be your daily experience and commitment. For peace comes from God. Peace comes from that Touch as you reach out to your Heavenly Father in prayer. Peace comes with your desire to be in harmony with God, with your efforts to choose that which is of light, to choose that which is beyond the human condition into the true reality of your soul in relationship with God’s great Soul.

This reality we help you to cultivate, to reach for, to acknowledge in your daily life. As you continue to reach and do so with great effort and practice, then it becomes your foundation, your reality. Thus, peace will come. Not in the moment, but each and every day a sense of peace, a sense of well-being and joy. For as God’s Love continues to infill your soul and change your soul, transforming it into something divine, so all else that is you must come into alignment with this and be in harmony with it.

So you are challenged and yet you are given a great gift of life, a life that can indeed be truly in harmony with God, provided you are willing to capitulate, in a way, to that which is of God. For the human condition and the nature of humanity as it continues to dominate your world is to be in control and to be empowered by your daily life. Yet, true empowerment comes when you are able to capitulate to God, to allow God to express His blessings for you and through you and all about you in your life. This means you must let go, beloveds, let go of that need within your mind to feel so secure and have the empowerment of choice to such a degree that God’s Will and God’s influence is negated by your will and your desire.

Allow yourself to rest in the arms of God. Allow yourself to live your life in harmony with God’s Will. Allow yourself to know and feel and express the great Love that God has for you, that this may flow through you and touch everything in your life. In this way, you will know peace. In this way, deep peace will come to you and all the worries and cares, distractions and concerns of a material life will recede into the background and the predominant feeling will be that of peace and joy and love.

This is what we wish for you, beloved souls. This is what may come, provided you are willing to release yourself to God and to be in harmony with His laws, His Love, His Will. It all comes as you continue to pray and contemplate and be with God daily, many times in your day. Thus, the gifts of your soul will emerge and show themselves to you, provided you have the mind and consciousness to accept them and to acknowledge them. For they do not come when a mind is filled with material concerns. It comes in the quietness and the peace, this understanding and acknowledgment that is deep within you and longs to be expressed and longs to be a true part of your life, integrated in every way, expressed in many ways.

May you come to know your true selves, my beloved friends, come to know who you are, what God has truly created, that beautiful being which is unique and wondrous and of light. Be with God, beloveds, be with God and all else will come in its stead. May His Love continue to abide within you and grow and transform you. My love is with you. I am Keea Atta Kem, and I love you, my beloveds, I love you dearly. God bless you on your journey and in your efforts to bring truth to the world. I love you. I love you. I love you. God bless you.
