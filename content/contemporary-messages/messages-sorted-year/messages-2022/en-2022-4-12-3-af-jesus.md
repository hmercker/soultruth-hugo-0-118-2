# Guidance for our Service to God

April 12<sup>th</sup>, 2022

Soul Awakening Tour, Barbados

Jesus received by Al Fike

I come as your brother and your friend, and as the master of the Celestial Heavens; I am Jesus. I come to thank you for your efforts that you have made together to bring greater light to this place, this country. I come to ask of you that you may continue to bring the Truth of God’s Love to where you reside in the world, to be as my disciples of this truth. For, how can others know of it if it is not shared and spoken? How can others feel the gift, the Touch of God’s Love, if they do not pray to receive it and to experience it? I beseech you to gather your brothers and sisters together in Circles of Light, to say this simple prayer and to help God bring this Gift to each soul.

With your gifts and your capacities and light, you may be a channel for God and bring the truth to others. As you walk this truth and live this truth, so you are an example and a channel for this truth. It is of the utmost importance to spread the word of the salvation of humanity. For, when the time comes and there is great need in humanity, some will know the truth and the antidote to these conditions and to their suffering. So we continue to uphold and uplift those brothers and sisters who are in alignment with this truth, in hopes that they may indeed do God’s work, may indeed be God’s channels of light and love in the world.

Each of you, my beloved brothers and sisters, know within your hearts and your souls that God has called you, that there is a purpose, there is a destiny for each of you. In this understanding, comes the joy, the faith, the love, the wisdom, the light that is needed in order to do this work and to truly be a channel for God, to do His Work upon this world. You may stumble, you may be challenged, you may find the work difficult and at times, ungratifying. But indeed, in your soul, you know to step forward and to continue on in this work, for this is the fulfilment of your commitment to God, and that commitment began before you were even incarnate in the world.

The time has come for your commitment to find its realisation and goal, as a worker, a channel for God, to bring love forth, to bring truth forth, to bring comfort, healing and peace to your brothers and sisters. God will guide you, my beloveds. God will show you the way by which you may work and be this channel. This inspiration and expression will come with each day. For God will give you the blessing of a day, that he may use you and allow the wonderment and beauty of a soul in alignment with God, to express their gifts, to be a channel of love to their brothers and sisters.

It is not something that requires great strain and effort and thought. Rather, it is in your commitment, your dedications in prayer, your receptivity to God’s whispers and guidance to you. When you are innocent in this way and open to what is to come, then your days are filled with opportunity to serve, opportunities to bring greater light, not only to yourselves, but to your loved ones and to the world around you.

It is a simple matter to be in the Light of God, to pray each day to be used as a channel, an instrument of God. But that prayer and desire must be pure, not tainted by the wilful mind of one who reaches and desires to be noticed and acclaimed. Rather, the humble walk of God’s servant has the mark of simplicity and love. In this way, you will indeed be noticed, noticed by those who are in need, noticed by those who are seeking truth and are drawn to light. It will not be those with great power and resources, rather, it is your humble brothers and sisters, those who have within them, some knowledge of their soul and some expression of that soul. Yet, the yearning continues within them, as it does within you, the desire for more of God.

So together, you will have the opportunity to seek to fulfill that desire in prayer. For those who are not yet able to see and recognize this within them, you will be an instrument to help release the barriers so that they may know, and see, and hear. And for those who are lost and are seeking comfort, so you will reach out and touch them and tell them that they are loved and that God loves them deeply.

There are many in your world who are in need. In truth, all upon your world are in need. So, the work is great, it is vast in scope. But indeed, you must follow your guidance and walk gently upon the Earth, and be that channel with every breath, every moment, a beacon of light. In this way, God may use you in many ways that you will not know of nor understand, but, you will be used. That light will be utilised to help comfort, and heal, and uplift many.

There are few upon the Earth who truly understand how this may be accomplished, who truly understand the power of the soul awakened by the Essence of God. But around me, at this moment, are beautiful lights, beautiful souls who have a yearning to be of service. I tell you, God will not waste these opportunities. God will not ignore your desires and your willingness to step forward, but rather, it is for you to maintain the opening, the light, the way in which God may utilise you. To walk in the truth and love of God is a powerful way of serving God, and it has many layers and aspects to it, much of which you are unaware. But as time goes by and you grow and grow in the Love, so the faculties of your souls will open and the perceptions will grow, and you will truly understand that the workings and gifts of the soul awakened in love, are many and powerful. You will come to know this and see this for yourselves. You will be a testimony of truth, beloved souls, a channel of love, a bringer of light. May you come to see yourselves in this way, and understand how truly God has blessed you and keeps you in light and nurtures you along this Path Divine, so beautiful, so full of light.

Beloved souls, do not waste your time in idle pursuits, but come forward and declare yourselves to God with a willingness to serve, to be in alignment with the truth of His Love and the power of His Love to transform you and remake you into what you are meant to be, the seeds and potentials within you awakened by His Touch. Beloved and beautiful brothers and sisters, you are truly loved, and each of you has with you angels to assist and to utilise your gifts, for they too are God’s channels and instruments. In this, the flow will be powerful and wide reaching to many.

Beloveds, see your life from this perspective. See your day as an opportunity to serve God. In this way, you will see the doors opening, the beloved souls drawing close, and much will come in the stead of your prayers and desires, and commitment to be God’s channels of truth and love.

I am with you, each of you. I see you. I see your souls. I see your desires. I see the potentials within, and I see God’s plan for each one of you. So I come to declare my commitment to you, to assist you upon the road, and to work together as brothers and sisters in alignment with God’s Truth and Love. So we are together, beloveds. So we are together and shall do so and be so for all eternity. Beloveds, my love is with you. His Love pours into your soul at this very moment. Feel its power and glory. Know its effects upon you. Be in the joy and awareness and understanding of this greatest of all blessings, this most powerful of all truths. It is yours for the asking, beloveds. It is yours as you yearn and pray for the inflowing of the Father’s Love.

Blessings to each of you, beloveds. Blessings to uplift and to help dissolve any barriers to your soul. Blessings of light to protect you and to draw the angels close. Blessings of peace, so that your mind may not interfere with the workings of your soul. Blessings of truth, so that you may truly understand and walk in truth. So you are blessed, beloveds, so you are blessed and shall be blessed over and over, many times times many times, for all eternity.

Peace be with you, beloveds. Peace be with you and keep you in His Love. I am Jesus, and I come to encourage and to uplift and to acknowledge the beauty of your souls, beloved friends, brothers and sisters all, upon the path of soul awakening. God bless you. God bless you, beloveds. I love you. I love you for all eternity, beloveds, all eternity.
