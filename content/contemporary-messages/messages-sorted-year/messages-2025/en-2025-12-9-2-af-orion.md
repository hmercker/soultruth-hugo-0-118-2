# You Are Held in Place by the Efforts of Many

December 9<sup>th</sup>, 2025

Direct Voice, Sechelt, BC, Canada

Orion received by Al Fike

God bless you, my friends. It is Orion. You are being held in place and you shall be held in place to continue this work. What I mean by this is that God is pouring Light, protection, Love and grace upon you. In that blessing, your angel friends surround you and embrace you. We too are there, embracing and upholding. All who are about you are praying for you, praying for your protection, praying for your continued growth and awakening within the soul. A great army of souls are there to keep you in place, to keep you in the light, to help you on your journey toward greater light and greater love.

God provides everything for you. There is no need for struggle. There is no need for pain, because you have the strength, the blessing, the peace that passes all understanding, all that comes with God’s Love. How blessed you are. What a tragedy it is in the world that so few avail themselves of this blessing. Often, those who do still are involved, entrenched and a part of the human condition. We wish to extract you from that condition, to help you find detachment from it, to be with you upon that journey of soul awakening, transformation, inspiring and encouraging. Yet, God’s emanations, God’s desire for you, does not change. It is not weak. It is powerful and it is strong.

Unfortunately, the level, the reality of this condition, these blessings, are often not attuned to or aligned with because of your engagement with the material world. Yet, if you were fully aligned, open and aware through your soul’s perceptions of what is given to you, then you would truly be amazed. You would be humbled by what is given so freely, what is awakened in you because of all the outside efforts and your own combined, so that this awakening dawns within your consciousness, step by step, moment by moment. Our wish is that the awakening would be revelatory and powerful.

Yet, as is often the case in your world, it is slow but relentless in this awakening and knowing. You build faith step by step, brick by brick. You build consciousness in this way as well. This is somewhat tedious. It requires daily contributions, efforts and prayers, but it is given to you in all its beauty and all its wonder. It is given to you and so you are a part of a movement, a collaboration in your material world that requires tremendous support and tremendous effort. It is all given and it is necessary that you contribute equally to what has been given. That way, that balance of collaboration will bring tremendous results, will bring everything that you desire and everything that God desires, together in harmony and purposefulness in the ways and means of adding to the changes that are required in the world.

My beautiful friends, consider each day an opportunity and a responsibility to grow, to add to this great effort, to pray for it, to consider it, to make it truly a part of you. The desire to be God’s instruments, be our cohorts in the great efforts that are of today and tomorrow must be foremost in your consciousness and in what you do in your daily life. When this becomes a focus, a commitment and a true desire, then all will unfold in relation to your efforts, to your contributions and to your desires. My beloved friends, it is time to step through the threshold, leaving aside debts, leaving aside the old habits, being renewed in this reality, this form of consciousness that is of the soul, finding the freedom and the joy and the wonderment there, finding the capacity to love and to be love, accepting love, all of these things you are edging toward bit by bit.

Our desire is now that you step boldly in this direction and truly become a part of the efforts that we make together, upholding your responsibility and your role in complete harmony and desire to do so. God gives you all that you require. How many times have we said this, my friends? Seek this out. Seek out what is given. Make efforts daily to acknowledge, to be aware and to embrace the blessings that flow to each one of you. None of you are left out. Each of you is administered to equally and powerfully. It is for you to acknowledge this, to recognise it and to do so with gratitude and joy, to awaken, awaken to all these things and to much more.

God bless you, my beloved friends. I am Orion. Oh, I love you, beautiful, intrepid souls, seeking for that higher place and higher consciousness, that depth of love and depth of wisdom. May the joy build within you and the freedom flourish in your life. God bless you, my friends. God bless you all.
