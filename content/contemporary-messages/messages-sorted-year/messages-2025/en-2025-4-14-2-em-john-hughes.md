# Much Is Being Given to the Earth

April 14<sup>th</sup>, 2025

One Nation Circle of Light, Liverpool, NY, USA

John Hughes received by Elizabeth Mack

As you lift your souls, your intentions and your longings, you are blessed with the mightiest of blessings that will bring you forward along your path. Pray deeply, deeply children, for much is being given at this time, much that will facilitate movement forward toward the Kingdom that the Father builds upon this Earth.

As you are channels of light and love, instruments of the Father and prayer, you are given this task to remain open and we shall help you do that throughout this prayer. For much is being given to the Earth through you and others who are in tune to the Father and the Father's Love. The work is great and we take every opportunity to utilize you, your willingness to serve, to assist your brothers and sisters upon this Earth and in the spirit world to progress and to transform. Open your souls. Bring in deeply this Love. Be grateful, for you are greatly blessed at this very moment.

I am your brother, John, my dear sister's beloved. We come in great numbers to fulfil your desires and to serve God. We love you dearly.
