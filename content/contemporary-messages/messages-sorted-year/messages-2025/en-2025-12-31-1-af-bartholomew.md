# Opens the Portal In Our Suite at the Plumtree

December 31<sup>st</sup>, 2025

Barbados Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Bartholomew received by Al Fike

Greetings and blessings to you, my friends. I am Bartholomew. We are very pleased that you have gathered once again to do the work that is laid up before you. I wish you to know that each and every day that you are together and here on this beautiful island, you will be utilised as instruments of God. I wish you to be aware of this and cognisant of the fact that you are important players in a plan that we have devised, that God intends, that you perk up your awareness of these things, to be within the flow of what is intended and God’s Will and, of course, to be a channel of love wherever you go.

The work that we intend to carry forth is not just what you do in prayer together, but each and every day, each and every moment, will be purposeful and will have blessings, Light, Love, healing, many, many blessings from God that will come to you and through you as you go about your day and even in your sleep state. It will be a very intense time. I want you to know that you must be in good condition, spiritual condition, to be in alignment with what we have in mind.

My beautiful friends, this prayer marks the beginning of many prayers and many actions of service for humanity. Be prayerful in your days, be loving in your actions and think of one another. For this connection, this bond that you share, will serve our purposes well and will bring upliftment to one another. There is much to do and there are many souls to connect with. God has a wonderful plan and you are each a part of it, an integral part of it. Be joyful within your hearts that your lives have a deeper meaning than most, because you will, and have, assigned yourself to God’s Will and God’s purpose. It shall be because you have elected to be His instruments.

God bless you, beloved souls. Continue to long and pray for the great Love of God. Continue to grow in this Love, for this is the key to your effectiveness and the power of your gifts ignited and expressed in all their beauty, wonderment and effectiveness. Much to do, my friends. Much to do. God bless you. I am Bartholomew. My love is with you. As you well know, there are many angels surrounding you, many eager to work through you and around you, above you and below you. There is much activity and much effort being made on our part. God bless you, beloved souls. God bless you.
