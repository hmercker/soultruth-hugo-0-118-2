# The Coming Change and the Way of the Soul

October 3<sup>rd</sup>, 2025

Online Circle of Light, Sechelt, BC, Canada

White Cloud received by Al Fike

I greet you as a brother and sister. I am White Cloud. When I walked the Earth, which was not that long ago, the world was still innocent. Certainly, the world that I inhabited was more a reflection of the harmony with God than what it is today. When I walked the Earth, the Europeans were just coming to be a part of this land. You can see how the attitudes and ideas of that place in the world have affected everything. The world was innocent in my day. Yet, those from the northern lands of Europe (...) the minds and the actions of so many, creating a reality, a viewpoint and an expression of biases and desires that has objectified this beautiful Earth and made it something that it is truly not.

The common belief was that the Earth was made for men, but indeed, man would take advantage of all that the Earth had to offer. The Earth was made to foster life and all its variety and diversity and man, humanity, is just one part of this. Because of the power of thought and deed that humanity has, they have manipulated the world and utilised much of the world for their own advantage and their own gratification. Thus, you have what you call a materialistic society.

That is about to change, my friends. For like every civilisation, it peaks and then falls rapidly. So it is with yours. With this perspective, with this material viewpoint, you are all ensnared in this perspective. You are all participating in it. Unfortunately, you will all suffer the consequences of the collapse of this society, these peoples that desire to control and to dominate the world. You will all be humbled, my beloved friends. You will all come to know how you have depreciated this world by your thoughts and actions. This is not punishment by God. This is merely the natural order re-establishing itself. Those of you who are wise within your souls, who seek the guidance of God, the Creator of all, and who seek to be a light, a channel of love, will find less impact from these changes than those who are in ignorance and who have blithely gone along the road of human endeavour and its conditions.

My friends, difficult times are coming. You will find that the material gratification will no longer be a part of your life in a way that it is now. I am not suggesting that the roof over your head and the food in your bellies will be removed, but the way in which this will be will change in accordance with the changes that are coming. God will look after you. God always does to those who are faithful and who desire to be in harmony with His Laws of Love. Yet, when you pray to be protected and guided, do you know what the answer to that prayer is at any given moment? No, my friends, it comes, often surprisingly and often not in accordance with your desires and your expectations. Yet, your needs are met and the blessings are given, and so it shall be.

Be humble in your actions, my friends. Be true to your souls, which are true to God. You will find a great detachment from these conditions, addictions, predilections and desires that come from the material key point. Thus, your life will be simpler and more in harmony with the way God has intended. We were in harmony with God's intentions. We lived a life that was healthy and strong. We honoured one another. We loved one another. You may take a page from our book, our experience and examples. You cannot turn back, my friends. You can only forge ahead. The adjustments and conditions that will evolve will reflect more harmony and will also adapt to what has been and what is now in your world, but change in a way that will bring greater harmony.

There is much coming. We have told you this many times. It creeps closer. Do you not feel it? Do you not know that you must prepare yourselves? Be strong, faithful, ardent in your prayers and desires to be in alignment with God. In this way, you will avoid the tumult in ways that others cannot. You will also be channels of love, channels of wisdom, channels of light for God, who will guide you and use you to bring harmony back to the world. You pray for this and so it is and will come in its own way and means. Develop your sensitivities, the gifts of your soul which come with God's Love blessing your soul. In this way, the road forward will be made clear and the capacities and gifts of your soul will be utilised to comfort and help many.

Yes, humanity has made life difficult. God's creation suffers. You suffer, although you have distracted yourselves well from that suffering. Indeed, everything on this Earth is suffering. It is time for God's intervention to set forth a new way, to set aside the way of man and reimpose the way of God. It shall be, my friends, and you who aspire to be in alignment with the way of God will know the way, be guided forward and be blessed accordingly. I am White Cloud and my love for you is great. I bring compassion. I still live in harmony, but that harmony is now the harmony of the Celestial Heavens, where nature and life abound in such beauty and harmony. It would bring you to tears to see it as it is meant to be in these beautiful realms. Humanity's creativity has made for great and beautiful expressions of life in co-creation with God. You will learn how to do this, my friends. In time, humanity will learn to make a paradise on Earth, not through the exertions of the mind, but through the wisdom of the soul.

God bless you, my friends. White Cloud loves you. Indeed, I enjoy my connection and friendship with your dear friend, Barbara. We are now closer than ever. I am glad that I was her guide. I continue to walk with her as she walks with me in the Halls of Peace, heaven, beauty and light. God's Heaven of Love is ours and we are truly, joyfully a part of it. Blessings to you, my friends. May the great White Spirit touch you deeply with this Love. God bless you. God bless you. God bless you all.
