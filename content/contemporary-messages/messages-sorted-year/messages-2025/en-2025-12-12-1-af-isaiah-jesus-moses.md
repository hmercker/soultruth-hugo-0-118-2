# You Are the Living Channels of God's Blessings on Earth

December 12<sup>th</sup>, 2025

Online Circle of Light, Sechelt, BC, Canada

Isaiah, Jesus & Moses received by Al Fike

**Isaiah:**I come to you in an onrush of light and blessings. I am Isaiah. I come to bring blessings through you, as the prisms of God's blessings that you are, each bringing your own colour, pure and beautiful, into the firmament of this Earth. May you continue to be that channel, that beautiful blessing for others, for it is not just you that we come for. It is for all humanity that we come. Those who are willing to be God's instruments may be utilised in such a way that the blessings will flow, the purity of light shall be given and all that is of God's Love will pour upon this world of yours, as the Holy Spirit brings its touch upon your souls and upon all souls who seek out this gift.

My beloved and beautiful friends, take heed of the truth that you know. Live it with every breath. In this way, you will become and express a pure channel of Love for God. You will join the annals of all those who have come before you, who have worked as God's instruments. You will be part of that great chain of truth, that beautiful flow of love that comes through His instruments. You are meant to be that channel, each one of you and many more. For God puts great effort to bless, heal and transform this world of yours from a dark place to that of light, truth and harmony. Each of you is part of this plan. Each of you may step forward as you are guided. As you do so, the angels of heaven shall be by your side, shall supply their protection and light and shall bring clarity to your guidance and upliftment to your soul.

We are here to be with you, beloveds. We are here as God's channels, as you are here. I bring the light. I bring a blessing. I bring all that God wishes for you, as do many angels who come to join you in this prayer, the Circle of Light. Uphold the truth, beloveds, and be true to God. As your soul awakens to the power of His Love, as your soul is transformed by the blessings of His Love, so it is given and so it shall be. For the world is destined to be reclaimed and purified and gifted with all that is of light. Those of you who truly have faith in this, who truly know this, your souls joyful for it, will be a part of this great transformation, this great healing of the world. Be true to God in your prayers. Open yourself fully to His Touch and all that is required of you, all that must fall away from you, all that shall be transformed within you, shall be given with the onrush of God's Love into your soul.

God bless you, beloveds. I am Isaiah. I come to pray with you. I am with you in love and I shall be with you as you continue your journey and awaken to truth. God bless you all. My love is with you. My love shall be with you. May you carry the blessing that will open the door to many blessings, that of your Father's Love shall awaken you further. God bless you, beloved souls. Isaiah loves you. God bless you.

**Jesus:** My dear brother comes, as do I. I am your brother and friend, Jesus. I come with him to bring light to your world. Together, we work, for this shall continue to be in God's service, to bring the blessings of light, love and truth to humanity. I am here with you, my beloveds, as are many beautiful angels, to bring the light into your midst, to bring the light through you, the prisms that are God's souls within each one of you. God bless you. God bless you all in His Love. I love you.

**Moses:** I too come. I am Moses. I come to bring blessings. I come to enforce the light. I come to bless my brothers and sisters on Earth. I come to pray with you. I am here with you, beloveds. All the power and glory that we bring, it is shed upon you and upon the Earth. God bless you. Moses loves you. God bless you.
