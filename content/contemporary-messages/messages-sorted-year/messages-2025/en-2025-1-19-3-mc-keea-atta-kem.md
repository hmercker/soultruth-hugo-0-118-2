# Invites Us Into the Realms of Inner Peace and Wisdom

January 19<sup>th</sup>, 2025

St Michael, Barbados

Keea Atta Kem received by Maureen Cardoso

I am Keea Atta Kem. I invite you to relax, to be at peace and to be aware of your inner world. That place where there is great joy and peace, where truth exists and where a well of wisdom is held. I invite you to allow yourself to put down your outside needs and move inward to your inner needs.

It is within this place within you that you commune with God, where you meet your Celestial friends and where you find your true essence, your soul. Allow every part of your thinking mind to flow into the centre of your being and to feel what you feel there. Acknowledge anything that may be open and expansive. Acknowledge anything that may feel restrictive or closed.

These are the areas of light and darkness, your unconditioned qualities where you find Divine Love and your human qualities where you find your human-conditioned love. At this point in your journey of transformation, both exist within you. Do not fear or push away those restrictive aspects that you may be able to perceive. Rather, allow the love that your soul carries to nurture this part of you, to transform, soothe and acknowledge.

This is part of the Law of Love in its workings, where the Divine Essence that you carry can nurture those aspects that are insecure, that hold you back, that stifle and restrict the pure essence of your soul’s expression. As you are open to this great blessing and inflowing of more Divine Love, this light and this Love expands. You are accumulating, obtaining and being transformed by the Substance of God’s Love.

This is who you are becoming. You are becoming pure Divine Love. In these moments that you relish, in this light and in the peace, drink deeply. Allow yourself to be saturated, where every cell in your being, every part of your soul, your body and your mind merges into the Living Waters, these pristine, cleansing and purifying Waters. You are being washed and awakened, birthing a soul that is new, that is divine, no longer merely the image. In due time, you shall become a divine soul. Allow these Waters to fill you and see the level of love rising. See the light of your soul dazzling and sparkling, illuminated by God’s Will and your will merging into one.

Experience this peace, beloveds. Allow yourself to fall into the grace of God’s ocean of Love. God bless you.
