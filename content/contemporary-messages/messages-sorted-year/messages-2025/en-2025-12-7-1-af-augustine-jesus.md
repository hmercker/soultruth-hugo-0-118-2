# Surrendering to God's Will: The Path of the True Instrument of Love

December 7<sup>th</sup>, 2025

Online Circle of Light, Sechelt, BC, Canada

Augustine & Jesus received by Al Fike

**Augustine:** May God bless you with His glorious Divine Love. I am your teacher, Augustine. What is of the highest will in the universe? It is the Will of God. When you supplant your will with God's Will, what will be the results? For the wisdom, knowing and intention of God is far greater than any spirit in existence and therefore the outcomes will be of greater harmony and higher expression than anything that humanity or the Celestial Angels may come and express.

When you seek out the Will of God, you are seeking for the highest outcomes, the most blessed experience and situation that may be accomplished. When you are in alignment with God, receiving the great gift of Divine Love in sufficient abundance, then you may be that clear channel of God's Will. You may be that child acting in harmony and in obedience with God's Will. I know in your present day cultures and attitudes, releasing that individual will is difficult. You have been taught from an early age that it is your will that will overcome any condition, any barrier, any situation, if it is applied properly.

Yet, is this the truth? You have each expressed your will in many different ways and thousands of different situations. At times, the outcomes have been blessed and positive and at times they have not. For who here possesses the insight, the perception, the capacity to understand the truth of the matter in any given situation? You have a limited perception, beloved souls. Even we have a limited perception compared to God. What have we done as we have entered into the Celestial Kingdom? We have allowed God's Will to supplant our own. Because you call us angels, and we truly are angels, our desire and our motivation is to do God's Will, to express that in every aspect of our being, every moment of our existence.

My beloved and beautiful students, as you wish to be transformed by the Father's Love, as you walk that path of transformation, healing and awakening, there will come a day when you will release the predominance of your own wilful nature and being and embrace God's Will in your life to such an extent that you will be considered an instrument of God, an angel. It will not only bring the power of Love into your lives and the lives that you touch, but also bring the wisdom and Will of God through you for the benefit and blessing, not only of yourself but for others.

We desire this of you, to have angels walking upon the Earth, true angels, those who are truly in alignment with God's Will. You can and will be in time. You have inklings of God's Will now. Some of you can be readily guided, others desire to be readily guided. As you grow in the Father's Love, the capacities, the soul perceptions, the soul gifts will work toward these ends. Your day will be an expression of God's guidance, intention and Will in your lives. It grows within you. The awareness is in your soul. As you continue to honour your soul and desire the guidance, blessings and wisdom that comes from your soul, so you forge the alignment between you and God. You forge this pact that says, *“I will act on your behalf, beloved Father. I will be your instrument of light. I will walk in the world as a light, a channel of love. I will utilise each and every day as an opportunity to bring light, to bring love and to bring about Your Will in the world.”*

The world is destined to have God's Will predominant and you are the forerunners, beloved souls, of such a thing. If you are able to overcome the obstacles and to swim against the current of human will in favour of God's Will, then you will have accomplished something magnificent, something so very needed in the world. To carry the wisdom, the perceptions and the expressions that reflect God's Will into your life is a tremendous accomplishment and I urge you to pray for this. As you pray for the inflowing of God's Love, pray for the inflowing of God's Will into your consciousness that you may know. For God wishes to guide each and every one of you, every child upon this world. God's desire is to guide, to help, to show you the road, the way toward greater harmony and peace and joy. In this way, you will find fulfilment. You will find the blessings of the Father’s Touch, in all its aspects, all its beauty and all its power.

We are all children of God. We, in the Celestial Heavens, have come to know God's Will as second nature to our own and therefore the amalgamation of the two makes sure a powerful catalyst to bring blessings to your world, to the spirit world and to the universe. My beloved and beautiful friends, my students, choose God's Will. In your day, in your thoughts, in your actions, choose God's Will and the highest outcomes will be yours. The greatest Light will come to you and through you. The wisdom of God's Will will be (...annointed) in a world that is so ignorant of this. Be that channel of light, the harbinger of truth, expression of Divine Love and you will truly be what your souls desire to be, that instrument of God in the world.

God bless you, my beloved students. I am your teacher, Augustine. I come to encourage and uplift, to bring my love to you all, as do many angels that crowd around you and continue to uphold you. God bless you, beloved souls. My love is with you. 

**Jesus:** I come to bless you, beloveds. Your brother and friend, Jesus, is here. I come to bless you and assure you of how loved you are. The immensity of God's Love is there for you to receive and to gather into your soul through prayer and longing. Receive in abundance, beloveds, and you shall know its grace and power to transform, given to you, freely given because God loves you and we love you. There is love all around, beloved souls. Drink it in, drink in these Living Waters and be refreshed by its blessing.

God bless you, beloveds. I come to be with you in prayer and I love you.
