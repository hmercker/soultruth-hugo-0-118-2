# Walking the Eternal Path of Divine Love

December 21<sup>st</sup>, 2025

Online Circle of Light, Sechelt, BC, Canada

Jesus & Mary received by Al Fike

**Jesus:** Thank you, my brother for reciting my prayer, a prayer that I gave that describes the truth that I shared when I walked the Earth. I am happy to be with you today. I am your brother and friend, Jesus. I am happy that you celebrate my coming to Earth on this designated day of celebration. I am happy that you are here, that there is a unity and there is a desire for the great gift of God's Love, that gift that was bestowed when I came to Earth, that gift that shall transform the soul and awaken you to the glories of God and the glories of your own soul.

So many do not understand my message and so few follow the truth of it. Yet, when I see you all gathered and the many more who practise this truth and live it in prayer and daily expression, then I am filled with hope that sometime soon, the world will know of this truth. Sometime soon, the power of God's Love will be expressed through many souls and, in this expression, true change and healing will come to your Earth.

Since the time I walked this world of yours, until now, I have been diligent in bringing this truth to all souls that will listen. Indeed, the angels of heaven have all been engaged in this pursuit. We toil diligently and earnestly for the benefit of humanity and all the spirits in the spirit world to show them the way towards the Celestial Heavens, to help them to understand that they have a choice and an opportunity to walk this journey towards at-onement with God, the journey that requires the inflowing of God's Essence, the Divine Love, that they too may be transformed by it, that they too may understand the journey and be enthusiastic with great desire to follow this path Divine.

Each of you are living examples of this journey, the power of transformation, the power of Love expressed through your beautiful light. Each of you is my brother and sister. Each of you follow the way of the Master, the Truth and the glory of Love, and so you are my disciples on Earth and you will be my friends, truly, truly. The bond that we share is the bond of Love that we share for God and God's Love that is shared for each one of us. We walk the same path, a path eternal, a path glorious in love, truth, joy and light. We are bonded for eternity, my beloveds. Each of you holds a special place in my heart and a special place in my prayers, that we may be closer in this bond and rapport together, that we may walk as one, a unified body of truth, love and light.

We are true children of God, my beloveds. Do you feel the joy welling up in your hearts, the knowingness, knowing that what you have expressed to God, the desires, the longing of your soul, has changed you, has made a change that is forever? You continue to walk this path of change and transformation, and you will know greater joy, greater truth and greater love. As God's instruments, you will be channels of love for others. I urge you to share the good tidings of this, the soul of God's great gift of Love. For every soul needs to know this. Though they celebrate my birth, do they truly understand what it means? Do they truly know the gifts that I brought as God's instrument to the world? This they must know. If they are to celebrate me, then they must celebrate the truth, for the truth shall set all free from the shackles and burdens of the material world, that they may find the inner joy, the expansiveness of a soul that receives this great gift of God's Soul.

My beloveds, work towards these ends. Bring the truth to all who will listen and know that you do this in my name and in the name of truth. In the name of God, you bring the highest blessing, the potential to receive it. May you pray and seek to be that conduit of truth so that others may understand and be inspired by it. My beloveds, we work together, you and I, and we shall do so for all the days of your life upon this Earth and all the days in the spirit world, until there comes a time when I will greet you at the Gates of Heaven and bid you to enter therein. My beloveds, we are on a sacred journey, a journey of truth, a journey of transformation and this will never end. Joy will never end but expand. Truth will never end but expand. The awakening of your soul and the healing of your soul through God's Love is just the beginning of an eternal journey, one that you may not understand now, but as you continue on this journey, you will truly know it for what it is and how it will be in your consciousness and being.

May God's Love continue to enter into your soul, the Holy Spirit conveying this gift as it is asked for and desired by you. It is given in accordance to the law. God bless you, my beloveds. I am truly your brother and friend and I love you so. God bless you and keep you in His Love. I am Jesus and I walk with you for eternity. God bless you. God bless you.

**Mary:** I gave birth to my son. It is Mary. The story that is told is true. His birth was in a very humble setting, one in which the angels were present and God's Light shined upon us and how his glorious life changed the hearts of many. I was privileged to be his mother. How I benefited in so many ways to raise this beautiful soul and to assure that his mission was completed.

My beloved friends, God has a purpose for each one of you. God has a mission for you to fulfil. I ask you, humbly and with love, seek within your heart. Seek out that mission, that purpose within your soul. For what you will do will be the most fulfilling thing of your life and the most powerful expression of your existence. For when God can utilise those who have purpose that He has implanted within them, then the greatest of blessings, the highest of blessings, shall be theirs and shall come and manifest through each one. Seek out the truth that lies within and the Truth that lies within God's great Soul and the way and means of expressing that true purpose will come and manifest in all its glory.

God bless you, my beloveds. I am Mary, Mother of Jesus. Indeed, in many ways, I am your mother as well. I love you all. God bless you and keep you in the Truth of His Love.
