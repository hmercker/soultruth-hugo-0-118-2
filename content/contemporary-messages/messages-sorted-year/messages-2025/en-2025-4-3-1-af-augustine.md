# Q&A

April 3<sup>rd</sup>, 2025

Other Prayer Groups, Sechelt, BC, Canada

Augustine received by Al Fike

**Question:** *Let us start with a question about reincarnation. Why do so many mediums believe in reincarnation?*

Mediums are sensitives. They are vulnerable and open to communication from spirits. Depending on their sensitivity, the condition and memories of that spirit may be readily transferred to the medium. They believe, that is, the medium believes, that these memories and visions are theirs, that they belong to them. In fact, they belong to the spirit. Spirits may, by design, share these communications, this information and these visions, or may not, but because the medium is open to various communications, then the sharing is done voluntarily or involuntarily.

A medium is also open to various other influences and is open to what you describe as the Akashic records. With their openness, they are able to align themselves with these records and, again, receive information, visions and memories that are a part of these records. With all of these elements in play with the medium, and if the medium is not particularly discerning or developed in their soul's perceptions, then it is understandable that they would believe that these memories are a part of their life and a life that they did not lead on Earth at present, but a life that they led on Earth at times past. They have experienced these things in a way, with such an intensity and clarity, that they believe that it is truly a part of them and part of their soul's memories.

**Question:** *Does it harm the afterlife if the body of a deceased person is cremated or buried?*

A collection of chemicals does not have any bearing on the soul or the spirit of the individual upon passing over. It is God's design that these elements and chemicals that created a human being, or any life form, may return back to the Earth. An identical copy of the physical body is retained by the spirit body, which houses the soul. This body cannot be destroyed, cannot be burnt or sent back to the elements. It is a life force that God has given to each individual soul and therefore will remain so and cannot be altered, other than by the will and the experiences of the individual who is living on the Earth plane or living in the spirit world.

The spirit body continues to exist and will exist for a very long time indeed, as the individual experiences the spirit world, progressing through the planes of spirit until they arrive at the natural love heaven, which is the Sixth Sphere, or the Celestial Heavens, which is beyond the Seventh Sphere. I believe this has answered your question.

**Question:** *What are ghosts?*

Ghosts are Earth-bound spirits that are so attached to the material plane that, at times, they take on a sort of material form that is described as “ghosts”. In other words, individuals who live on the Earth plane and have material senses can detect, feel and see, at times, these spirits as they continue to inhabit the material world.

Ghosts have not progressed beyond this plane. They are stuck within the material plane. They are in need of your prayers and possibly your intervention to help them move on. For there is nothing of the material plane that will help them in their progression. They often become fixated on various things, such as an event that was traumatic, or material things that are hidden, such as money or objects of value that they wish to show others whom they know. Or the trauma that they carry is so intense that they cannot see or feel anything other than that experience and that condition. Therefore, seeing beyond the material trauma is very difficult for that spirit and they become very much a part of the Earth plane, unable to let go of whatever it is that keeps them there.

**Question:** *What happens to an unborn child after an abortion or miscarriage?*

If the soul has attached to the foetus and therefore is a viable child in utero, and if that child is destroyed in whatever way that destruction happens, then that unformed but very much alive being will enter into the spirit world, into a place called Summerland in the Second Sphere and will be nurtured and cared for by those who are engaged in such service. They will grow into a fully formed child and eventually into an adult in the spirit world. That time of growing and forming into the adult is much quicker than it is in the material plane. The individual child becomes a fully formed spirit in a short time and can make choices as to their progression during this period of development.

**Question:** *Does the person go to hell if they abort a baby?*

It completely depends upon the circumstances. If the individual aborts a child out of malice, hatred, guilt and all human conditions that are dark and they carry those feelings and thoughts with them until the time comes when they enter into the spirit world, then there needs to be some reconciliation within the individual and possibly within the child that was aborted, so that there is greater harmony. If there is extreme disharmony, then that individual will go to a darker place, a place that suits their condition.

It is not a punishment. It is solely the result of the individual, the mother, who has taken on dark conditions, feelings and judgments about herself that have remained unresolved and unhealed. There is a possibility that that individual may go to a dark or semi-dark place before they can progress into light, but  it is not always the case. If the individual forgives themselves and has made peace with the situation, then they are more likely to enter into a lighter condition.

**Question:** *Would choosing MAiD, which is medical assistance in dying, result in going to hell?*

If the individual chooses to end their life prematurely, there are consequences. If that life is ended within a short period of time before death, as a merciful way of pre-empting great pain and difficulties, then there is no harm done. Yet, if the individual commits a sort of suicide through taking their life or through arranging for another to take their life, there are consequences that are difficult and harsh because they have countermanded God's blessing of life and have decided that they do not wish to live a material life. Often, these poor individuals are unaware that the conditions that they carry with them upon death are exactly the same as when they were in life in the material sense. Thus, if they are in great pain and turmoil, they will go to a place that reflects these conditions.

Much help is offered by brighter spirits when the individual goes to these places. Of course, it is up to them to accept the assistance and to learn how they may evolve out of these conditions. Those who are dying, in great physical distress and have had their life prematurely ended, but not to a great degree, are not in danger of suffering as a consequence of their choice, nor is the individual who administers whatever remedy given in danger. Both are engaged in an act of compassion and both are indeed in harmony with the Laws of God.

**Question:** *What is hell? Is it fire and brimstone?*

No, there is no fire and brimstone in hell. This is a dramatic description created many, many years ago in order to scare those who are dedicated to various religions, especially that of Catholicism and Protestantism, to encourage them to dedicate themselves to the religious order that they subscribe to. Hell is not a hot and searing place. Hell is a very cold place, cold in emotion and cold in temperature. It is dark, foreboding. It is filled with sensations and senses that are repulsive and repugnant.

Hell is the creation of dark souls who do not know any better and are in this condition themselves. The place in which they inhabit is a reflection of their condition, their thoughts, their actions, actions of the past and actions of the present. They feed these conditions through their thoughts and actions in this place. It is a place that, if individuals knew of and had a moment of experiencing it, they would be more worried and frightened than the thoughts of hell in brimstone and fire. For this horrible place, the darkest of hells, is a place that makes Earth look like heaven, no matter how much suffering the individual might have on the Earth plane.

There are gradients of hell. Some are lighter. Some have a measure of light. Yet, as the Master says, all levels of spirit are hells compared to the heavens of the Celestial Kingdom. They are gradients, levels and conditions reflecting the condition of the individual.

**Question:** *To grow spiritually, does one need to cut out alcohol or meat?*

These things are not required, unless the individual does these things in excess. Like any consumption, if it is done in excess, if it is fixated upon, if it becomes an addiction, then, of course, it will affect the condition of the individual adversely. All things that, in moderation, do not require abstinence. They require wisdom. They require an attitude of love and peace when in the act of consuming these substances.

**Question:** *Is it okay to simply enjoy sex, or is there a spiritual consequence to that?*

Every action has its spiritual consequence. An action done in love is an action that brings light and a benefit to the individual. An action done that uses another individual for their own pleasure and gratification will not bring light and harmony. It will bring the opposite. Those who engage in sexual activity and do so in a selfish, self-concerned and unloving way may enjoy the pleasure derived from it, but will suffer the consequences spiritually of that act.

**Question:** *Are there any unforgivable sins?*

All sins are forgivable in God's world, in God's creation. Forgiveness comes with the individual, once they have the realisation of the sin that they committed, of the disharmony and consequences of their sin, their actions, their thoughts. It is the individual who forgives themselves that is required. Some are stuck within an unforgiving condition that they have created and so the process of forgiveness may take a very, very long time, possibly even thousands of your Earth years, before they are liberated and realise that they are forgiven within themselves.

Forgiveness comes on the heels of the individual's capacity and ability to self-forgive. Yet, there are no unpardonable sins. There are sins that bring harsh consequences. There are sins that bring a moderate form of consequence. There are sins that can be dispelled readily through prayer and, especially, in receiving the blessing of Divine Love within the soul. When one receives Divine Love within the soul, it ignites a series of openings, perceptions, of gifts within the soul that facilitate forgiveness more readily than those who go through the step-by-step process of raking through the coals of their life on Earth, even their life in the spirit world, and forgiving themselves for every action, every part, every aspect of their life, portrayed and remembered within them.

It is a formidable task, and one that can take a very long time indeed. There are two ways to garner forgiveness. There is the way of the mind and the spirit who walks upon the path of the natural man and the way of the divine that walks upon the Divine Path, receiving the great gift of God's Love, which brings as a blessing, forgiveness and healing.

**Question:** *Is it okay to have money, or do we have to live humbly in order to be spiritual?*

This is a difficult question to answer in your day and age. It is a day and age of excesses, excessive consumption, excessive material desires, excessive efforts in the material plane to reinforce your reality and your material thinking. It is acceptable to have money in your world, money is necessary, so it is acceptable to utilise the coin of the realm, so to speak.

How is that utilised? Does it bring harmony, or does it continue to feed the desires and the excess within the material individual, so that they are out of harmony with God? If this is the case, then they are indeed counterproductive in their spiritual journey. If your resources are used for good, for yourself, for others, in the way of service, in the way of generosity, in the way of creating a harmonious life for yourself, then indeed, there is no reason for condemnation or to suffer the consequences within.

**Question:** *A lot of spiritualists believe that you can sage the area and then you would remove negative entities or energies. Is that true?*

The actual plant and smoke that is derived from the plant opens a portal, a door for spirit to utilise this expression of God's creation to help cleanse and bring some light to a particular situation or individual. It does require the active energies and blessings of those spirits in order to transform and utilise these energies and these conditions in order to bring the blessings forward. The act of saging in itself has no real benefit without this interaction.

**Question:** *Lots of people are doing ayahuasca or going on mushroom trips because they believe that would open up their spiritual selves. Do you suggest that is a good idea for them to do?*

There are many layers to this answer. God gave His creation to humanity to utilise as it will. Humanity, being a creative force in the world, finds ways and means of utilising these plants and other things for their benefit. If these things are utilised in an environment that is safe, guided, protected and done so in a moderate way, there can be benefits. Unfortunately, many who are engaged in these things are not so spiritually attuned, adept or progressed that they may guide and protect the individual in question. There can be great harm and there can be great danger.

It is a difficult question to answer. Ideally, the individual does not require such assistance. Ideally, the individual can receive and experience tremendous and powerful visions and experiences that are of the soul and are of God. This is the highest and purest form of experience of the soul and this is encouraged. Indeed, we come to help the individuals to come to that place, so that they may have a deep experience that will increase their faith, understanding and realisation of who and what they are as a soul and who and what they can experience as a soul.

Yet, many are eager to try a quick method, something that bypasses the work and efforts required to reach those places of understanding. These individuals often are not aware of the danger that they may be in, or will often have an experience that is negative, harmful and hurtful, very disorienting. Some suffer from great mental anguish afterwards and great mental distortions. It takes a great deal of effort to right this imbalance and difficulty.

I do not recommend the easy road. There may be a time in humanity's future where these substances are used for the benefit and health of the individual, but at the moment, what we see is an abuse of something that God has created. It is harmful often and will not bring the results that are permanent. Rather, they are temporary, because they often create conditions within the mind that are a distortion and are not practical in terms of spiritual progression.

If you mean the new world, although I would not use the word "order" beside it, because this indicates that there is some governing body, some great body that will control and create this new world. They will also come because the conditions that are changing within the world will insist upon them. There are many forces at work, many layers of energetic influences, as you would call them, that are coming into play and working on each and every soul that inhabits the planet, and those souls in the Earth planes that are seeking are benefiting simultaneously from all that is taking place at this time.

The centre of these changes begins with the Earth plane and moves out toward the darker planes below the Earth plane and the lighter planes above the Earth plane. Many, many spirits and souls are being affected and will desire to be in harmony, in that place of peace and in that place of light. This is ultimately the goal, that that choice will be made clearly, understood clearly and this choice will be honoured and will dictate the journey that mankind will take in the future.

**Question:** *There are lots of mediums out there who say they channel Jesus or yourself. How can we trust that who you say you are is really coming through?*

When a Celestial Spirit comes to a medium, they bring their love to them and that love is a reflection of Divine Love. It is Divine Love. When the medium feels that unique quality of Love that is Divine Love, then this is an indication that it is a Celestial Spirit that is coming to make rapport with the individual. There are also bright spirits who have a substantial amount of Divine Love within their soul, but have not yet reached the Celestial Kingdom. They too carry this signature, this blessing of Love.

Each medium must put the spirit through their paces, so to speak, must feel comfortable with that spirit, must come to know and recognise what Divine Love feels like. There is no other real proof. Many psychics will see spirits and there are spirits who can pretend to be certain individuals, such as Jesus. Yet, is that Love, that condition of Love, present? It is not natural love and often these spirits possess a measure of natural love. It is Divine Love that will prove the existence of that spirit as a Celestial being and one that can be trusted.
