# Be the Living Lesson of Love and Light

December 14<sup>th</sup>, 2025

Online Circle of Light, Sechelt, BC, Canada

Confucius received by Al Fike

God bless you, beloved souls. I am Confucius. Yes, Rejoice, beloveds. Rejoice, for you know the key that opens your heart and your soul to the wonders that God has created within you, that key of Love that heals, that makes you whole, that takes you on the journey of soul consciousness and awakens you to the existence of your Creator.

What thing in the world could do this? Nothing could do this. It is the relationship between you and your Creator that makes this possible. As you seek out the Love of God, the Essence of the great Soul of God, this unlocks the door. This brings the awarenesses, the blessings, the revelations and the joy to your hearts. As you grow in your faith, your trust and your ability to reach God in a palpable way, so the joy grows within, so the capacity to love awakens within your soul and you are truly a redeemed child of God.

It is simple. God does not make His universe so complicated that only a few may enter into the consciousness of God, the awakening of the soul and its transformation. No, not just you, but everyone, all of you, all your loved ones, all the peoples of the world and the spirits of the spirit world. Everyone has the possibility to know this truth, to awaken to it, to apply it and have it truly be a part of their lives and their experience in life. I ask you, my beloved friends, to share the truth that you have with your brothers and sisters, to bring this truth in ways that will inspire them to seek it and to know it. The greatest inspiration of all is the light that shines in your soul, the joy that erupts from your being, the truth that is spoken through your lips. All of these things together constitute a powerful example and lesson for others.

Be that lesson, beloved souls. Be that lesson of truth, that harbinger of light, that beautiful soul expressed in the world in a way that is truly believable from others and truly understood by others. Can you do this, beloved souls, even though many of you are gentle, meek and somewhat reluctant to go into the world, that harsh place that is full of judgement, love and lacks love? Within you is a strength, a tenacity, a solidity of the beautiful souls that you are and you must trust in this. Trust in yourself. Trust in who you are in the eyes of God. If you can do this, my beloved and beautiful friends, then you will do God's work in the world, not the clanging of bells and the voices raised, the judgement and fingers pointed. No, none of this. It is in being love, that gentle light that sweeps over another and brings comfort and trust.

It is for you to find your way. For each of you are unique. Each of you carries a purpose. Each of you have your own set of gifts and abilities. As the Love of God continues to grow within you, so these abilities and gifts will flourish and bloom in all their beauty and uniqueness. Trust in this, my beloved souls, that it is inevitable that God will use you as His channels of Love. It must be so. You, my beloved and beautiful friends, have no idea of who you truly are and what you can truly give to the world, but you must venture forth and test the waters. Be strong and be of great faith, for when you do so, and each step you take is a step and a test of your own faith and your own abilities, you will find that God will guide you. You will find that the world will open to you in ways you would not imagine, but it is faith and strength and the willingness to step forward that will bring about the results that I suggest.

Be with God, my beloved and beautiful souls, precious children of God. Be His channel, His children, His light in the world, a world so bereft of light, a world so in need of love. Be that light and express that love. You will find the miracles of God shall work through you and touch many souls.

God bless you, beloveds. I am Confucius. I love you. I am with you. Many of us are with you. We encourage you, we protect you and we love you. God bless you, beloved souls. God bless you in God's Love. May you carry the flame of truth with you, wherever you go and whomever you meet. Be that channel of love and you will be blessed as you do so. Confucius loves you. God bless you. God bless you all.
