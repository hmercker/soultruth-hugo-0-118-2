# Reverence for God's Creation and the Call to Love the Earth

December 5<sup>th</sup>, 2025

Online Circle of Light, Sechelt, BC, Canada

White Feather received by Al Fike

The whispers of God's Touch flow amongst you, His gentle Voice in your ear, His Love enveloping you, so that you may know how precious you truly are in God's sight, how loved you truly are by God. As you come to acknowledge these things, may you acknowledge all of God's creation, how precious it is and how beautiful it is. Many live in conditions and situations where they cannot be close to nature and God's creation. Instead, they live in a wholly man-made condition. Yet, each of you are fortunate. Each of you live by nature and are aware of the touch of nature in your lives. This gift of the natural wonders of God's creation is important. My hope is that you will acknowledge this and appreciate this, for it is truly a blessing upon you.

I am White Feather and I come to you in love. I come to you to acknowledge your efforts to help uplift this world, this place where we have all engaged in life at some point. Many of us now, those of us in spirit, continue to revere nature, but in a different way, in a different form, for we now have the ability to create our environment as we see fit. Yet, the template has always been given by God. The true expression of God's creation, what you call “nature,” is something that is imprinted within each of us. At times, we add to, we extrapolate, the qualities and beauty of God's creation.

This is allowable. It is in harmony. Yet, for you on Earth, it is important that you revere what God has created, to protect it and to be with it in love, appreciation, perception and communion. For when you commune with God's creations, you come closer to God. When you are in love with life, and all that life is, you acknowledge the gift that is life and the gift that God gave you with life. So much needs your attention, your focus, your appreciation and love, for the world is suffering by lack of love. The world needs your love. The world needs your appreciation and efforts to protect it, because so few are able to engage in the way that you are able to engage. So few have that beautiful perception and capacities that come as the soul is awakened in Divine Love. Be that child of God in every way. Allow God's Touch to influence, inspire, uplift and flow through you to your brothers and sisters and to all creation. For when you are in that state of harmony with the Laws of Creation and the Laws of Love, then you are truly a light in the world.

My beautiful friends, it is good that you come together in prayer and pray for the Earth. You draw many of us to pray with you. I am here as an ambassador to many. I speak to you as one who speaks for many. May you be blessed, beloved and beautiful souls on the Path of Divine Love, the highest path of all, and be in harmony with all. God bless you. I am White Feather. My love is with you all. God bless you.
