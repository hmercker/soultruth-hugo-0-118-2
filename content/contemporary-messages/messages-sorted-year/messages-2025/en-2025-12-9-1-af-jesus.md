# Waves of Divine Intervention and the Call to Be God's Instruments

December 9<sup>th</sup>, 2025

Online Circle of Light, Sechelt, BC, Canada

Jesus received by Al Fike

God bless you, my beloveds. I am your brother and friend, Jesus. I come once again to pray with you and to assure you that divine intervention is coming and has come to help uplift humanity from its darkness. The mercy of God, the Love of God, the ministrations of God are focused upon this planet in ways and means that will bring insight, comfort and love, truth and light to all upon this world. For those who are receptive and willing to listen and be sensitive to the effects and blessings of these interventions, they will benefit greatly from God's Hand upon us and they will come to know God, who, for many, is a stranger.

At this time, they will come to know God and His Love. They will come to know God and His comfort. They will come to know God and His Truth and wisdom. These things will come in waves of loving mercy, of true upliftment, healing, grace and peace. They are meant for humanity, for none of you can do this by yourself. None of you is alone. God is with you all. As God's Touch pours upon you, so we in the Celestial Kingdom are able to come closer and to bring our love and attentions to you. I have been with each one of you and many others. I focus my prayers, my efforts upon this world, for it is a crucial time, a time of transformation and change, a time where humanity will shed its garb of pain and disharmony and don that of light, truth and harmony.

It comes in waves, my beloveds, and you will feel the intensity, the power of those blessings. Pray to be open and receptive. Pray to be transformed and healed. Pray to be God's instruments in the world and you will be utilised by God to intensify these blessings through you to others. You will be guided and inspired. You will feel more readily the opening of your souls to His Love. The faculties of your soul will be more acute and will be more powerful in their exercise as God's instruments, God's blessings pouring through you.

Much is coming quickly, beloved souls. As changes intensify, so do the blessings of God to comfort and uphold His children. You are all in alignment with these great waves of blessing, this beautiful anointment that comes to each one of you. Open yourselves, beloved souls. Feel the power of God's Touch and you will also feel the unburdening that comes, the healing and the joy, the opening of gifts and faculties, the transformation of the soul. The healing of the mind will also come, so it too will come into alignment with the power of soul consciousness and the power of God's Consciousness coming into your awareness, influencing all that you see, all that you are, recalibrating your desires and that which you wish to be in your life, your priorities and your focus.

As God's Hand continues to bless you, so all of these things will respond accordingly. Continue to place your desires and longings of soul to God. Do so with all the intensity of your heart, open, vulnerable and needing God's Touch, for this will open the door and awaken you to many blessings. My beloved brothers and sisters, I am with you. I come to be close and we pray together. Open yourselves wide and embrace God with all your heart and soul. Indeed, we will be lifted up, for we follow the Laws of Love and feel its blessings upon us.

God bless you, beloveds. I am Jesus and I love you, I embrace you and I walk with you always. God bless you, beloveds.
