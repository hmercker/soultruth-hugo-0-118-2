# To Truly Recognize God's Whispers in Your Souls

January 12<sup>th</sup>, 2025

Barbados Retreat, St Michael, Barbados

Keea Atta Kem received by Maureen Cardoso

I am Keea Atta Kem. While your circle is not complete, meaning those who are yet to arrive and join us in our prayer but also, more specifically, in our time of development, I wish to come and encourage you to truly search within yourself, that deep place within your soul, to begin to truly recognise the whispers that are held within the soul that come from God, the purpose that lies within you, the ways in which your instrumentality has the potential to change from this time that we will spend with you in your development. We will honour what it is that our role holds in this time together. More than ever before, you need to truly consider how it is that you will allow God's plan to move through you that is for you.

This beautiful country in the area of islands known as the Caribbean needs the blessings and the Touch of God's Love. We will partner with those who are present and those who live within this beautiful area of the world and desire to have their instrumentality serve in a deeper way. We will partner with you in a new way, providing you allow it, providing that you can search within yourself and put down those things that are of the material that truly distract you from the spiritual and allow the spiritual part of you, your essence, to come forward.

You all desire this. You are all on the cusp of great change, yet you must search within yourself what it is that holds you back from being in greater alignment in harmony with the Presence of God and the essence of your light. This is a time and a gathering that can mark great change in your life, setting you on a new trajectory where it is God's Presence and God's Essence that you are most attuned to. You are all well aware that it is your soul that God communes to and through, just as your Celestial Angels do.

Let us enter into this powerful time of transformation, dedication, perseverance and tenacity. Allow the grace of God to make its mark through your life, individually and collectively. I am Keea Atta Kem, one of the great Celestial teachers who surround you, support you, love you and will be present in this time of development. God bless you.
