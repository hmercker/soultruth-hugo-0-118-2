# The Concept of Time: in the Reality of the Soul, Time Does Not Exist

February 23<sup>rd</sup>, 2025

Beams of Light, Abbotsford, BC, Canada

Augustine received by Maureen Cardoso

It is your teacher, Augustine. I come in response to some of your conversation, for we think it is very interesting, the way mortals experience time and their relationship with time. You may be wondering, do I have enough time to fulfil my purpose while I am here on Earth, as the dear daughter had mentioned. Though lightly stated, these things, when you state them in some form of lightness, are a true concern that you hold.

I put back to you to review how it is that you use the time that is a blessing and a gift in every day. In truth, you are not guaranteed any extended period of time. In truth, you only have now, the present moment in time. If you put into consideration what this really means in the way that you use the energy of time throughout your day, in pockets of your day, in an extension of a week, a month, a year, your decades, you sometimes put markers or goals upon yourself. *“In five years' time, I wish to achieve such and such”.* How is it that you come to achieve these goals, that they materialise? It is that you put focus and attention into the ways that you use your time.

When you have something that is very passionate to you, you say how quickly the time goes by, because you are immersed full-heartedly, body, mind and soul, in the joy of the ways your creative energy flows within time. It is not that you will achieve every potential in your life while you are here on Earth in your earthly body. Remember that, ultimately, your essence is soul. That mission that you have been imbued with, that you feel an instilling of within your soul, carries on with you as you transition into the spirit world, potentially becoming more refined and defined as you progress spiritually.

This gift of time that you have on Earth as an earthling, a soul in a body with a wonderful mind, gifted by God, under time and space that your Earth is under a rhythm with, it is for you to decide, your free will choice of how you will, through your own free will, use your time, the gift that God has blessed you with. Not that I wish to come as a heavy hand, though it is often that that is my role, you need to be more alert, conscious of the way in which you allow yourself to flow through time and space while you are here.

Your spiritual life can help to guide the way your material life unfolds. As your souls develop in and awaken by God's grace, God's Love that comes into your soul, in time you will become aware of the stirrings of your soul, the strength that is awakening, leading, prompting, guiding you from within, in ways to discipline how your daily lives are unfolding, how you use your time and what you engage in. Are you fulfilling the purpose that you feel that you are meant to fulfil, or desire to fulfil? If one is not aware of their purpose, merely allow yourself to feel the loving guidance, the feelings that come from within you that are filled with goodness. That will be you fulfilling your purpose. Every soul has the purpose to express love, for love is the ultimate expression of your soul.

Consider the way your mind aligns with the blessing of the wisdom that is within the soul and its promptings. Release yourself from the distractions that provide you with an illusion of joy, a temporary satisfaction, a temporary fulfilment. Increase your choice of the way you do spend time, attuning to the wisdom that is within you. For as you do this, you grow in your ability to realise that the reality is in the moment.

Time is here and now. Do not wait for your future. Enact the blessing of the Love that is within the soul. Allow this to freely flow from you and you will be using and engaging in time in the highest way. Be creative. Allow the creative energy of your soul to flow freely.  This is using your talents and your gifts. Strengthen those things that are new to you in creative ways. Be graceful in the way that you allow the creative forces to move through you.

Use your time in this way, beloved souls, and you will never question if you have time on Earth to fulfil a purpose, because every moment in time that you allow your soul to fulfil its blessing of enacting love, kindness, joy and peace, where you align with your presence, your very essence, this is using time in its highest form. It is not a mystery. It really is not something that, innately, you are not instilled with. You only have to put your attention and focus upon it, because in that way, all energies will help you to discern what it is that truly matters in your life, that will truly make a difference. Every moment, with every breath, is an opportunity to fulfil the expression of love and to receive the gift of Love.

Grow in this way, beloved souls. Grow in God's Love, both receiving and demonstrating. You will not wonder about time, because you will have achieved a higher realm from within and the expression of time. For in the true reality of the soul, time does not exist. It merely is in all moments. Be present within yourself and discover the blessing of the real moments, those moments of soulful awareness. Then you will experience the gift of time as a blessing.

I am Augustine, your teacher, always happy to come to guide and to respond to those things that we have the opportunity of hearing, listening to and knowing about you. We can come and guide you, for we only can love you. At times, it may feel a little more direct or stern, but this too is all in love and truth. God bless you.
