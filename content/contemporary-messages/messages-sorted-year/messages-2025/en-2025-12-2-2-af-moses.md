# You Must Reconcile Mind and Soul to be God's Instruments in Times to Come

December 2<sup>nd</sup>, 2025

Direct Voice, Sechelt, BC, Canada

Moses received by Al Fike

God bless you, beloved souls. I am Moses.

Every day is a test of faith. Every day presents its opportunities to be in alignment with God's Will. Yet, there are days in your life upon the Earthly plane that continue to be a mystery where God's Will is not known. You prattle through your day seeking to know your purpose for that day. Often the mind desires to fill your thoughts with ideas and directions of action that are not necessarily of God's Will, but most certainly of your own. This is not faith in action. This is free will demonstrated. This is the condition that humanity has indeed cultivated since the beginning.

The test is, my friends, to know God's Will and to know it with such deep faith and such deep commitment that it is unmistakable, clear and powerful, a powerful motivation in your life. Are you able to clear away the clutter of mindful concerns, desires and put forth the Will of God into your life? When you are able to do this, then you are ready to bring through these manifestations that you so wish to have and to demonstrate. It is very simple. It does not require much study and complicated explanations, but true faith, true trust in God, true awareness of God's Will that comes with the opening of your soul's faculties are what will bring about these manifestations.

Nothing else will suffice, my beloved friends. There is a magic formula, but that formula is so simple that there is nothing magical about it. It is your commitment, your desire, your faith and the power of God's Love within your soul. This is the formula. This has been repeated countless times. You have heard this countless times and yet I see you continue to fall back on the material concerns and the mindful endeavours that contradict these simple edicts. How strong are you in faith, my friends? How powerful are your soul's longings? How clear are your soul's perceptions? How are you going to work with God if you are not strong in the abilities of the soul? For it is your soul and God's Soul that collaborate with this great enterprise. Indeed, your mind has its role, but it is minor. It is the power of the soul that is important and needed. Yet, you dance back and forth between these two consciousnesses. Often you do not trust what comes through with your soul's awareness. This is usually because that awareness is weak. It is given with whispers and perceptions that are often overshadowed by mindful interpretation. Thus, you do not trust yourselves on this road that God has designated for you. You find yourself floundering at times, at other times strong and full of vigour and desire to serve God.

You are still human. Indeed, as you continue to grow in God's Love, that humanness fades and is replaced by a soul deeply connected to God, a soul with great faith, the strength of faith, the strength of clarity and perception of the soul. These things must replace your humanness. For how else are you going to be leaders demonstrating this truth? The demonstration is not primarily in words. It is in the state of being. It is in the light that you carry. It is in your wise, soulful perceptions and presence with others. This is how Jesus did it in his day. This is why you still remember him and indeed, you must do the same.

Yes, it is a tall order. It requires much of you. It is not simple in terms of your present condition in the world, but the simplicity of gaining soul awareness, transformation, expression, faith and demonstration is yours to be had. It is at your fingertips. It is yours to choose. It is given freely. You have all the resources on Earth and in the Celestial Heavens to accomplish this. I remind you once again of your responsibilities and your choices. I suggest that you take time to contemplate these things, to consider them, for time is running short, my beloved friends, and the transformation of the world will happen whether you are ready for it or not. It will happen nonetheless.

If you are ready, you will ride that great wave of change and be in sync, in harmony with it. But if you are not ready, you continue to doubt and to express the stance of mindfulness and soulfulness without the two being integrated in any clear way, then you will be swept away with the rest, unable to navigate, confused and unclear, a lack of faith, a lack of strength. It must come as your soul grows, my friends. You must be intent upon that growth, that desire to serve God, that desire to truly be in alignment with your Heavenly Father. There is still time, but you must be intense and focused and true to this journey of soul awakening and transformation.

God bless you, my beloved friends. I am Moses. I come with the intensity of my desire that you may succeed. I merely reflect the desire of millions of angels, your stellar friends, bright spirits, all who wish to see you demonstrate the truth, to be the truth, to live it, so that you may be those instruments needed in times to come, a time that comes soon. God bless you, my friends. Moses is there to assist you, to offer my strength and my faith to help uplift you and inspire you, as are many. God bless you and keep you in His Love.
