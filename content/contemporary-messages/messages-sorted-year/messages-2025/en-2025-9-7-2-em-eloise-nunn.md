# Each One Here is Receiving a Gift Crafted Just for You

September 7<sup>th</sup>, 2025

Harrow Retreat, Harrow, UK

Eloise Nunn received by Elizabeth Mack

I am here. I am your beloved mother, Eloise. I come in truth, in blessings, in love to bestow upon this gathering the gifts that my Father has asked me to bestow, each one a special gift crafted just for you, one by one.

I serve God in this way at this time, for God has special purposes for each of His Celestial Angels, many, many, many of whom are here to also bless, enlighten, pray with and uplift you. You are all dear children of our Heavenly Father and Mother as one Soul, known to some of you as the great Oversoul, known to others as God. The names change, but the love remains true, complete for each one. The Essence of God is bestowed upon you, if it is your desire to receive it.

I trust that many of you who are here desire, long for, open to, aspire for, crave this Love Divine. It is the one thing. It is the one thing that will open you, change you, transform you into Love Divine. As these souls have been explaining, it will change you, bring you closer to at-onement with God, bring you everlasting joy, continuous happiness and so much love. That Love will be all that you know. All else will fall away but this Love and your commitment to the Father's Will, for the Love is so great, so powerful.

You must come closer. It will become your aching desire to come closer, healing every step of the way, joining with brothers and sisters in this Love. You are blessed. You are blessed. You are blessed with this Love. It will draw you forward onto the path that brings you home to your true home, your real home, the home of Love.

I am your sister, Eloise. I am cherished by God, as are each one of you. May you be open continuously to the gifts intended for each one of you. I thank you for this opportunity to bless you. God bless you. My love is with you.
