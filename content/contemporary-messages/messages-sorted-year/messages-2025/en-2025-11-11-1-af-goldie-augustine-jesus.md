# The Power of Persistent Love: Awakening the Soul to God's Truth

November 11<sup>th</sup>, 2025

Online Circle of Light, Sechelt, BC, Canada

Goldie, Augustine & Jesus received by Al Fike

**Goldie:** Bless you, beautiful, precious souls of Earth. I am Goldie. Today, I come as a teacher to help inspire and to bless you with words of truth. I see so much suffering in your world. Yet, each of you have your needs met. God is shining His Light upon you all. I do not see great suffering here. I see faithful souls willing to walk in the world and be instruments of light, peace and love for the world. This is beautiful to behold, all the beautiful souls here arrayed in the Father's Love.

God wishes for you to be ever closer, my beloved friends. God wishes for you to be in deep rapport with Him, so that He may bless you, guide you, keep you in the Light, protect you and infill into your consciousness the truth of the matter, the truth of life. When you receive more of this great gift of Love, you open those faculties that allow you to receive deeper truth and deeper understanding. Your beautiful hearts glow in love and joy more abundantly with each day. As you walk in the world, growing in love, being a light, being closer to God, that instrumentality, that flow of love, increases, becomes more powerful and more substantial. Those around you will feel its impact and its blessing.

It really is a very simple way. It does not require much understanding on your part, merely deep faith and deep desire to be in the grace of God's Love. Some of you have studied many scriptures and teachings. Yet, were you any further ahead in your spiritual development? Your mind absorbed many things that were lessons regarding conduct and morals. These things are good, but did you really understand your soul in the process as you continued to seek for truth?

Here we do not judge, nor do we speak much of moral conduct. We expect this of you because you are seeking the highest blessing from God. So is there a need to speak of these things? The codes and conducts of your societies, your countries, your religions, etc., are clear. If you seek to know these things, they are readily available to you. Our emphasis and our focus is on the development and growth of your soul.

As your soul grows in the Father's Love, so your behaviour changes. It is a natural outcome of this growth, that you will be morally uplifted. The expressions of your actions and thoughts will reflect the Love within your soul. This is what comes with the Father's Love, along with many other blessings and things. You see yourself differently. You see the world differently. You have far more compassion for your brothers and sisters. You look back on your own life and see where you have faltered and where you have been uplifted and have made the right choices. That faculty within you, that of your soul's mind, sees and knows, because it is empowered with God's Love. That part of you that is the expression of love, which is your soul, your whole entire soul, will express more deeply and more beautifully this love, as you grow in this love, as it expands within you and as you receive more.

It is so simple, my beloved friends. Allow yourself to awaken to the truth of the matter, the power of love within you, the power of love that continues to grow as you ask for this gift. Many, many blessings will come. Yes, you will release the burdens. You will release the tendencies toward dark thoughts and deeds. For they cannot exist alongside your beautiful soul, brightening in light with each day. You must be redeemed in Love. It comes with every prayer, with every desire and thought that seeks out God and seeks out the blessings of His powerful Touch upon you. The Holy Spirit awakens all of you. May you come to know this in all its glory, my friends, for this is where true joy is. This is where true love is. This is where true truth is. It all comes with the faculties of the soul awakening in Love and God provides for you all the ways and the means for this awakening to take place.

Will you come to know this truly and have faith in this completely, to walk along the road of love and truth in every way, in all aspects of your being? In this way, awakening comes and transformation comes. All that is a potential within you comes to be realised in time, although there is no time on this path. For the path is eternal, you will be eternal and you will walk with us in the Celestial Kingdom, my friends, and we will have that joyful communion together and with God. Do you look forward to this, my beloved friends? I look forward to you joining us there and knowing the true joy of the soul awakened, transformed and being that beautiful Angel of God. We are all His children. We are all connected by love and shall be for all eternity.

God bless you, my friends. I am Goldie. I love you, so the blessings of His Love are upon you at this moment. Open your soul wide to receive and, indeed, you will be inching ever closer to the Celestial Kingdom and true joy, true at-onement with God. God bless you. Goldie loves you. God bless you all in His Love.

**Augustine:** The need for persistence is great, my beloved friends, students of truth. I am Augustine. Indeed, our beloved Goldie gives you a gentle reminder of the truth, but what is desperately needed in you and in everyone on Earth is the persistent seeking of this truth, awakening to this truth, understanding this truth and applying this truth.

If you do so, all that Goldie speaks of will be yours. This you know within your soul. This, I promise you. Thus, you have the angels surrounding you, each one, because we are intent on your success in knowing the transformational Touch of God's Love. It is given with every prayer and every breath in prayer. Open yourselves now, my beloved friends, and receive abundantly the great gift that God wishes to give to you, His Essence of Soul given to you. It shall be given for all eternity, for this blessing can never be depleted but shall continue to be added unto you, my friends, my beautiful students.

God bless you. Augustine loves you. God bless you all in His Love.

**Jesus:** I too come, your brother and friend. My hope is you know me well. When I come, I need not even identify myself, but your hearts will leap with joy that I am with you. For I come in love, the great Love of God, and shall be with you in love always. My beloved brothers and sisters, I am Jesus and I come to pray with you today. God bless you, beloved souls. My love is with you. God bless you.
