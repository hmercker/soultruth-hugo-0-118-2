# The True Meaning of Christmas is Love

December 23<sup>rd</sup>, 2025

Online Circle of Light, Sechelt, BC, Canada

Thomas Aquinas received by Al Fike

Amen, beloved sister. Well said. I am Thomas Aquinas. I do not speak often through this instrument, but today I have the privilege of doing so. I am a resident of the Celestial Kingdom. It took some time for me to shed my misconceptions and ideas of what Jesus brought as truth to the world, but when I truly understood, my progress was swift and I entered the Celestial Kingdom a very long time ago now.

My friends, earnest seekers of truth, I want you to consider what is truth and how you may apply it. For much of what is said this time of year regarding our beautiful Master Jesus, is incorrect, and you are well aware of this. I urge you to spread love, kindness and generosity, the Christ Spirit working within you and touching others. Indeed, that is the true meaning of Christmas. I urge you to express that meaning in all that you do, all the events, all the connections, the gatherings and the times together. Spread love. Spread goodwill. Be generous of spirit and you will do God's work and you will honour Jesus in this way. For this is what he expressed and represented when he walked the Earth and certainly now, as he does his ministrations throughout the spheres of spirit and in the halls of the Celestial Kingdom.

He is love. He is acceptance. He embraces all and sees them all as his brothers and sisters. I urge you to do the same. All those in the Celestial Kingdom carry this attitude and this expression as they move around the Earthly plane and other planes, as they are together in celebration of the beloved Master. This is the time of joy and celebration, for we commemorate the re-bestowal of the privilege of receiving the great gift of God's Love to humanity. This is what Jesus brought to the world. This was his message, and it is as true today as it was then. I have the humble privilege of reminding you and being with you in prayer, that the truth of Christmas is the truth of love. The wonderment of Christmas is the wonderment of love. The power of this time of celebration is the time to embrace your brothers and sisters in love. Most of all, it is the time to reach out to your Heavenly Father and seek out this gift that has been bestowed upon the world, upon all souls of this world and the next.

Celebrate the wonderment of your own soul. Celebrate the opportunities that God lays before you. Celebrate the fact that you know the truth. Though simple it may be, it is the most powerful truth in the universe. Be that child of truth, my friends, and be true to God. God asks only that you love Him and only that you accept His Love. In this, all goodness comes. In this, all beauty, all light, all joy, all truth comes. It comes in the special Love that God has for you. That Love is palpable and a gift to each soul who accepts it.

Rejoice in this, truly. Rejoice in the wonderment of God's compassion and Love for each one of us, for He gives us the keys to heaven, and He does so without restrictions and without judgement, merely love. Consider this, my beautiful friends, God gives all in love. I urge each of you to give all that you can in love. In this way, you are harbingers of the truth. In this way, you are instruments of God. In this way, you honour Jesus' ministry and efforts to bring the salvation of the soul to humanity. We all work together in this way, and we are all truly brothers and sisters. As has been told to you many times, Jesus loves you dearly and you are all close and dear to his heart. Acknowledge this during this season and truly be alive and expressive in the Truth of God's Love.

God bless you, my friends. I am Thomas Aquinas and my love is with you. Be in the light. Enjoy life. Enjoy the truth that you know. Enjoy the wonderment of your own soul and acknowledge that all of this comes from God. God bless you, my friends. My love is with you. God bless you.
