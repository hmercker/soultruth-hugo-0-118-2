# I Implore You to Open and Receive Directly From Our Father

June 9<sup>th</sup>, 2025

One Nation Circle of Light, Liverpool, NY, USA

Jesus received by Elizabeth Mack

I am here also. I am Jesus. I am the Master of the Celestial Heavens. It is my honour to come here today to bless you with the Father's blessings on such an important day. My love comes to each of your souls with a special blessing for each of you.

This Love will bring you to your greatest fulfilment, the greatest harmony and the greatest opportunity of being at one with God. It is your choice to partake in this Love to the extent that you desire it. There is no higher Law, no higher Love, no greater opportunity than to partake of this Love of the Father. I implore you to open and to receive directly from our Father His blessing of this Love.

May God bless you. May you accept this blessing in full measure as it is offered at this time and every time that you choose to open to it. I love you each and every one so deeply, so dearly. I am your brother and I encourage you to come forward in your love. I am Jesus.
