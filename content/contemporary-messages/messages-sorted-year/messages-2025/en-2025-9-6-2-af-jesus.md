# This Gift Will Guide You Through the Changing Times to Come

September 6<sup>th</sup>, 2025

Harrow Retreat, Harrow, UK

Jesus received by Al Fike

(...) We know you well. We know all your hidden secrets, all your life that has come to this place, all that you are and we know all that you can be. Thus, each one of you is cared for by God. Each one of you has your protectors, your guides and your gatekeepers. Each one of you is loved and yes, you know very well your own flaws and your own challenges.

Yet, this does not restrict God from loving you. It merely restricts your acceptance of that Love. We help you to open yourself, to be vulnerable, to awaken and to accept God's Love for you. It is your choice to accept this gift, a gift that requires your prayers and asking to receive. I tell you, beloved souls, that once you have a taste of this gift, you will not turn back but will desire this gift forevermore. It shall be given forevermore, freely and abundantly.

My beloveds, you are on the precipice of great change in this world. I urge you to prepare yourselves. The most potent preparation is to prepare your souls in Love, for that is where the wisdom, the strength and the capacity to withstand the winds of change will come in. It will not be of the mind, for God now calls the souls of humanity to recognise themselves in the great firmament of God's creation, to be truly awakened and cognisant of who they are and how they may proceed in a world that will change and open its doors to those who are prepared and willing to step through to a new world, a world with greater harmony and peace, a world that must come, because the world of man cannot sustain.

My beloved and beautiful brothers and sisters, take one step forward, followed by another. You will be guided and you will find your way beyond the tumult, the confusion and the chaos. You will find your way. Remember my words, beloveds, for they are resounding within your soul and they will give you strength and clarity in the times to come.

God bless you. I am your brother. I am your friend. I am Jesus, Master of the Celestial Heavens. I am honoured to come to you today to speak of soulful truth, so that you may come to know and recognise that clarion call within each one of you to seek out the awakening that is offered through Love. God bless you.
