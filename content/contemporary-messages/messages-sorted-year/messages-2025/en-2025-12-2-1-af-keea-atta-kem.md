# Honour Who You Are in God's Creation

December 2<sup>nd</sup>, 2025

Online Prayer Circles, Sechelt, BC, Canada

Keea Atta Kem received by Al Fike

God bless you, my beloveds. I am Keea Atta Kem. How blessed we all are to have this bond of love, to be connected in this way. How magnificent is God's plan that opened the doors for this connection. How powerful is the Truth of God's Love that helped us, each one of us, to open ourselves to the wonders of our own souls and the beauty of our relationship with our Creator.

God created a wondrous universe and in that universe, we exist, we are a part of God's creation. When we acknowledge this, acknowledge all the aspects of our creation and honour it and love who we are and what we are and who God is and what God is, then we have built a firm foundation that embodies truth, light and love. How blessed we are, my beloved friends, to be within that flow of God's Will, blessings and plan. Truly come to understand what that means and what it is, but truly act upon it as our awareness grows, as our consciousness expands in God's Love, our souls eagerly drinking of the Living Waters, seeking greater truth, seeking great development and progression.

We know the key to this. We are practising this together as beautiful souls on the same journey, the journey to at-onement with God. We are blessed, my friends, beyond measure, beyond our limited understanding. We are blessed. As we progress upon that great journey of soul awakening and transformation, so the awarenesses will grow, the depth and breadth of our understanding will expand and become truly a part of us. Yes, how wondrous is God's creation, to be a part of this magnificent creation of God, His wondrous universe and know that we are loved, loved beyond measure and that we have a place, a precious place within God's universe.

My beloved and beautiful friends, continue to honour who you are, who God is, your life, your experience, your awareness, the openings and blessings that come in prayer and contemplation, the guidance that flows to you. Do not ignore this, but honour it, embrace it and truly be a part of it. In this way, we journey together, a onement of desire, effort and love that is a part of God's plan, that will bring us in alignment with that plan, show us the way beyond our fickle minds, interpretations and filtering, to that place of pure truth, pure awareness that comes with our soul's faculties awakened. Be truly God's instruments, my beloved friends. Be truly in God's beautiful consciousness and Presence. You will know the joys. You will know the knowledge, the understanding of truth. You will perceive and understand many things as your souls are awakened and transformed in the Father's Love.

God bless you, my beloved friends. We pray together upon this continuous journey that never ends. We awaken and continue to awaken and that too will never end. All that is of God's Love is eternal, expansive and includes us all in that great rush of light, love and joy. God bless you all. I am Keea Atta Kem. I love you all. I pray with you now, along with many others, I pray with you and I am with you. God bless you all in His Love. I love you. God bless you.
