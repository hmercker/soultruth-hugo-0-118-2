# Much Work to be Accomplished Touching Souls

September 3<sup>rd</sup>, 2025

Harrow Retreat, Harrow, UK

Mary received by Elizabeth Mack

I am Mary. I will be with you today, all of you. There is much work to do today and much compassion to be shown to your brothers and sisters who are without the great Love that you have within you. We will touch their souls with this compassion, with the Love within you, ever so gently and greatly, their souls will know. They will be given help. They are bereft of hope at this time, but it will help them feel that there is hope. Bless each one. Whether you speak or just be, bless each soul who comes to you today.

This is the work of the day, as well as other gifts that will be expressed that you may or may not be aware of. There will be healings. There will even be joy. We will be with you to bring to you those who you can touch most readily and even one who may not be receptive but will be curious.

God bless you as you travel through this city today. You will feel us and they will feel you. I am your sister, loving you each step of the way. I am Mary. God bless you.
