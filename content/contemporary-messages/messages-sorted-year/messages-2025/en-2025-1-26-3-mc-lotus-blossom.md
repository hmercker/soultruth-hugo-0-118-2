# Soul Transformation With Divine Love

January 26<sup>th</sup>, 2025

Starpoint Retreat, Christ Church, Barbados

Lotus Blossom received by Maureen Cardoso

I am Lotus Blossom. You have all been given a great opportunity to come to understand a little more about this teaching of God's Love, the blessing that it has upon your souls and the transformation that will occur. There has been much that has been given to you through God's Essence, through the angels speaking to you and to these beautiful souls, sharing their experiences, helping you to come to understand, to inspire you and to open desires within your souls, planting small seeds.

Now, it is for you to take up the mantle, to allow yourself to be blessed by God each day. At any moment in time, this Love is available to you. All it requires is for you to open, for it is a Love that is continuously flowing. The only one that cuts off any communing with God is you. Is that not wonderful to know, that it all rests in your own hands, your own timing and your own pace?

You have learned a lot and you have received a lot. In these next days, allow the healing blessings, the Substance of Love that has come into your soul and the teachings that have been shared to assimilate into you. Continue to open your souls as you desire to receive this blessing, to create a loving relationship with God and with your angel friends.

We are willing to serve you, but we will not interfere in your own choices. As long as you ask and as long as you invite us, we will be there and we will walk with you. Even as you pray, invite us to sit with you, because each one who walks this path has a Celestial angel designated to their progression. It is not that you need to know our names, but rather that you sincerely desire to have us with you.

It is our promise that we serve God in this way. We are very invested in the mortals of Earth and the changes that are happening on Earth, the balance that is coming and the harmony that is coming. We are a ways away from that, but know that each moment, each drop of Love that you receive, you are adding to the change. You are adding to God's plan for humanity to live in peace and live in love.

We are here to serve you. I am Lotus Blossom. God bless you.
