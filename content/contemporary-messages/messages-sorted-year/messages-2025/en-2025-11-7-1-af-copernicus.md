# Beyond the Material: Discovering the Many Layers of God's Creation

November 7<sup>th</sup>, 2025

Online Circle of Light, Sechelt, BC, Canada

Copernicus received by Al Fike

God bless you, beloved souls. I am Copernicus. I continue to study the Earth, as I did when I walked this Earth of yours and viewed it. It saddens me that with all the research, all the scientific discoveries and explorations, still there is much to be learned and understood about the workings of your planet, the many layers of life that exist, not merely the material layer, but all the layers, the spiritual and the energetic, the interaction that is not of a material nature, but still is a valid way of communication.

Within you, my beloved friends, there are many layers, as there are in all of creation. It is for you to discover within you that world of many expressions, those levels of soul consciousness, the understanding of psychic and telekinetic expressions. Indeed, some have discovered much about themselves. Yet, are they widely understood and accepted? No, they are greeted with suspicion. For the world continues to see reality, as they call it, from a strictly material viewpoint. Yet, each of you is eager to discover other levels and aspects of your being, and aspects of God’s Being and expression. It is important for you to step forward upon your journey of discovery and understanding of what life truly is.

My beloved friends, In all the time I have been in the spirit world, and that is several hundred years, I feel as if I have just scratched the surface of truly knowing and understanding God’s universe. I wish to tell you and encourage you to continue in your efforts to come to know yourselves and to come to know the world. There is much to know. Yes, your scientists are discovering the minutiae of the material world. This, too, is interesting and exciting, but they only see half of the picture at most. There is so much more to discover and understand. There are so many more dynamics to realise and to acknowledge as truly part of God’s creation. As you grow in soul, the perceptions of your soul will help you to come to understand these things and to realise the many layers of life that God wants you to know, to understand and to experience these things. For God has given you the capacities, the gifts and abilities to see, to know, to feel, to perceive and to love.

It is for you, my beautiful friends, to complete the picture for others, to help guide them along the way, the journey of discovery. The world will open itself to you and to humanity if you are open and willing to accept and embrace all that is of the world, all that is of God’s creation. I come to help you to discover these things, as do many others. We will continue on this beautiful journey. Each of you will have a remarkable journey toward truth and knowledge, awakening of soul faculties and abilities, all these things that come with God’s Love. Seek them out, my friends. You will have the assistance and the ability to have the knowledge, the awakenings and the truth of the matter.

God bless you. I am Copernicus. I come to encourage you upon your journeys. I encourage you to continue to seek out God’s Love, which opens the doorway to many things, to all of truth and all of God’s creation and all that God is.  God bless you, my beloved friends. God bless you.
