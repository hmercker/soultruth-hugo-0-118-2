# You Are Part of God's Plan for the Healing of the World

November 30<sup>th</sup>, 2025

Online Circle of Light, Sechelt, BC, Canada

Jesus & Mary received by Al Fike

**Jesus:** The world waits for healing and redemption. I come. I am your brother and friend, Jesus. I come to encourage you to be a part of God's plan that will be the healing and redemption of this world. We are all connected to this world of yours, whether we live in the Celestial Kingdom or whether you are on the Earth plane in the fleshy body. We are all one family, one planet spinning through the cosmos. In this, we are interconnected. I must say, we are interconnected with others who live on other planets, for every soul that is a creation of God is connected to God's creation of souls. We seek and struggle and make effort to bring light and truth, joy and peace to the world.

You upon the Earthly plane, we in the Celestial Heavens and many, many more are engaged in this effort, this tremendous effort, to bring peace to your world. For if peace reigns upon the planet Earth, then peace reigns throughout all the spheres of spirit and the connection between Earth and heaven is less inhibited by the conditions of humanity. Indeed, in that peace and light, many other souls will come to be a part of and to connect with this world. God has a plan that stretches far beyond your imaginings. God's desire to bring this world back into harmony involves many, many souls, many souls making choices and efforts and prayers to bring peace to this world. You are a part of that collective of souls that God gathers together, inspires, guides and blesses so that you are truly a part of His plan and that you are in alignment with all that is meant to be.

My beloved brothers and sisters, we are as one body, working for light, for truth, for love. We are connected together. We are a family. Think of yourselves as part of this family, the family of truth, a family that aspires toward God's Love, a family that has subscribed to service, to walk in the world as God's instruments, to be in the world as God's instruments that are of a spirit body. We are no different, my beloveds, other than some have souls that have been redeemed by God's Love and some are upon the path towards this redemption. All are as one and, in many ways, all provide an equal effort, an equal responsibility, an equal presence within God's plan.

Acknowledge this, my beloved brothers and sisters. Acknowledge your part. Acknowledge who you are. Acknowledge your gifts. Acknowledge the power of your own soul. All will come into alignment with God's plan and God's great Soul. We are a part of all that is in this plan. In God's great Soul we find our habitation, our connection, our joy. Seek out His Love so that you may know and feel and experience the unity, the onement that comes with a soul redeemed by God's Love. There will be joy, my beloved friends. There will be truth, there will be light and there will be purpose, a purpose that you may all come to know that is truly a part of your soul, that budding kernel of truth emerging into your consciousness.

My beloveds, we continue to serve God. We continue to grow in His Love, which is a journey eternal. We are truly brothers and sisters, seeking out God's guidance, seeking His comfort, His Hand upon us. May you know this from the very depths of your being to the consciousness that you carry. May you know the Will of God, the Love of God, the Truth of God and in this you will know great joy and great peace.

God bless you, my beloved and beautiful friends. I am your brother. I am Jesus and I love you so. God bless you, beloveds. God bless you.

**Mary:** I am Mary. The world is in great need of you, my beloveds. Seek to be a light in the world. Seek to uplift those who are downtrodden, who are hungry, alone and lost. Seek to love the unlovables. Seek to love all, and you will in this do God's work, God's bidding.

It is for you to choose each day how you might serve God. When you say a prayer that includes a desire to serve God, then the response will come and the guidance will come clearly and powerfully. God is eager to utilise each of you in service of light and truth to bring peace to the world. Acknowledge the power of your own soul to add light to this world and to change it with your light. Be true to God's Will, my beloveds. You will know the joy of it, the ecstasy and the bliss of it, the wonderment of it and all that is good and all that is of God.

God bless you, my beloveds. I am Mary and I come as well to uplift you and encourage you upon the path of love.
