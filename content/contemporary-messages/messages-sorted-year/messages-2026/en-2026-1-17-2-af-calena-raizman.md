# Her Journey in Spirit and Encourages All to Walk the One True Path to God

January 17<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Starpoint Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Calena Raizman received by Al Fike

My friends in love, I especially come to my dear friend and my husband. I am Calena. I want you to know about my journey to truth. I want you to know that when I entered into the spirit world after my battle with cancer, I was depleted and tired, yet God sent to me beautiful angels of love and upheld me in my recovery. That began a journey of faith for me. I had faith when I walked the Earth, yet the faith that I gained when in the spirit world was far deeper and wider than what I had before. Some of the beliefs that I carried when I walked the Earth were no longer relevant and no longer important. I shed that cloak of religion and I donned a new cloak, the cloak of truth, which is beyond religion. I realised truly that God loved me and that Love was tangible and beautiful.

There was no argument within my mind and my soul when I came to know God as I do. My prayers to receive the great gift of Divine Love were intense and consumed me in the reverie that it brings. The Holy Spirit that brings it was my friend, was the nurturing source for my soul. Now I am in the Celestial Kingdom. Now I know and am in complete alignment with Truth and Love and God. I know who Jesus is, for I have met him on several occasions. I have seen him here with you, for I have been here. My beloved George, every time you are here, I am here. When you think of me, I am close. You see, the bonds of love bring us all together, heart to heart, soul to soul. My beloved, I have not left you. I continue to be with you. I continue to pray for you. I continue to uphold you and support you, for I am your soulmate. We are destined to be together for all eternity.

My dear friend and sister, allow the Love to pour into your soul, for it is the redemption that Jesus talked of. It is the saving grace that comes and is available to every soul. For all of you, beautiful souls, continue in your efforts in prayer. Continue to seek at-onement with God, and you will find through these efforts in your prayers and your collaborations, that you will find the truth that will be crystal clear to each one of you. It will be a powerful motivator and inspiration for your work and for your journey, to all truth, to all that is love, to all that can bring love and truth.

Oh, my friends, I am happy to have this opportunity to speak, to acknowledge my loved ones, to acknowledge truth. For everyone takes their own journey, everyone their own path. Yet indeed, as you open your soul and open your mind to God and all that God has to give, that journey merges with others and that truth emerges with the truth that others carry. It is a mighty stream in the direction of the progress of the soul and at-onement with God. My beloved friends, know that many of us gather around. Many of us are seeking to uphold you, to pray with you and to help you towards clarity, towards the truth of the soul, the Truth of God's Soul and the relationship between the two. This is what is important. The ideas and precepts of the churches may or may not bring one to God. It is up to the individual and their own prayers, their own desires, their own efforts to be with God that brings them to our beautiful Creator.

Nothing else can truly do this, no books, no Bibles. Nothing can truly bring the soul to God. It is up to that individual and their openness, their desire to be with God that shall bring the truth forward. If that soul is open to God and open to His Love, then they too will know the New Birth, the beautiful transformation of the soul. It is open to everyone, no matter their religion, their culture, their skin colour, their life's experience. This truth is open to everyone, available to everyone. If they but believe and subscribe to it, it will become a real thing within them. It will become a beautiful experience that they may avail themselves to as they pray and seek it. It is not withheld, but given freely.

My beautiful friends, thank you for listening to me. My dear George, know that I am close, will always be so. Do not shed any more tears for me, but be happy, because I am in the happiest place you could ever be. In the Heavens of Love I reside. I am joyful beyond joyful, fulfilled beyond fulfilled, and loved beyond love. What more could I want? What more could any of you want, but to be in that place, in the joy and wonderment of the Celestial Kingdom? I am there waiting for you all. I shall pray for each one, that you may enter into this place soon and quickly and with joy in your heart.

God bless you, beloveds. I am Calena. I love you. I am here. I have been here. I will remain here to support you all. God bless you.
