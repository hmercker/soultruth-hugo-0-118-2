# The Angels of Heaven Come to be With You in Grace and Humility

January 7<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Barbados Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Jesus received by Al Fike

I am Jesus, and I too will accompany you upon this journey and bring the power of my soul and blessings to you as God's channel, as God's instrument. The hallmark of those of us who reside in the Celestial Kingdom is humility and grace. This is what brings us so far.

We must travel to the earthly plane. We do so with love. We do so with humility. We bring our grace with us to uphold you and uplift you. We come to be with you in this time of need, this time of desire, this time of upliftment, this time of learning and experiencing the truth of the soul.

God bless you, my beloveds. I shall remain with you. My prayers are with you and many angels surround you in the grace of God's Love. We are here for you and for the world. God bless you, beloved souls. My love is with you. God bless you.
