# The Path is between you and God

February 9<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Online Circle of Light, Nashville, TN, USA

George Gurdjieff received by Jimbeau Walsh

I am here, your brother in Christ, George Gurdjieff, Papa G.

I am drawn once again into your luminous circle so filled with sincere desire to receive the essence of our Creator, and I wish to speak to those who struggle and remind them that even if they are new to this path of Love Divine, they have taken a giant step towards at-onement with God.

Let us revisit the spiritual seeker, who, underneath it all desires happiness, joy, and freedom, and may follow an intellectual path of knowledge, reading all the books, going to lectures and seminars; or the path of yoga, the yogi, fasting, pure diet, breathing, postures and asanas; or the path of the monk who prays and recites the rituals of words written, and who also may fast and abstain from worldly pleasures. I tell you that whatever path appeals most to your nature, ultimately it is between you and your Creator. I met many teachers, many gurus, and at first, I will admit, I wished them to enlighten me, fill me with some wisdom, give me the secret of the universe, show me the proper postures, the right books etcetera. And of course, one can cross continents to meet such people or follow them around the world, attend every session and still wonder, why am I not feeling this happiness, feeling this love? And so, in each case, it is different, but I will say as a rule, some great shock comes into one’s life, maybe something like bankruptcy, it may be the death of a spouse, a loved one, maybe a serious injury, it may be war, famine, to the extreme of life and death. If this kind of shock awakens one to a world living in a dream state, one finds oneself in a state of shock, and in that state, if pursued rightly, great things can happen. The first requirement is: humbleness, going before the Creator with an open heart and a great soul longing. It does not matter the words, because the intention is responded to, and if one can take a heart in fear and open it in trust and faith, one moment of true longing, true prayer will produce divine results, for the heavenly Father responds.

So, wherever you are on this journey, and whenever you feel unworthy or that you have somehow regressed to a state of fear, anger, or judgment, find that place of humility that seeks out the Creator’s love. Open your heart. Relax your mind. Allow the blessing of Almighty God to come into your hearts and souls, and what was once a struggle shall become your very being permeated in bliss, in love, awakened, and arisen. Possessing some kind of erudite knowledge of the reality of the will of God that astonishes you in every moment. What a way to walk through the world and make a difference. And the other paradoxical thing I might add is the greater this soul-possession of the love of God, the greater the wisdom, the greater the sense of peace, the more one becomes a servant, willingly, joyfully, prayerfully, and humbly.

I wish to thank my spiritual grandson for being open to receiving this message from me and for all of you dear souls, so beautiful, so blessed. May the joy of God’s love be yours, and the knowing of your walk into eternity is blessed and bright beyond what you can imagine. Trust these words and above all trust in God. Have faith. Rejoice. I am your brother and friend in Christ, a redeemed soul in the celestial kingdom.

I am George Gurdjieff. Go in peace.
