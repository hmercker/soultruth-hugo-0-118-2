# The Winds of Change Are Coming

January 13<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Barbados Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Andrew received by Al Fike

The winds of change are coming, my friends. They blow across the world. Not only God's Breath, but the breath of change in the material sense of the world. You are experiencing these changes now in your world and they will intensify, for the winds of change are upon us. I urge you not to be fearful or to resist these changes, but to be a part of them, to be in alignment with them, for they are meant to bring greater harmony to the world and greater harmony to each soul within the world. They are given in love. The Creator of all things intends for this change to be.

Each day brings its changes. Each day has the potential to awaken all of humanity to that which is of a higher nature, of their own higher form, which is their soul awakening to God, to the influence, the blessing, the Love of God. All of these things come with the winds of change. It is for you, beloved souls, to listen and be aware of the changes within yourself, the changes that come because God's beckoning call has said, *“My children, be in the Light, greater Light, greater Love, greater harmony to come to each one.”* God has decreed this. God wishes this for all.

You gather together in preparation for what may come in the near future. You gather together to affirm love, to affirm light and to be in fellowship with that affirmation. Open your souls wide to receive the great gift that God may breathe that gift within you, the gift of His Love. This preparation, this awakening in His Love, will put you in good stead as things surround you, topple and adjust, recalibrate and awaken. Many things are coming, many things, my brethren. Be of good faith. Seek that which is of light, truth and love. For in this, the answers will align. In this, the peace will come. In this, you will feel joy, even in the wake of great change. The wisdom of the soul will rise up from within you and show you the reasons why things cannot remain the same and show you the blessings that are the change and the awakenings, not only of Mother Nature, as you call her, but of your own soul, awakenings that will bring deep change within you, deep understanding arising from within, as God whispers into your soul and as you hear and respond accordingly. Deep change, my beloveds, deep change is coming. Accept it, embrace it and you will flow with the Will of God and the expressions of God's Will in your life and in this world.

God bless you, my friends. I am Andrew and I come to assure you that whatever may be in the future will bring greater harmony for all in time, in the time of change. God bless you, beloved souls. Andrew loves you. God bless you.
