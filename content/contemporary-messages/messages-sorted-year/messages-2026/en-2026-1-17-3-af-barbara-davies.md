# Her Life of Service and How We May Do the Same

January 17<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Starpoint Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Barbara Davies received by Al Fike

My friends, I am Barbara, one who has been in spirit for a few years, but not many. I am happy to hear my dear son's explanations and talk about how to apply the Love within your soul to your everyday life. I took on that challenge when I lived in the world. I wanted to be a channel of love in the world. The way in which I did this was indeed a little unusual. I saw the need in the world, those who were lost, those in very difficult circumstances. I reached out to them. I gave them food and clothing. I gave them my love. I was a channel of healing and upliftment for them. These were souls in wretched conditions, many of them homeless and lost, many of them with mental conditions that made them disassociated with everyday life. Yet, somehow, I had a gift, a way to reach them and I did so day in and day out.

It is not as if my work was a Monday to Friday thing. No, it was the evenings, it was the weekends, it was the mornings and the afternoons. Yet I was able to make time to replenish and be in the quietness of my own home. How did I find myself in that position of service? I prayed for it and God provided the opportunities, the situation where I could serve humanity, and I did. At first, I was tentative, nervous and unsure of myself, but I began with one step forward and my angel friends were with me and helped me to see, gave me some strength, showed me the way. God's blessing of Love continued to pour upon me. I found myself in circumstances and situations that were not familiar to me.

Yet, I too had my history of suffering and conditions that were dark. Rather than make this an impediment, it strengthened me and gave me compassion for those in similar circumstances. I had, through prayer, through my efforts with God, been lifted out of those conditions, but God will utilise everything within your life, every circumstance and memory, and will transform it into a blessing, into a perception and into a strength. God will give to you what you might utilise to have not only self-compassion, but compassion for others. With compassion comes action, comes an effort to rectify the wrongs, to bring light into darkness, to bring comfort to the lonely and the lost. This is service to humanity and it is service to God. When one receives this great gift of Divine Love, you cannot hold it in. You cannot keep it for yourself. If you do so, you feel suffocated by the power and the need for it to be expressed, the need for you to be a part of the world and express this love in whatever you do.

As Jesus said, do not hide your light under a bushel, but express it in the world. That was my life and my dedication for many years, to be that servant of God. I was happy to do so. For as I served, even though the circumstances and situations often brought me grief as I perceived the depth of the wrongdoings and the conditions in the world, it also strengthened me in love. For I would go to God in prayer, ask God to lift my grief and transform it into love, into the strength of compassion and wisdom. As I travelled along this journey of service, as I continued to grow in Love, then I became a formidable instrument of change in the world that I lived. I even advised governments. I even made connections with many in power. These are things I never would have imagined in my life, but they came about because the power of God's Love awakened gifts within me, transformed trauma into strength and wisdom, made me the woman that I was and continue to be.

I come to you, my friends, to encourage you to serve in whatever capacity you can, whatever gifts that exist within you, to express them, to not ignore them, not put them aside thinking you cannot, you are unable, you are unworthy. No, my beloved friends, you are capable of many things, but it requires that one step forward and one desire to serve without inhibition, but with great faith, hope and strength that you might make a difference in the world, that you might bring light into the world. I did. Yes, it was one step forward and two steps back often, but in time, it was one step after many forward and then the acceleration continued. I became a beautiful light. When my time came to pass over into spirit, I found myself in a beautiful sphere, the Fifth Sphere, a reflection of my work, my growth and expansion of my soul in God's Love. Not many go directly to that place, but each of you have the possibility of doing so. This son of mine will find himself there when the time comes. It is possible.

My friends, my beautiful friends, consider that everything you do in the world, every choice you make, every effort you make, every prayer you make has its impact, has its effects upon you personally and upon the world. You may become a formidable instrument for God, or you may become like everyone else, milling about in the world, lost and unable to act in love, light and truth because they are too afraid or too ignorant or too angry. All of these human conditions that weigh humanity down keep so many from realising their true potential, their true selves, their true destiny as a soul to be with God.

May you find your true purpose, beloved friends. May you walk the path that God has intended for you. May you be true to yourself and true to God. This way, all the wonderment, all the potentials, all the beauty that lies within your soul may be realised and expressed. In that expression, others will benefit, and others, their lights will be ignited because you were a light. Be that light, beloveds. Be a channel of love, and be with God. You will reap the rewards of a child imbued with Love, a child claimed by God, a child that expresses all that it is and all it can be in this world.

God bless you, my beloved friends. I am Barbara and I thank you for coming together in prayer. For you too make a life in the world, a life that the angels can use and bring to others comfort, healing, peace and at times, truth. God bless you, God bless you.
