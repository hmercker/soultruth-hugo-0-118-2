# Do Not Idle: Stand Firmly in the Simple Truth of God's Love

January 4<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Barbados Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Bartholomew received by Al Fike

I come to greet my charges. I come to enforce the Will of God with you and to encourage you upon that road of service, for this Circle of Light has great purpose and great destiny. Consider this, beloved souls, that you were brought together for a purpose and that you have great purpose. Each of you carries a calling, a gift that God intends to use to uplift not only those around you in this place that you reside, but this great area of islands, nations and peoples. In the coming times, your voices will be heard, the message will be given, the true way to salvation will be shared. As you shine in the Father's Love, so others will feel your presence, your message and will benefit from your gifts to uplift and to heal and to inspire the soul to open to God.

There is much work to do. I suggest that you do not waste your time in idle pursuits and in false doctrines but grasp the simple Truth of God's Love and make this your focus, your prayer and to apply this Love in everything that you are and everything that you do. For it requires application. It requires expression. It requires your understanding and for you to embrace this Truth within all of you, within all the aspects of your life, within all the days and moments of your life.

You cannot go out into the world as God's instruments and be ambivalent or walk upon two paths. There must be the unity and the strength of the commitment of the soul that knows and understands the truth. You must dive deep into your soul; commune there and truly be in alignment with God. In this way, God's guidance and Will will come to you in such a beautiful and forceful presence that there will be no doubt. There will be no turning back. There will be the road ahead, the road that God will light, guide and show to you what is required and what will be. Do not shirk your responsibilities, but seek in prayer each and every day guidance. Seek to grow in the Father's Love. Seek to shed all that which is contrary to His Love and seek a mighty inflowing, that which shall transform you and make you whole in the eyes of your Creator and bring you into at-onement with all that is holy and of God.

There is no other way to do this, my beloved friends. You are well aware of this truth. I urge you to live it with every breath and to be the truth with everything that you do. Oh, my beloved friends, the time grows short and you must avail yourself of these calm waters before the storm. I say again, do not idle in your time, but be forthright in your pursuit of soul awakening and transformation, for this is what is required in the world at this time, leaders, those of light, who are willing to step forward and be claimed by God and to be God's emissary, to show the way for God's embrace of Love to transform, heal and uplift. You cannot do this for another, but you can show the way. You can be an example of the way. You can be a light that will help light the way. Do this and you do God's work. Be this and you are truly a child of God.

My beloved friends, God has gathered you together for a purpose. He shall show you what that purpose is and how it may be accomplished. May you be blessed upon the road of awakening, of knowing and truly embracing the truth. May God's Love be known to you in all its aspects and wonderment. May you truly walk this path of truth and be God's instruments, channels of Love in the world. I am Bartholomew. I am with you. I shall uphold you. This is my promise and this is my work, to uphold my brothers and sisters of these islands and nations and to be an instrument for change and reconciliation with God, our heavenly Father. God bless you, my beloveds. I am with you. I am with you and I love you. God bless you.
