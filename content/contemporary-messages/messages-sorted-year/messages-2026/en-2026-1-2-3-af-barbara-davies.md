# Faith and Desire Will Open the Doors to Your Instrumentality

January 2<sup>nd</sup>, 2026

Barbados Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Barbara Davies received by Al Fike

Oh, what children we are when we inhabit the Earth. See so very little, understand so very little. Yet it is faith that carries us, is it not? Having faith in our true selves, having faith in God's Will, having faith that we are turning into beautiful lights, shedding the darkness, the distortions, the lack of love and the judgements we carry. All of these things, [our beautiful Augustine](/contemporary-messages/messages-sorted-year/messages-2026/en-2026-1-2-2-af-augustine/) wishes you to be, that beautiful channel of love, to shed all that inhibits this, to be in alignment with God's Will.

My friends, each of you tries and I understand the impediments, for it was not that long ago that I inhabited the Earth. I recognised so often my failures and my faults. Yet, I also knew that I was loved by God and that I did God's work. As imperfect as I was, God utilised me in many ways and I wanted to be an instrument. I wanted to be a light and I wanted God to use me. This is the key, my friends, to want this with all of your heart. In that wanting and desire, the guidance comes and the opportunities manifest. God's Will is done, however imperfectly, it is done nonetheless. You will feel joy and great gratification in what may come through you. It will surprise you. It will also challenge you, but it will be rewarding and add a dimension and depth to your life that could not happen any other way.

My friends, there is so much in you that still needs expression and development and awakening. Use each day to come closer to your true self and your true gifts. God will bless you along that road and show you the way. Every step, every breath, it will be given in all its wonderment, complexities and depth.

God bless you, my friends. I am Barbara. I know the struggles you have, for I had them too, but we will prevail, my friends. We will prevail in light, in truth and love. God bless you. My love is with you all. God bless you.
