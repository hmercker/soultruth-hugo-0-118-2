# Put Aside the Will of the Mind and Adopt the Will of the Soul

January 9<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Barbados Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Augustine received by Al Fike

God bless you, my students. I am your teacher, Augustine. The will of man is something highly prized in your world, to be able to choose, to control, to manipulate and to utilise the resources of this material world. These are the things that are valued in your western cultures. All over the world, there is a desire to accumulate material goods and wealth, to be an assertive, wilful individual creating a life that is part of that flow of human endeavour that involves will and desire.

We come with a different message. We come with the encouragement to put aside your will, inasmuch as your mind has its desires, and to adopt the will of the soul, which has its own set of desires. The soul has far more wisdom. The soul’s will is that of love, harmony, peace and all things that are an attribute of God, that God has put within the soul of humanity. Unfortunately, this understanding of what constitutes true will, true desire, is buried within the consciousness of the mind that desires much different outcomes and benefits than that of the soul. Clear your mind of these desires, of these attitudes, of that which is of material hungering and needs. For here you come with your souls laid bare, opening this vulnerable place and exposing the true desires of your soul, your true self. Ask for God’s Love, for this is the thing that the soul desires. This is what nourishes the soul.

This is what you truly need, my brothers and sisters, to be in alignment with God in this way and to put aside the human condition. It is the goal that we aspire to together, the thing that we wish humanity to consider and the possibility of their desires aligning with soul desires, aligning with God's desire. All of these things in harmony will bring peace, will bring the nourishment of the soul through the gift of God's Love, will bring many insights and perceptions that the soul has but is not listened to, because the mind is busy with its own agenda, its own desires, its own conception of what is needed and important in the world. Be with God, my friends. Be at peace and know the blessings of God's Love for your soul. You will come to desire differently. Your will be transmuted into something that brings you closer to God and knowing God's Will.

My beloved students of Truth, seek for the highest. Do not allow the will of your mind to interfere with the will of your soul. We will elevate and move higher and higher into the realms of peace, light and love.

God bless you, my friends. Your teacher, Augustine, is here, as are many to help elevate, to teach and to love. God bless you, my friends. God bless you all. Augustine loves you.
