# Distinguish Between Spiritual Perceptions and Soul Consciousness

January 7<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Barbados Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Seretta Kem received by Al Fike

God bless you, my beloved friends. I am Seretta Kem. This instrument has done well to describe the circle that you are engaged in. I am pleased that he has listened to our guidance and our descriptions over the years. Your souls are the most powerful, precious, beautiful and lasting part of your being. They are indeed created in the image of the Creator, but in that image is the potential to become something more than it is in birth, something more than can be accomplished through any manner of effort done, both in the physical and spiritual realms.

It can become at-one with God through this great blessing of Divine Love. This Essence given, not reflexively from God, but from prayer and effort to receive it. It must be longed for and desired for. It must be prayed for and asked for. It cannot be given any other way, for this is the law that comes with the creation of this element, this aspect of God. It is the greatest gift given to humanity. When I say humanity, I mean all of humanity in the universe.

We are here as your support, as your companions, as workers and servants of God. The Celestial Angels have come a long way to be a part of this circle. Indeed, all you must do is relax, put all mental conditions aside and pray earnestly. Open yourselves to that tender and vulnerable place of the soul and ask for it to be blessed by God. We will do the rest of what is required and we will work diligently on your behalf. Our hope is that new insights, new gifts, new awakenings of the soul will come to you as you continue with this circle of development. For some, awakening comes easy. For some, it is hard won. Yet every one of you has the opportunity to grow and to have the faculties of your soul further awakened and developed.

I assure you that each of you is worthy, each of you is gifted and, of course, each of you possesses a soul. We are not talking about awakening psychic gifts that are of the material and spirit body. We are talking about awakening soulful gifts that are of the soul and the soul consciousness. Indeed, your challenge is to dive deep beyond these other two levels of consciousness and to reside within the soul in a conscious way. It is easy to misinterpret the experience of the spirit body and mind with that of the soul mind and body. I urge you to be discerning and to yearn and ask in prayer for that discernment, that you may truly experience the soul and what it is. Be open, be innocent, be longing, feel the emotions of the soul and sometimes the sadness that is there, a sadness that can be healed with love.

There is much to do in these times together. I urge you to be diligent and focused. Though the distractions of the material world are present, do not make them a priority or a focus. Yes, you must attend to your bodies, and you must eat and do the things that your bodies require, but where is your mind? This is what I ask of you. Be cognisant of where your consciousness lies and how you may, in every breath, attune your consciousness of the soul and to God. You will find that we will be with you in this. As you make your concerted efforts, you draw us close. Our rapport will be strong, for it is the yearnings of the soul and the light of the soul that brings us to you. We will attempt to communicate with each one of you, individually and collectively. We are on an adventure together, a time that can truly recalibrate your thoughts, your motivations and your desires. A time where you may know more intimately and powerfully the workings of your own soul and indeed to reside in that place in full consciousness and to be with God in that consciousness.

There is much to do, my friends. We shall endeavour to accomplish our tasks and to be with you in harmony and grace, peace, light and love, each with their angels with them, each blessed abundantly. God bless you, my beloved friends. I am Seretta Kem and I love you. I am with you all in this beautiful Circle of Light. God bless you.
