# Finds Humour in Our Conversations About Truth

January 10<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Barbados Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Confucius received by Al Fike

God bless you, beloved souls. I am Confucius. I am greatly assured that you all know what you are talking about. You have your own words and your own thoughts. This is inevitable. No one man or woman here knows all the truth, but each of you is a walking example of truth. Each of you have something to share and to add to the body of truth of humanity's search for truth. When you see yourselves in that light, then you may add your perspective with humility and grace to the body of knowledge, to the search for truth that humanity is engaged within.

Whether they are consciously aware of this or not, everyone in the world is seeking truth in their own way and has a perspective of truth through their own journey. Honour this, for in the honouring, that is love. In the speaking of your own truth with honour and integrity, that is love. Be assured, beloved souls, that God’s Love will bring you the truth always. It will refine the truth of your knowledge in such a way that you will speak eloquently and in ways that others may hear.

God bless you, beloved souls. Teachers of truth, go forth, for you know what you are speaking of. It is your truth. It cannot be anyone else's truth and it is a powerful gift to everyone. God bless you. Oh, how I love you. Oh, how entertaining it is to hear you all in your voices, in your explanations, in your love and your concern for one another. That is beautiful. Remember, there is always a higher truth, a higher way to express that truth. It shall be refined again and again. As you grow in the Father's Love, it shall manifest in its beauty, clarity, power and love, in all the ways that you may creatively and expressively bring that truth forward.

God bless you, beloved souls. You seek refinement. You seek to be a high teacher. Indeed, at some point, that will be the case truly, for as you walk this path, truth must truly manifest through you. It may be rudimentary at this time, it may be awkward, it may be unbeautiful, but in time, it shall be magnificent and shall turn many heads, many souls who will seek you out. For you will shine the truth forth. The words, though simple, may carry a power that will change the trajectory of many souls towards greater truth and love. God bless you, my friends. Confucius loves you. I see you all. Indeed, as your plumage turns colours and becomes more exquisite, as you grow in Love, I have joy in this and love you so as you grow and change and transform. God bless you. Confucius loves you. Oh, I love you so. God bless you. God bless you.
