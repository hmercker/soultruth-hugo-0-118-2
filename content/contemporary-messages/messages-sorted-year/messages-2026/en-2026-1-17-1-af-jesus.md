# We All Walk in the Same Direction

January 17<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Starpoint Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Jesus received by Al Fike

I am your brother and friend. I am the Jesus of whom you speak of and love. I come to assure you that I love each one of you and I am close to each one of you, for you are truly my brother and sister. We all walk in the same direction toward our Heavenly Father and we shall find our way upon that path that brings us to at-onement.

The beliefs of the mind carry no weight upon that journey. It is the understanding and experience of the soul that shall bring you into the clarity, Light and Love that is of God. It matters not your struggles of belief, for they do not bring you the joy and happiness that the awareness and experience of the soul of God shall bring to you all. I am here and with you to lead the way and I shall do so for each one of you. I am here. I am truly your brother. I am truly your friend. As you, I love God with all my heart, with all my soul. I am His child, His son, as I am your brother.

God bless you, my beloveds. I come to you in love. I come to you to uphold you, to pray with you and to be with you. God bless you in the Truth of God’s Love. God bless you.
