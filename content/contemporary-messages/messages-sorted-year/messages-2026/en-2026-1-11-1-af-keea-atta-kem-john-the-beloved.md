# A Soul in Relationship with God Knows No Limits

January 11<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Barbados Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Keea Atta Kem & John the Beloved received by Al Fike

**Keea Atta Kem**: Bless you, beloved souls. I am Keea Atta Kem. Welcome to this Circle of Light, this place of light, these beings of light that gather on this day to seek out light further, greater, deeper, the light of God's Love, sparkling and glowing within, that blessing coming from the Creator of all. I urge you to make God a priority in your lives, to seek out God each and every day, to seek to serve God in each and every day and to make prayers to God, sincere, deep and beautiful prayers, so that your soul may continue to be awakened and grow in Love. For when you are in relationship with your Creator, in a conscious and beautiful relationship, you will find great peace and joy, fulfilment and love in this relationship.

Many in your world wish to bypass God and empower their own minds in meditations and rituals that satisfy the mind. Yet, I say to you, the deeper you go, the greater is the experience with God, for when you go beyond the mindful practices, the rituals and routines, you are with God in your soul. This is where you and God may have a powerful relationship, one that is full of blessings, insights and downloads, as you call them, of truth. Of course, when you desire this, the inflowing of the Essence of God's Soul, my beloved friends, there are whole realms of consciousness that you still do not know or have not explored. It merely requires your willingness to go deeper into yourself and to be within that chamber of light and love that is your soul, expressing it and connecting with it to the great Oversoul, the Great Creator of all.

When this connection is made, then the possibilities become endless. The depth and breadth of your experiences will be beyond your imaginings. For when you are without that cloak of mindful awareness and you have gone beyond this place, the expansiveness of the soul that is in relationship with God and receives the Essence of God within has no limitations and no form or ritual. It is merely you and God together, you and God establishing a bond of Love that shall continue to grow for all eternity.

God has only Love for you, unconditional, expansive, glorious Love that shall bring you upon a journey of soul awakening, so that you may understand your own soul, the faculties and aspects of your soul that are difficult to reach and to comprehend from the mindful consciousness and the material ways of your world. Rather, when you set the world aside and you go to God in sincere desire and prayer and longing, oh, that other world opens to you, my beloved friends. A world so different from what this worldly plane has to offer, a world that will bring to you spiritual enlightenment, knowledge, experience and Love beyond anything that is part of the natural man. It is something that is of the divine.

There are many who think that they already are of the divine. Indeed, they are creations of God, reflections of God, but they are not imbued with the Essence of God, the Essence which we call Divine Love. That Essence opens up the door to many things within you. Once received and acknowledged, it becomes the key to all that is of the soul and the soul's capacity to experience, to understand, to awaken to and to be transformed by this Love. The Essence of God then is within the individual and a process that is entirely of the soul and unique to the individual takes place.

God has created every soul unique and in this uniqueness comes the faculties and awareness, the experiences, the feelings, the Love that brings the soul alive, so that you are well aware of who you are, truly that unique soul that God created, so that you awaken to the many aspects of this unique soul that is you, so that you may know that your capacities and abilities expressed here on Earth will be enhanced and awakened with Love. In this, you may serve God with more clarity and power, depth and loving expression that comes with the awakened soul, that cannot come with a soul that has not been imbued with the Father's Love. Many faculties may be expressed without it, but many, many more are expressed and awakened with it.

My beautiful friends, beautiful souls, seeking souls, take that time to be with God. Dedicate yourself, your longings, your desires for truth to God. Ask God to enlighten you, to awaken you and transform you. You will enter into a world like none other, a place that is sacred within you, that is awakened to the divine that is beyond you. This connection, this relationship, is the highest calling and journey you can make. It brings the highest gifts and blessings that can come. All is given in Love, freely and abundantly. God's Love for you is so immense you cannot comprehend it, but you can feel it. You can sense it. It is a palpable thing and it can be yours as a palpable gift within your soul.

May you be blessed with the awareness of this gift as God gives it to you in your prayers, longings and askings. May it be a real and powerful experience, that it helps you to truly understand the awakenings that God wishes for you, the Love that God has for you. God bless you, my beloved friends. I am Keea atta Kem and I come to encourage you, to help you to understand what your soul truly is. It is the doorway to God and the capacity to awaken to all that God has put within you and all its wonderment and beauty. God bless you, beloved souls, and keep you in His Light. I love you. I am with you in your circles and I love you.

**John the Beloved:** … upon you and within you, beloveds. I am John, known as John the Beloved. I come to pray with you and to acknowledge each one of you, to bring this onrush, this Pentecost.

This Touch is within your scope and ability to draw this blessing to you. Open yourselves to this blessing. Long for it, desire it with all the intensity of your heart and soul and you will find that God will respond in accordance to that intensity and that effort made.

May you be blessed, beloveds, with the onrush, the blessings of God's Soul to yours. God wishes this for you, and it will come as you wish for yourself. God bless you, beloved souls. God bless you.
