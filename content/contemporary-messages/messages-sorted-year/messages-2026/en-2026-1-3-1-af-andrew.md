# You Will Not Falter When You Live in Alignment with God's Will

January 3<sup>rd</sup>, 2026

Barbados Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Andrew received by Al Fike

May understanding be yours, my beloved friends. I am Andrew. Is it not wonderful how God brings you together in places that, not long ago, you would have had no idea you would be here in this place, in at this time? Yet God’s plan is far-reaching. Indeed, as you are open and receptive to His blessings and guidance, many unusual things will take place. Many people that you do not know now will come into your lives. Much action, as dear daughter suggests, will take place and materialise in your lives. As you go deeper into your soul’s awareness, you will find the guidance becomes clearer and more complex. Therefore, what is asked of you will be more challenging at times, but also more gratifying at times.

Being on the Divine Path, seeking the Father's Love to enter into your soul, makes for a very rich life, a life full of surprises and a life full of opportunities. For God utilises you in every way possible and God opens the doors to many things so that you may walk through and do His bidding, His Will in the world. Yet this relies upon your receptivity, your faith, your prayers and desires. The concepts and rigidity of your minds often are instrumental in filtering the guidance given. It is reformed and recalibrated to suit the desires of the mind and therefore, what is asked of you and what may come from you and through you reflects the degree of this filtering and these biases.

I suggest you continue to pray to have those concepts of the mind less forward in your thinking and doing, and the knowings of your soul truly coming forward and making for you a clear picture, clear understanding of what God wishes. When God’s Will is done, there is no element of negativity or judgement or fear. These human conditions are overshadowed by love, and you feel a sense of expansiveness and freedom in your expressions regarding God’s Will through you. Indeed, we say, *“be a channel of love.”* A channel is something that is open and that is unencumbered by obstruction. When it comes from the highest of all sources, then it is a clear and powerful expression that benefits all who are affected by it.

You are continuing to grow, transform and awaken. You are continuing to release the inhibitions and the obstructions to your channel, your beautiful gifts. As you continue to be purified and clarified in your instrumentality, then wondrous things may come and beautiful blessings will be yours. There is so much yet to manifest, my beloved friends, and so much God wishes in your world, but He needs His instruments. He needs those channels. He needs those who step forward with great faith, innocence and love to do His bidding, to accept with humility His Will and to not question or doubt, to not be afraid, to not resist, but to truly be in alignment with God’s Will, this great river of God’s intention that is meant for all of humanity, for this world, for the universe.

Be true to your souls, my beloveds. Be true to all that is of God’s Will, God’s Love and you will not falter. There will be no side-setting of your purpose and God’s intention through you, but a clear and resounding expression through you of all that is meant to be. This is what you must align yourself with in these coming days. Be innocent and yet wise. Be faithful and strong in purpose and do not allow the intentions and desires of your material mind to get in the way of what God plans for these days together. For there is much work to do, my friends. There is much to be accomplished. There are many to embrace and we are prepared. Many of us come in to do the work, to reinforce God’s Will and to assist you in bringing through these things with clarity and of the highest expression.

Oh, my beloveds, all this is done in love, awakened in love, expressed in love, given in abundance and love. Allow the abundance to flow, the blessings to come and the Love to enter your soul in great abundance. Oh, my beloved friends, we unite in purpose. We are God’s instruments, all of us, and we shall be at this moment and every moment to come. May you give this up to God truly, humbly and completely. You will see the wonders manifest and the blessings flow and the Love abound. God bless you, my friends, I am Andrew. I am happy to be a part of this Circle of Light, be a part of God’s plan and to be close to you. May God bless you in His Love.
