# Be Diligent and Make Great Effort

January 7<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Barbados Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Confucius received by Al Fike

Yes, you have many angels with you, and you shall have them with you for the duration of your time together. You will benefit greatly from our presence and our assistance. Little birds, we want you to fly high into the light, to know the wings of your soul that shall propel you to God, to know the gifts that God has given you, that you may go out into the world and touch many, in love, in truth, in healing and in peace.

So much to give to the world that is indeed in great need. So many, many souls lost, unaware and unalive in their souls. There is so much work that needs to be done. Indeed, God has a plan that shall accomplish these tasks, tasks that if you were to consider the immensity of what is needed, you could not possibly bear up to this realisation. Instead, you must be humble and find your part in God's great plan for the salvation of humanity, find your way to this place of true knowing, this place of understanding your purpose, your gifts and to step forward as you become aware, become empowered with this awareness and empowered with love.

If love is not the preceding and primary expression of your being, then God cannot use you as He is able. Yes, you will have gifts and abilities, but the scope that is needed in this work requires a soul awakened and transformed in Love. That way, we will proceed with clarity, with power, with truth unadorned, with gifts awakened, with the wings of your soul spreading wide and like an eagle, that you may soar over the masses of your brothers and sisters and bring light as you go.

So much is needed and as you are well aware, the time grows short. You, my beloved and beautiful birds of truth and love, must be diligent and must make great effort. It can only be this way, my beloveds, because the scope of the need, the immensity of what is needed, the power of God working through you must be utilised through a clear and beautiful channel. Put aside your doubts and fears, my beloveds, and be free. Recognise the wings that God has given you, the abilities that are within you, the Love that God has placed within, the truth that you know, the awakenings that are happening at this very moment. My beloved, beloved children of truth, we are here for you. We will never abandon you, so you may rely upon us, and we shall be with you always. This is our commitment. This is God's commitment to you who ascribe to be the instruments, the channels of love for God. We are together, and so we shall awaken the potentials that are present and a part of each of you.

Confucius loves you. Oh, I love you so. The hope of the world, my dears, the hope of the world lies in your hands and the very few others that are in this world. Oh, how we pray for you, how we bring our love to you, how we uphold you and carry you. We are here as your guardians, your teachers, your healers and your companions in love. God bless you. God bless you all. My little birds, may you fly and know your place with God.
