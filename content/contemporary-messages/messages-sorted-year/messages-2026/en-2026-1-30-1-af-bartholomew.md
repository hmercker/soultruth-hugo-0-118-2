# Welcome to Trinidad

January 30<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Trinidad Retreat, Grand Couva, Trinidad

Bartholomew received by Al Fike

God bless you. It is Bartholomew. Welcome. Welcome to this place, this island, these people. God's children reside here, as they do everywhere. It is good that you make the effort, the journey to be here in prayer, your instrumentality shining forth for the benefit of those who live here and for this dear daughter to my left, who is earnest to bring the Truth of Divine Love to this island nation.

Yes, much work to do, my beloved friends. The souls trickle in, somewhat timid but curious, so I urge you to be gentle but firm with the truth. The power of love is what unites people, that unwritten language is what will surmount any barriers. Giving kindness, embracing others in love and respect, honouring the differences, yet bringing unity is important. We will be here to guide, to protect and to show the way. Ask for our influence, our guidance, ask God to influence and guide and there will be a flow of love, truth, light and peace for all who gather, all who come. Indeed, their souls will be touched, and they will come to a measure of awakening, a shift in consciousness that will help them in their lives.

For many, it is a difficult life in this place. There are many barriers to surmount, economic, cultural and spiritual. It is not an easy place to bring something so different as this message of Divine Love to people. They are used to something very different indeed, or they have not considered these things. You must start with the basics, talk about your own experience and be there as an equal, as their brother and sister, and honour love and appreciation for who they are and what they have managed to surmount at this time. They are eager. They are seeking, but often many are ignorant and unable to piece together this journey, this puzzle of truth that you have to offer.

Go slowly, my friends. Be respectful and kind. Be simple in your words. Always be loving in your actions and you will have a successful retreat with these beautiful souls. Of course, we will be with you. The angels will surround you, God's Hand upon you. Be faithful. The power of faith that brings you here, the power of faith that will ensure that love, light and truth are in the room. The power of faith that brings the flow of God's Love through you. These things, so important and yet so simple.

May you be blessed, my beloved souls, in all the goodness and light, all the love and joy that are a part of you and all that comes through you. Yes, there is much work to do. It is a formidable journey, but you continue to step forward to say, *“Yes, I am willing. I am here. I wish to do your work, dear God,”* and to do it in love, to do it in faith, to do it trusting that what we are meant to do and say will happen and be shared amongst the modest group that will form. The beginnings are always modest, but they will grow. They will grow, especially with this dear instrument to guide and to help, the one who is steadfast and is a member of this community.

May you all be blessed. May insights come. May there be peace amongst you and joy. God bless you, my friends. God bless you in His Light. I am Bartholomew and I am eager to begin this work, not only here but all through the nations of this part of this world. God bless you all, beloved souls. God bless you all.
