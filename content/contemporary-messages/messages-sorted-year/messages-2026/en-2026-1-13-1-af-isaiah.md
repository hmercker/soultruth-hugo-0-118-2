# God Offers You a New Life in His Love

January 13<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Barbados Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Isaiah received by Al Fike

… will stand forth in the flame and glory of this light, so much so that none will oppose or can oppose the truth that they bring and the manifestations of their being that are of God, working through them. You must be those flames of light, beloved souls. You step on the precipice of a great journey and service for God, yet you hesitate, for you feel what may come will change everything in your lives. I urge you not to hesitate, beloved souls, but to step forward with faith, faith that will uphold you, faith that will inform your journey, a soul that is on the precipice of awakening and realising its fate, its purpose, its destiny.

Only you can step forward. Only you can choose. Yet, I urge you that what may appear to you to be thin air is what God will use to uphold you and that support will be strong and unyielding. You must take that leap of faith and walk within the Light and Breath of God. In this, the work shall truly begin. In this, your way of life shall be transformed and you will enter into something entirely new. Like the rebirth of the soul, your life will be born again in the Light of God's Will and purpose and fate. Beloveds, you will have my support, the support of the angels, the Hand of God upon you in ways you will not have imagined, in ways that shall bring about the manifestations and blessings that God so wishes to give to others.

Have faith in yourself, beloved souls. Trust in the knowings of your soul. Trust in the guidance that God has to give and you will know a life like none other, a journey like none other, a fulfilment of joy like none other. As you feel and know the power of your own soul and the power of your soul's instrumentality expressing God's Will. My beloved and beautiful friends, you are on the precipice. Take that leap of faith and you will find new life, new ways, new expressions that come from deep within.

God bless you. I am Isaiah. I come to bring that invitation and that prophecy, that God offers you a new life with His Love. May you truly embrace it, know it for what it is and find the joy of it for what it can be. God bless you, my friends. Isaiah is with you and loves you.
