# Fellow Explorers

February 23<sup>rd</sup>, 2026

Online Circle of Light, Nashville, TN, USA

Galileo Galilei received by Jimbeau Walsh

It is I, Galileo, your brother in Christ.

I come to greet you in the magnetism of God’s love. Fellow explorers, those who have gone beyond the belief systems that keep the planet in darkness. Each one of you has gone into your soul longings, seeking a real and true connection with your Creator. I shall revisit momentarily what I shared through this brother before because I wish to make a point. I shared my camaraderie with Copernicus and his ideas, which I also found to be true through my scientific endeavors, a cosmology that was heliocentric, planets spinning around the sun rather than the beliefs of the church based on Aristotle and others that were geocentric, planets revolving around the earth. Of course, there was an inquisition, and I was put under house arrest, and most people do know this story who have looked at my life. The point I wish to make is this: I sought out the truth, and I believed when I found this truth that it was in harmony with the Bible and not opposed to it as those in positions of power asserted. Well, ultimately heliocentrism prevailed, and of course it’s common knowledge now. And, in fact, the astronomers of your day have looked into worlds, into universes beyond anything we could see or imagine in the vastness of Creation.

Whether you have a microcosmic or macrocosmic view, you have discovered the great key to opening the doors of celestial perception in the love of God. And when you feel that love permeating your being, opening your heart, bringing a light to your soul, it brings with it a great desire to share, to serve, to lift others up from their limited point of view, from their clinging to materialism and their dogmatic beliefs. And so, you speak the truth, and you tell them that if you simply add a prayerful longing to your Creator to receive the gift of Divine Love, it will change you. This advice to others is often met with resistance, similar to the resistance I encountered when I discovered a great truth. But you see, when you put it out there, however it is received or not, you have floated into the atmosphere, the truth of light and God’s love. You have shone a light in the darkness, and the glow of your soul and the joy that you feel is contagious. And if I may use the word again, ultimately it will be appreciated and taken to heart.

So, what seems to be perhaps a great struggle with the forces of darkness, a battle between good and evil, is really a simple matter of love, God’s love, which, as we always say, has already conquered all.

I do not come to this earth plane very often, but I really wished to share this through my brother and with you all, and I will admit I am drawn to the light and the love and the magnetism of your souls, and I am honored to be here as your guest. I shall step back in the peace of God’s love, and I shall tell you before I go, there are many here with you at this gathering. So, just relax, keep an open heart, trust the longings in your souls, and be with us in this light and in the presence of almighty God. I thank you.

I am Galileo.
