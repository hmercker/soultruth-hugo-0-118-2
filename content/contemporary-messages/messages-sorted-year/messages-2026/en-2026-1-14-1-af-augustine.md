# Each Day is a Blessing and Opportunity to Progress

January 14<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Barbados Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Augustine received by Al Fike

God bless you all. You may come to the end of a segment of development and awakening of the soul, but this surely is not the end of your efforts together and the development that is needed. My beloved friends, students of truth, we carry on. Each day is a blessing and an opportunity for further awakening and development. I urge you to continue in your prayers. I urge you to continue together, supporting one another. I urge the efforts of the angels to be close and the Hand of God upon you.

God bless you, beloved souls. Beloved students, we carry on. Although the venue, the opportunities and the possibilities may change, the journey will not and the awakenings will come. Whether you are on your own or with others, we continue to minister to you, to work with you and to work through you. God bless you as you continue to be open to the possibilities, to God's plan and to God's blessings. Thank you for your efforts. Our love is with you all and my love is with you all. I am Augustine, your teacher. My love is with you.
