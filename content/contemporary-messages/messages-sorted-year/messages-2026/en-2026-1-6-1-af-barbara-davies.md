# The World Can Be Changed With the Power of God's Love

January 6<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Barbados Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Barbara Davies received by Al Fike

I am happy you are here, my sisters. I am Barbara. In this time for prayer and replenishment, my hope is that each of you here will find greater insight, capacity for love and brightness of the soul through the Father's Love. It is wonderful that you are dedicating your time in prayer, for not only do you benefit, but the people of this place benefit, your families benefit, many spirits benefit from your efforts, from God's blessings upon each of us.

We will come close, the rapport will be strong, the time will be sacred and Love will flow abundantly. There is much for us to do, for we do not waste time but utilise every opportunity to open further gifts, insights and truth into each individual. As you pray for the Father's Love, so this ignites so much within you, so that you are receptive and able to perceive that which is given by the Divine Touch, by God's Love, by our guidance and influence, by all that is meant for each of you to flow and truly be a part of you.

My hope is that your conversations will be spiritual, that your efforts together will be harmonious, that as you come together in various configurations and groups, the Will of God will be done within your Circles of Light, and the beauty of God's Touch will be felt deeply within each of you. It is a time to love, to uplift, to nurture, to grow in truth, for the soul to be predominant, strong and forthright in its desires for further connection with God, further connection with one another, further understanding of who you truly are.

My beloved friends, I love each one of you. Each one is a light. Each one is serving God in their own way. Each one is truly blessed in that service, for God is utilising many in the world at this time to help awaken humanity, to help bring peace, to help bring comfort and love to many. Your work is great, my friends, so great. Yet on the surface it seems so humble and simple. In the depths of it, in the core of it, it is the greatest and most powerful work you can do, my friends, my loved ones, for with the power of love, the world can be changed. With the power of God's Love, great blessings that will bring harmony to this world may be shed upon the Earth and through these instruments of love, channels of love.

Oh, my loved ones, I am happy that you are together. I am happy that you continue to pursue the light and the truth and to be a channel of love. So many need you. So many clamour for you. This is a time for replenishment, upliftment, development, peace and healing. All these things will be given. Even though your numbers will be small, the many that will benefit from the light that you create in your prayers, your efforts together, will reach many, many souls, so many on my side of life and so many on yours. It will come in waves. It will come in light. It will come in revelations. It will come as the Holy Spirit touches your soul.

So much is here for you. Indeed, as you have made the effort to come together, that effort will be deeply blessed. You will come to further awakenings, further transformation, brighter light and greater Love. It is all here for you, my beloveds. It is here because God is here. Your angel friends are here, and the bright spirits are here.

May you be uplifted. May you see, feel and sense all the goodness of God's blessings for you. God bless you, my loved ones. I am Barbara. Oh, how I love you all. I love you all. God bless you. I will be close, as will many. God bless you.
