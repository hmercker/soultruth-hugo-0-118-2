# You Are Building a Bridge Between Yourself and God

January 8<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Barbados Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Andrew received by Al Fike

The bridge between you and God continues to be built every time you pray. Every time you put a thought, a desire, to God, you add more to this bridge and it becomes more solid, something you may walk upon, not just in the moment but for all eternity. You build the structure, the way, the path to your Heavenly Father.

Be earnest in your prayers, my beautiful friends. Have faith that God is listening to everyone and that indeed God will respond to everyone in some way. It will be a response. This is the law. This is how God ensures that you are blessed and cared for and that you may receive more of this great gift of Love. It is in prayer, my beloved friends. It is in your efforts to reach God.

May those efforts be clear, beautiful and powerful, for this ensures that this bridge between you and God is built and is permanent. May your soul continue to grow and awaken with all that is of God's blessings. We are with you. God is with you. God bless you all in His Love. I am Andrew and I love you. God bless you.
