# We Are United in God's Love

January 4<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Barbados Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Jesus received by Al Fike

God be with you, my beloveds. I am your brother and friend, Jesus. I come to pray with you, to be with you, to affirm that I am your brother and your friend in this Truth of Love, the Love of God, that is your saving grace and mine. We band together to bring light, to bring love to ourselves and to this world. We are God's children, seeking to be in alignment and in harmony with our beloved Creator. We choose to be of the highest, to be blessed by the greatest of Your blessings, that our souls may be open to a great inflowing of Your Love, dear God, Your Love, the Essence of Your Soul.

My beloveds, we are united in this truth for all eternity and we shall walk that road that will bring us into at-onement with God. There is no other way that shall bring us to this unity, to this at-onement. I urge you to seek its blessing with the longings of your soul. Seek this blessing and it shall be given to you, as you have the yearning of a soul seeking out God and a soul seeking to be loved and a soul seeking to love. We are all here with you, my beloveds. We pray with you. We pray for this place. We join you in your prayers for this world and we shall continue to seek to be God's instruments of light, blessings and love for your world.

I love you, my beloved friends. Oh, I love you so and shall walk with you for all eternity. I am Jesus. Yes, I am he. There is no other than who I am and what I represent in the Christ Spirit, the first brother along the path and one who resides in the highest of the Celestial Kingdom. I am here. I am with you and I love you. God bless you, my beloveds. I love you.
