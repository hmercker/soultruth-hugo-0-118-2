# Mastery in Service

February 16<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Online Circle of Light, Nashville, TN, USA

Care Darby Walsh received by Jimbeau Walsh

My dear ones, it is I, your sister Care. I wish you to consider this evening what I will call soulful consideration. One can look at the world and see a plethora of things to be concerned about, and some are immersed in worry and those fearful feelings that come with that worry, and so, of course, we encourage you to pray, to get your guidance from on high. It is not a matter of never being concerned or wishing to solve a problem, correct an ill, or confront an evil. No, this is the world! But when you put these concerns into soulful consideration, truly asking for guidance from God who dispatches his angels. You begin to see things fall into place, things you perhaps thought could not be remedied, healed, or turned around, and suddenly the door opens, and you are able to shine your light. For as Matthew wrote, you are the bringers of light to this world. Of course, you are all familiar with his words of not putting the light under a bushel but setting it out on the table or the lamp stand that all may see the light, which is in fact a gift from God, living in your souls, and thus sharing this gift, shining this light, gives glory to God. It puts mastery in service, and it brings a healing to this world so in need, as you well know.

So, you begin to see those small acts done in faith, of sharing your love with others, of shining your light, make a difference, for it is true, love conquers all. And to those who feel they are up against formidable odds on their journey, on this path, know that you are in the company of celestial angels when you pray, an army greater than any on earth with the sword of God’s love piercing each heart, and the shield of protection from vexed spirits and those who would harm you.

Carry this love and allow your material concerns to take a back seat. And I remember these words of the Master to the man who asked, *“How do I follow you?”* Of course, this was answered with the bold words of, *“Sell all your possessions and give the money to the poor.”* Now, unless you are extremely poor to begin with, this is quite a challenge. But really, the Master has told me that he was simply trying to say, ‘make God your priority.’  The possession of material things can do much good in the world, and considering how much one may need at any given point is a good consideration. So, take these material benefits that are more than you need and give them to all who are in need, and do this with the love of God in your heart, if you wish to follow Jesus.

I know that my beloved, he does not prefer to have the discussion be about him but I was with him today while he was at the dentist and he was describing to the dentist our meeting with the angels when I was on earth and he was telling the dentist he was very attracted to my light, that he saw this light around me and the dentist said, *”well that’s what I see around you.”* And I don’t wish to embarrass anyone because, really, I’m telling this because it applies to all of you. Let your light shine on every being, as the song goes, that God who has blessed you may touch the lives of all.

I thank you, and I love you with all the love in my soul. I am your sister and friend, for all eternity. May God bless you.

I am Care.
