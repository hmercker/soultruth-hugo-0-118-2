# The Only Impediment to Truth Resides Within You

January 31<sup>st</sup>, 2026

Trinidad Retreat, Grand Couva, Trinidad

Augustine received by Al Fike

I am your teacher, Augustine, once the Bishop of Hippo. The only impediment to truth, beloved souls, resides within you. Within you are doubts, fears and judgement. Within you are the need for control and the desire to be independent from all things other than that which you feel comfortable with and wish to express in your life as truly your own. Yet, if you can overcome these impediments, you will find that when you collaborate with God. For God does not take over your free will, but God adds to your life, helps you to navigate and understand your life and the true meaning of life. When you are willing to come to that place of acceptance, of openness, putting aside your doubt and your fears, then God will open the floodgates of His Love in such abundance that you will be overwhelmed with joy, with the understanding that you are truly loved.

Yet, even that experience does not give you the full picture of the depth and breadth of how much God loves you and how much love is available to you as it is given, drop by drop, bit by bit. It will accumulate, change you and bring you to that place of awakening, soul awakening, not awakening of the mind, for that may come as well, but what must come first is the awakening of the soul, where your perceptions and your capacity to understand in ways that the mind cannot, will come to the forefront of your consciousness and bring you to that place of awareness. Indeed, you are a creature of God, you are a child of God and God’s wish is that you will know Him and love Him and in this He will love you. He already knows you and His Love exists, waiting as a gift to enter into your soul and awaken all the faculties, all the gifts that lie within you, things that you know nothing of now, my children, my beautiful students, but as you progress along this path of love, you will know it, feel it and recognise it. It will change you irrevocably forevermore.

Seek you the kingdom, my beloved and beautiful students, and all things shall come to you. All things shall come to you. May you be blessed in the Father’s Love and know it is a true and wondrous gift that shall reside in you for all eternity. It is yours for the asking. It is yours given in love and it shall indeed transform you, awaken you and bring you on that eternal path of love that shall not end. God bless you all, beloved souls in His Love. Feel the longings of your soul. Acknowledge that. Awaken to it. Put it forward in prayer and it shall bring its results. This, I promise you. This, I promise you. I am your teacher, Augustine, and I am with you in your prayers. Many angels surround you now and seek to show you the way to help you to experience this gift. God bless you all. God bless you all, seeking and beautiful souls that you are. May you be blessed in His Love.
