# This Love is Universal

January 14<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Barbados Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Orion received by Al Fike

God bless you, my friends. I am Orion. I come to reinforce the truth that has been given to you, to help you integrate and understand it, for these truths that have been shared are universal. Although our language and our experience of it may be somewhat different from yours, the actual law, practice and receptance of this gift is the same. I come from a different planet, one that is resplendent in this Love, this condition of Love. We come to uphold you in your desire for it. We come to uphold this world, to support you in the transition that must come to create favourable conditions and receptivity to this blessing. Much is given to you, my friends, not only from your angel friends, the bright spirits and your friends on Earth, but also those of us who are not of Earth, but seek to uphold this universe in love, to say that, yes, the source of this Love is universal and available to all.

Seek it out, my friends. Seek out this blessing and you will become like us. This world is destined to become like our world, a world of peace and harmony, filled with joy and adventure, creativity, laughter, all the goodness that life has to give and is present in abundance in my world. Our wish, our desire, is that your world may indeed partake of this great abundance of Love and that in this the world will be changed. Thus, every soul in your world will be changed, provided they have the desire to do so. First, they must know of this thing. They must have some recognition, some desire deep within them ignited, in order that the blessing may come into them.

This law is universal as well. It is not that our world was completely transformed and blessed merely because God wished it. We too had to discover it, although we did so at the beginning and thus the many, many centuries, the billions of years of our existence have been blessed, and the intensity and power of that Love of God is tremendous in scope. If you were to be within that world, taken instantaneously from here to there, you would find it very difficult to withstand the energy, as you would call it, the conditions that are a part of it. Your world must build gradually toward these conditions, must adjust and acclimatise to the power of this Love, this Essence of God that will not only inhabit your souls, but be reflected back and forth from one soul to another, the expression of that Love seeking to add to and bring upliftment to your brothers and sisters. This is how the conditions build, the reflection of that light back and forth, reinforced, reflected and reinforced until everything is imbued with light and thus, harmony.

Yes, when your angel friends say there is much work to do, believe me, there is a tremendous amount of work to do before your Earth finds its rightful place in the universe of God's creation. You have taken the hard road. You continue to struggle, even those of you who know this Truth, you struggle in these conditions. Thus, we are there, here with you as are your angel friends and others to help uphold you, uplift you, protect you, guide you along the course of action, awakening and transformation that must take place. Yet, you become the leavening for the dough, if I would put it that way. The souls of humanity are more readily awakened when you become awake and true to this beautiful transformation of soul. The power of this within you as mortals will have a far-reaching effect on humanity, leavening the dough, the starter for this process. You are given much. Much attention is upon you, much help and upliftment is given.

My beloved friends, you are at a crucial time, a time that will invariably bring great change. There is a plan in place for that change to be realised in the way that God intends. We are aware and the spirits of the higher realms of Celestial Kingdoms are aware. We follow that course, that plan, in order to help you on that journey, so that you may be cognisant of God's Will and God's plan, awakening, awakening along the path of soul consciousness. In that, it is easy to know God's Will and to know His plan. We urge you forward so that your souls may be ignited and come to the realisation of what you are meant to be and do.

We continue, my friends, on that road. Much is given, and you, in turn, will give much to the world. There are others, others that you will connect with and reach out to and find in this great world. God will ensure this. God will create the conditions and the opportunities for these connections to take place. You will recognise one another. You will know that they are truly your brothers and sisters, truly blessed as you are blessed. Oh yes, there is much work to do, many blessings to be given, many blessings will flow through you. Seek this out, my friends, and you will know the wonderment, fulfilment of your soul's purpose expressed. See the Light and power of the Love of God within you.

God bless you, my friends. Orion loves you dearly. I have been present with your prayers and ruminations. I too have been a part of this. God bless you all on this journey. God bless you in Love.
