# Seek the Limitless Love of God and Do Not Hold Yourself Back

January 7<sup>th</sup>, 2026

Barbados Retreat, Christchurch, Barbados

Augustine received by Al Fike

Beloved souls, I am your teacher, Augustine. I lived many centuries ago, and my path toward God was varied. It had its challenges. It had its error. It had its dead ends in the journey, but in time, I came to know God with my soul open and receptive to what God had to give to me. Are you any different from my journey? The story may be somewhat different, but the journey that most souls take to God often meanders and goes in directions that are not helpful. This is the story of life on Earth, is it not? A life that is one of distraction, a lack of focus and a lack of understanding.

Yet, God is always there to reach out to every soul, no matter how dark that soul is, no matter how misguided and no matter how lost. God is always there to embrace, to love and to forgive. So it is in this world of yours that the lost souls may find redemption, light and truth. So it is in this world of yours that God requires those who are willing to reach out in compassion and love to help uplift humanity. Are you that person? Are you that soul that has walked the road of their own making, their own desire, their own journey, Ibut has found light, has found that which gives them nourishment, spiritual nourishment. and in so doing, have you dedicated yourselves to service to humanity? For every soul that has ascended into light has a compulsion, a need, a desire to share that light, to share their gifts with another. The light that we share, the light that is all around you, is the Light of God's Love, a gift that is offered to each one of you. A gift that comes to you because you are a light and you have drawn light to you.

God reaches toward you as you reach toward God and says, *“I love you. You are precious in my sight. You are my creation and I wish for you to be uplifted and healed, that my grace, the grace that emanates from the great Soul of God, may come to you and be yours.”* God gives you this gift. God does so in Love. You come together today seeking upliftment and support, seeking healing and seeking peace. It is given freely and abundantly. It is for you to open and receive. It is for you to feel, in humbled gratitude, the magnificence, the wonderment of our Creator and all that our Creator is and all that our Creator gives.

My beloved students, do not hold back from opening, receiving, feeling and knowing all that God is and all that God has to give. For in His Love for us, He gives everything. Nothing is without. No matter what condition, no matter what frame of mind, what desire you have in your soul and mind, God is ever waiting to give. For what God has to give is limitless and the Love that God has for you is limitless. I urge you to seek it, this Divine Love. It is yours for the asking and it shall never cease to be given to you. As you ask and seek it out, it will change and transform, awaken and bring deep peace and joy. It is yours, beloved souls. Be the channel of love and light. Be within the flow of God's blessings Divine. It is yours at this very moment. Seek for this highest blessing. It is yours. It is yours.

God bless you, my beloved students. I am your teacher, Augustine. I reiterate what I have spoken countless times to remind those of you who know of this and to inform those of you who do not truly understand it, that today you sit in this sacred circle filled with love, Love of God, love of your angel friends, love of your mortal friends, all giving love. All shall benefit from that gift. God bless you, beloved souls. Augustine loves you and is here with you, along with many others. God bless you.
